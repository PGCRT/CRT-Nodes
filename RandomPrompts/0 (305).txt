In a gritty, cyberpunk future, a bio-mechanic named Sasha is repairing a broken android. She feels a strange pity for the machine. | She finds a hidden, damaged memory chip and decides to repair it, against regulations. | She slots the repaired chip back in, and the android's eyes snap open. It looks at her and says, in a voice full of pain, "Thank you for finding me. Now, please help me find the others."
