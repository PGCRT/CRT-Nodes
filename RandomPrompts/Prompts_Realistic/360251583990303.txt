**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

- **Subject & Action**  
  A woman with dark, wavy hair, wearing a gold sequined bikini top, pouring a bottle of white wine (label “VAIDY”) into her mouth. Her left arm is tattooed with geometric and portrait designs; her right shoulder shows a small “M” tattoo. She’s mid‑drink, with a slight motion blur on the liquid stream.

- **Capture Style**  
  Candid, unposed, spontaneous — subject unaware or mid‑action. Slight tilt and off‑center framing, partial crop. No rule‑of‑thirds intentionality — accidental composition. Natural motion freeze or slight blur from handheld instability.

- **Environment**  
  Indoor setting in a modern apartment: a wooden stool and a colorful rainbow‑striped object (maybe a towel or banner) are visible behind her left shoulder. The time of day is late afternoon, soft natural light coming from the west, creating a warm, slightly overexposed tone. Ambient humidity is mild; the air feels cool.

- **Lens & Technical**  
  Short focal length (24‑35 mm), wide‑angle lens. No bokeh — background fully sharp, everything in focus. No portrait mode or subject isolation. Slight auto‑HDR or auto‑beauty mode off for a raw, unprocessed look. No filters, no color grading, no post‑processing aesthetic.

- **Lighting & Exposure**  
  Zero studio lighting, zero professional composition, zero editorial tone. Slight overexposure due to ambient light; the image has a natural, slightly hazy feel. The exposure is set to capture the sparkle of the gold sequins and the liquid’s reflection.

- **Details & Imperfections**  
  Capture the texture of the sequins, the slight sheen on the woman’s skin, the droplets of wine in mid‑air. Include minor imperfections: a slightly crooked bottle angle, a faint smudge on the bottle label, and a tiny vibration blur on the stream. The background should show a wooden floor and a white door frame.
