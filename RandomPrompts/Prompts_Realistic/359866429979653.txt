**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

- **Subject & Pose**  
  A young woman with long, wavy brown hair standing in a sunlit living room, mid‑adjustment of a delicate pink lace lingerie set. Her hands are gently pulling the fabric upward near her chest, creating a slight dynamic tension. She’s angled slightly to the left, facing the camera but not directly — a casual, candid posture as if caught in an unplanned moment.

- **Lighting & Exposure**  
  Natural daylight from a large window on the left, creating soft overexposure on the subject’s upper body and a subtle warm glow. Slight handheld shake gives a natural motion blur around the edges; no auto‑beauty or filter enhancement — raw, unprocessed look.

- **Composition & Framing**  
  Wide‑angle lens (24‑35 mm) capturing a broad view of the scene: the woman occupies the central‑left third of the frame, with a potted plant on a wooden side table to her left, a brown leather sofa and a framed picture on the wall to her right. The framing is intentionally off‑center, slightly tilted, and crops the top of her head and lower legs out of frame for a spontaneous feel.

- **Environment Details**  
  Time of day: late morning, with bright but not harsh sunlight. A small potted plant sits on a dark wood table; a green houseplant is visible in the background near a window. A brown leather sofa with a floral pillow sits to the right. The wall is painted a light neutral tone, and a framed artwork hangs above the sofa.

- **Lens & Technical**  
  Short focal length (24‑35 mm) for wide coverage; everything from foreground to background is fully sharp — no bokeh or depth‑of‑field blur. No portrait mode or subject isolation. Slight overexposure on the subject’s chest area due to ambient light, giving a warm, natural tone.

- **Captured Mood**  
  The image feels like a spontaneous snapshot: the woman appears unaware of the camera, mid‑action, with a slight tilt and partial crop that adds authenticity. All elements — the woman, furniture, plants, and wall decor — are in focus, creating a rich, detailed realistic candid photograph.
