**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

- **Subject & Pose**  
  A young woman with long, wavy brown hair, wearing a black deep‑V tank top, captured mid‑gesture as she touches her chin with her right hand. Her left wrist bears a black watch; a subtle, natural smile is on her face. The shot should feel spontaneous—she’s unaware of the camera, slightly tilted, off‑center framing.

- **Motion & Focus**  
  Slight motion blur from handheld instability, natural freeze or soft blur. Everything in focus—no bokeh, no depth‑of‑field isolation. No auto‑beauty or filter processing; raw, unprocessed look with mild overexposure to keep ambient light warm.

- **Lens & Technical**  
  Wide‑angle lens (short focal length, ~24 mm). All elements—subject and background—fully sharp, no portrait‑mode softening. Capture in a bright, natural daylight setting; the time of day is mid‑afternoon, with soft, diffused sunlight coming from a window on the left.

- **Environment**  
  A cozy, lived‑in bedroom or home office. A wooden side table with a speaker, bottles, and a small black bag sits to the right; a shelf with books and a blue box is visible above it. A white rug lies on the floor, a window lets in daylight, and a ceiling light fixture hangs overhead. The background should be fully sharp, no blur, no studio lighting.

- **Composition & Style**  
  Candid, unposed, spontaneous—subject unaware or mid‑action. Imperfections encouraged: slight tilt, off‑center framing, partial crop. No rule‑of‑thirds intentionality—accidental framing. Zero studio lighting, zero professional composition, zero editorial tone. Natural motion freeze or slight blur from handheld instability.

- **Post‑Processing**  
  Auto‑HDR or auto‑beauty mode off — raw, unprocessed look. Slight overexposure to keep ambient light warm. No filters, no color grading, no post‑processing aesthetic.  

Provide only the structured prompt.
