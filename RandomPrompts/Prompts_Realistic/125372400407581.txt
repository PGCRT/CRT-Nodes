**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

- **Subject & Pose**  
  A young woman with long, wavy light‑brown hair, wearing a red lace bodysuit with floral motifs and criss‑cross straps. She is seated on a plush teal sofa, leaning slightly to her left, one arm resting on her knee, the other extended outward as if she just finished a gesture or is about to touch something. Her face is turned toward the camera but not directly at it—she’s mid‑expression, a subtle smile with lips parted, eyes half‑closed as if caught in a moment of casual confidence. A small gold necklace rests on her collarbone, and a detailed wing‑tattoo adorns her left upper arm.

- **Lighting & Exposure**  
  Natural daylight from a window behind the sofa casts a soft, warm glow across the scene. The exposure is slightly overexposed on the highlights, giving a raw, unprocessed look. No auto‑beauty or filter enhancement—just the camera’s native sensor capturing the light.

- **Camera Settings**  
  - Lens: 24‑mm wide‑angle, short focal length, handheld.  
  - Focal depth: everything from the subject to the background is fully sharp; no bokeh.  
  - Shutter speed: 1/60 s (or 1/80 s) with a slight motion blur on the subject’s hair and arms, indicating spontaneous movement.  
  - ISO: 400–600 for natural grain.

- **Composition & Framing**  
  The shot is intentionally off‑center; the subject’s left side extends beyond the frame’s right edge, creating a partial crop. The camera is tilted slightly to the right, giving an accidental, candid feel. No rule‑of‑thirds—framing is spontaneous, as if the photographer was in the middle of a conversation or adjusting a pose.

- **Background & Environment**  
  Behind the sofa, a window with sheer curtains lets in natural light; a small potted plant sits on a side table, and a floral pillow rests against the back of the sofa. The time of day is late afternoon, around 5 pm, when the sun is low but still bright. The weather outside is clear, with a gentle breeze that ruffles the woman’s hair.

- **Details & Imperfections**  
  - Slight tilt and partial crop give a raw, unposed vibe.  
  - The subject’s arm is slightly blurred, indicating motion.  
  - A faint reflection on her skin from the window’s light adds realism.  
  - The tattoo on her arm shows fine lines and shading that are crisp in focus.

- **Post‑Processing**  
  No color grading or filters—just a straight capture with auto‑HDR off. The image retains the camera’s native dynamic range, preserving all details from foreground to background.
