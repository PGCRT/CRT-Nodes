**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

- **Subject & Pose**  
  A young woman with long, dark hair kneeling on a cluster of smooth, moss‑covered stones in a tropical garden. She is naked, her body slightly turned toward the camera, hands resting on her thighs, head tilted a little to the left, eyes focused forward. A small rose tattoo adorns her left upper arm. Her posture is casual, as if she’re mid‑movement, with a faint handheld shake captured in the frame.

- **Lighting & Exposure**  
  Natural daylight from a high‑angle, late‑afternoon sun; slight overexposure on the upper body, creating a warm, soft glow. No auto‑HDR or beauty filters—raw, unprocessed look. The exposure is slightly uneven, giving an authentic, spontaneous feel.

- **Lens & Technical Settings**  
  Wide‑angle lens (24–35 mm), short focal length, everything in focus from foreground to background. No bokeh; the background remains sharp, no depth‑of‑field blur. Handheld shot with slight motion blur at the edges.

- **Environment**  
  A lush, green garden with palm fronds and a small, flowering plant behind her. Large, rounded stones surround her knees, a few fallen leaves on the ground. The scene is set in the early evening, with warm, golden light filtering through the foliage.

- **Composition & Framing**  
  Casual, off‑center framing; no rule‑of‑thirds intentionality. The shot is slightly tilted, giving an accidental, candid feel. The composition captures the subject’s full figure and a portion of the surrounding foliage, with a partial crop that includes a few stones and leaves at the edges.

- **Style Notes**  
  Raw, unposed, spontaneous—subject appears unaware of the camera. Slight tilt and handheld instability create a natural motion blur. No post‑processing color grading or filters; the image retains the genuine texture and imperfections of a real‑life snapshot.
