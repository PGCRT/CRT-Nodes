**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

- **Subject & Action**  
  A young woman in a rustic tavern, mid‑gesture as she draws a short sword from her belt. Her hair is tied back in a loose ponytail, a few strands escaping to frame her face. She wears a worn leather vest over a green tunic, with a brown sash and a small cross pendant hanging at her chest. Her left hand grips the hilt of a curved dagger, while her right hand holds a wooden staff; both hands are slightly blurred from motion.

- **Pose & Composition**  
  The shot is taken with a wide‑angle lens (24 mm), capturing a broad view of the scene. The framing is intentionally off‑center: the woman’s left side leans slightly to the right, creating a casual, spontaneous feel. The camera is handheld, producing a subtle shake and a tiny overexposure that gives the image an authentic, raw look.

- **Lighting & Exposure**  
  Ambient lighting comes from a cluster of candle flames on a wooden table to the left and a lantern hanging from the stone ceiling on the right. The light is warm, slightly yellow‑tinted, with a mild overexposure that brightens the central figure while keeping the background fully sharp. No post‑processing filters or color grading are applied; the exposure is natural, as if captured by an amateur photographer in a real setting.

- **Environment**  
  The location is a stone‑walled tavern, with rough limestone pillars and a wooden beam ceiling. A small table with a candle and a mug sits to the left, while a crate and a hanging lantern are visible on the right. In the background, a man in a grey shirt is partially visible, holding a spear, adding depth to the scene. The time of day is late afternoon, as indicated by the warm, slightly dim lighting.

- **Lens & Technical**  
  - Focal length: 24 mm (wide‑angle)  
  - Aperture: f/5.6 (mid‑range)  
  - Shutter speed: 1/125 s (producing slight motion blur)
