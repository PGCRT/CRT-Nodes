**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

- **Subject & Pose**  
  A young woman with shoulder‑length brown hair, wearing a sheer black halter top and distressed denim shorts, captured mid‑walk in a sunlit garden. Her left wrist is adorned with a pink–green double‑band; she’s slightly tilted to the right, catching a casual, spontaneous vibe.  

- **Lighting & Exposure**  
  Natural daylight from a high‑angle, late‑afternoon sun creates a soft overexposure on the subject’s face and a gentle warmth in the background. Slight handheld shake gives a subtle motion blur around the edges.  

- **Environment**  
  The setting is a lush, informal park with a variety of flowering plants (pink, yellow, white blooms) lining a gravel path. In the background, a few indistinct figures are walking or chatting, adding to the candid feel. Trees and a faint fence line the perimeter, giving depth without any intentional rule‑of‑thirds composition.  

- **Time of Day & Weather**  
  Captured at 5:30 pm on a clear, humid day; the sky is bright but not harsh, with a slight haze that keeps everything in focus.  

- **Lens & Technical Settings**  
  24‑mm wide‑angle lens (short focal length), f/4.0 aperture, 1/60 s shutter speed, ISO 400. No bokeh—background is fully sharp. Handheld shot with slight instability, producing a natural motion blur that’s not over‑processed.  

- **Capture Style**  
  Candid, unposed, spontaneous—subject unaware of the camera. Slight tilt and off‑center framing create an accidental, organic look. Zero studio lighting, zero professional composition, zero editorial tone.  

- **Post‑Processing**  
  Raw, unfiltered image with auto‑HDR off; no color grading or filters applied. Only minimal white‑balance adjustment to preserve the natural tones.
