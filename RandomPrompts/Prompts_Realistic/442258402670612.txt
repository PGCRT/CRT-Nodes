**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

- **Subject & Pose**  
  A young woman with a high ponytail, wearing a white back‑less camisole and white high‑waist jeans, captured mid‑turn as she holds a wine glass. Her head is slightly tilted to the right, eyes focused on something off‑camera, giving a candid, unposed feel.

- **Motion & Focus**  
  Slight motion blur at the edges from handheld shake; everything in focus—no portrait‑mode depth of field. The blur is subtle, preserving sharpness in the background.

- **Lighting & Exposure**  
  Natural daylight from a western window, slightly overexposed on the subject’s back, creating soft highlights on her skin and clothing. No color grading or filters—raw, unprocessed look.

- **Lens & Technical**  
  Wide‑angle lens (24‑35 mm), short focal length, no bokeh. Background fully sharp: a beige sofa with a green pillow, a small wooden coffee table, a potted plant in the corner, and a framed picture on the wall.

- **Environment**  
  A bright, casual living room during late morning. The time of day is early afternoon, with warm natural light casting soft shadows. A second woman in a white t‑shirt is partially visible in the background, holding a glass, adding to the candid vibe.

- **Composition & Framing**  
  No rule‑of‑thirds intentionality—accidental, off‑center framing that captures the subject’s back and side profile, with a slight tilt. The crop is partial, cutting off the top of the sofa and the lower part of the table, enhancing spontaneity.

- **Details & Imperfections**  
  Slight tilt in the camera angle, minor motion blur on the subject’s arm, natural imperfections in the focus and exposure that give a genuine, raw feel. The image has zero studio lighting and zero editorial tone—just a spontaneous snapshot of everyday life.
