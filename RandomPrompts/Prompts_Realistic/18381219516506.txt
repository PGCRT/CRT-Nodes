**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework – “Neon‑Bathed Back‑View”**

- **Subject & Pose**  
  A young woman with long, wavy auburn hair standing in a dimly lit urban loft at dusk. She’s captured from a rear‑three‑quarter angle, mid‑turn as she steps forward, one foot slightly ahead of the other. Her body is unposed, natural motion blur on the legs and hips, a faint handheld shake that gives a candid feel.

- **Lighting & Exposure**  
  Warm, reddish‑blue neon lights from a nearby street reflect off her skin; slight overexposure on the upper back and shoulders, underexposed lower legs for contrast. No auto‑beauty or filter processing – raw, unedited look. The background is fully sharp, no bokeh, no depth‑of‑field blur.

- **Environment**  
  Location: a concrete‑finished loft with exposed brick walls, a white table and a small stool in the background. Time of day: twilight (late afternoon to early evening). Ambient conditions: mild wind, slight humidity that gives a soft glow to the scene.

- **Lens & Technical**  
  Short focal length (24 mm) wide‑angle lens. Everything in focus – no portrait mode or subject isolation. Slight tilt and off‑center framing; partial crop that cuts off a bit of the left shoulder, giving an accidental, spontaneous feel. No rule‑of‑thirds intentionality.

- **Composition Notes**  
  The composition is “accidental” – the subject’s back is slightly tilted, her left hand rests on a table edge, right hand hangs free. The background includes a faint outline of a city skyline through a window, adding depth without losing sharpness. The prompt should emphasize raw realism, no color grading or post‑processing aesthetic, just a straight capture that feels like a genuine moment frozen in time.
