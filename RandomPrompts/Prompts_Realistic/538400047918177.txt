  No rule‑of‑thirds intentionality—accidental framing. The shot is a wide, candid capture that includes the subject’s full body from head to toe, with a portion of the cinema marquee and a street sign in the background. The frame is slightly crooked, giving an authentic, spontaneous feel.

- **Lighting**  
  Natural daylight, zero studio lighting. Slight overexposure on the bright sky, creating a warm tone. No post‑processing aesthetic—just raw, real imagery.
