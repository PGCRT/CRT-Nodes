**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

- **Subject & Pose**  
  A young woman with long, wavy dark hair, wearing a gray cropped hoodie and denim shorts, crouches on the floor of a casual coffee‑shop. She leans her elbow on her knee, looking slightly off‑camera with a relaxed, candid expression. Her beige Nike sneakers are visible, one foot planted, the other bent.  

- **Lighting & Exposure**  
  Natural daylight from a large window creates a soft, slightly overexposed highlight on her face and shoulder. The lighting is uneven—warm tones from the window, cool tones from the interior—giving a raw, unprocessed look. No auto‑HDR or beauty filters; just a handheld shot with slight instability.  

- **Composition & Framing**  
  Wide‑angle lens (24‑35 mm) captures a broad view of the scene. The frame is intentionally off‑center: the subject’s left side leans toward the left edge, her right foot near the bottom right corner. No rule‑of‑thirds—just spontaneous framing. Background is fully sharp, no bokeh.  

- **Environment**  
  Location: a cozy, modern coffee shop with exposed concrete walls and wooden shelving. A wooden table with coffee blenders, cups, and a small potted plant sits to the left; a green chair and a stack of magazines are on the right. A second customer is visible in the background, facing away. The time of day is mid‑afternoon, with warm sunlight streaming through the window.  

- **Lens & Technical**  
  Short focal length (24–35 mm), wide‑angle, handheld. All elements are in focus—no portrait‑mode depth‑of‑field blur. Slight motion blur on the subject’s hand and knee to suggest natural movement.  

- **Details & Imperfections**  
  The image has a slight tilt and partial crop, giving it a casual, unposed feel. A faint lens flare is present from the window light. The color palette is muted: gray hoodie, denim blue, beige shoes, warm wood tones, with a hint of green from a plant.
