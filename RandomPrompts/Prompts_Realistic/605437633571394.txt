**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

- **Subject & Pose**  
  A young woman with long, wavy brown hair standing on a sunlit city sidewalk. She’s wearing high‑waisted denim jeans and a plain white tee that’s slightly lifted at the front, revealing a hint of midriff. Her left hand is in her pocket, right arm relaxed by her side, body angled 30° toward the camera, head turned to face the lens with a subtle, natural smile.  

- **Capture Style**  
  Candid, unposed, spontaneous — she’s mid‑step, caught in a casual “just happened to be photographed” moment. Slight tilt and off‑center framing; the crop is partial, cutting off the top of her head and the lower part of her jeans. No rule‑of‑thirds intentionality—everything feels accidental, like a handheld shot.  

- **Motion & Focus**  
  Slight motion blur on the background and subject’s arms, indicating a handheld shutter speed of about 1/60 s. Everything remains fully sharp—no portrait‑mode depth‑of‑field blur; the background is in focus as well.  

- **Lighting & Exposure**  
  Natural daylight, late afternoon (around 5 pm). The sun is high but slightly to the left, creating a soft warm glow on her face and a gentle highlight on her right shoulder. Slight overexposure on the upper part of the frame gives a raw, unprocessed look.  

- **Auto‑HDR / Auto‑Beauty**  
  Auto‑HDR off, auto‑beauty mode off. No filters, no color grading, no post‑processing aesthetic—just the raw capture.  

- **Lens & Technical**  
  Short focal length (24 mm), wide‑angle lens. All elements are in focus: subject and background. No bokeh, no subject isolation.  

- **Environment**  
  Specific real‑world location: a tree‑lined city street with a concrete sidewalk, a low‑rise building on the right, a parked car in the distance, and a small green park on the left. Time of day: late afternoon, ambient light warm and slightly hazy. Weather: clear, mild breeze.  

- **Composition Notes**  
  The shot is taken with a wide‑angle lens, capturing a broad view of the subject’s upper body and a portion of her lower body, as well as a section of the sidewalk and background. The background is fully sharp, no depth‑of‑field blur.  

- **Prompt Summary for Text‑to‑Image Generation**  
  “A young woman with long wavy brown hair, wearing high‑waisted denim jeans and a plain white tee, stands on a sunlit city sidewalk at late afternoon. Capture style candid, unposed, spontaneous, slight tilt, off‑center framing, partial crop, no rule‑of‑thirds.”
