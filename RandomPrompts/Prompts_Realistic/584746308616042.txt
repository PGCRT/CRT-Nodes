**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

  Natural daylight from a large window behind her creates a soft, slightly overexposed highlight on her forehead and left shoulder. No auto‑beauty or filter enhancement; the look remains raw and unprocessed.

  Shot on a wide‑angle lens (24 mm), the frame is intentionally off‑center: the subject’s head sits slightly above the center of the frame, her left shoulder cuts off the right edge, and the top of the phone extends beyond the upper border. The composition is spontaneous—no rule‑of‑thirds intentionality, just a casual, candid capture.

The time of day is mid‑afternoon, with warm, diffused sunlight filtering through the leaves, creating soft dappled light on her face and clothing. - **Technical Settings**  
  - Focal length: 24 mm (wide angle)

  The image carries a slight tilt, a partial crop that cuts off the right side of her shoulder, and a faint motion blur around her hand and phone.
