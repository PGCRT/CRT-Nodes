**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

- **Subject & Pose**  
  A woman with long, dark hair standing on a large, weathered boulder in a sunlit meadow. She’s wearing a black lace bra and matching fishnet stockings with leather boots; one hand is raised to the back of her head, the other hanging by her side. Her left thigh shows a rose tattoo. She’s mid‑step, as if just having taken a pace, with a natural, unposed stance that feels spontaneous and candid.

- **Capture Style**  
  Candid, unposed, spontaneous — subject unaware or mid‑action. Slight tilt and off‑center framing; the composition is accidental, not rule‑of‑thirds. The shot has a raw, unprocessed look: no filters, no color grading, no post‑processing aesthetic. A hint of slight overexposure from ambient daylight gives it a natural warmth.

- **Motion & Focus**  
  Slight motion blur from handheld instability, but everything remains fully sharp — no bokeh, no depth‑of‑field blur. The background is completely in focus, giving a wide‑angle, short‑focal‑length feel.

- **Environment**  
  Specific real‑world location: a rocky outcrop in a dry, grassy field with scattered shrubs. Time of day: mid‑afternoon, with bright, high‑angle sunlight and a few fluffy white clouds drifting across a blue sky. Ambient conditions: warm, slightly breezy, with natural light that casts soft shadows.

- **Lens & Technical**  
  Short focal length (≈20 mm), wide‑angle lens. No studio lighting, no professional composition. All elements — subject, rocks, vegetation, sky — are in focus. Auto‑HDR or auto‑beauty mode off. The image retains a natural, raw aesthetic with slight overexposure or underexposure depending on ambient light.
