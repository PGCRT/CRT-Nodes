**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

  Shot with a wide‑angle lens (24–35 mm) at a short focal length, ensuring that the background remains fully sharp—no bokeh or depth‑of‑field blur. - **Composition & Style**  
  The composition is spontaneous and unposed—no rule‑of‑thirds intentionality. The frame is slightly tilted, and the crop is partial, cutting off the left side of her face and the right side of the sofa. The background is fully sharp, with no “rule‑of‑thirds” alignment; it’s a raw, handheld snapshot that feels natural and candid.

- **Post‑Processing**  
  No filters, color grading, or post‑processing aesthetic is applied. The image retains its original tones and textures, with only minimal adjustments to exposure and contrast.
