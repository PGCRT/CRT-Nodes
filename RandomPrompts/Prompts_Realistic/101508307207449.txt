**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework – “Neon‑Brewery Portrait”**

- **Subject & Pose**  
  A young woman with long, dark, wavy hair sits on a worn leather couch in a dimly lit, industrial‑style brewery. She’s mid‑gesture, hands framing her face as if she’re about to speak or adjust her glasses. Her left arm bears a detailed portrait tattoo of a woman’s face, surrounded by leaf motifs. She wears a black crop‑top and a thin gold bracelet on her right wrist; a small earring glints in her ear. Her makeup is natural, with subtle eyeliner and a hint of red lipstick.

- **Lighting & Exposure**  
  Ambient night‑time lighting from a single overhead neon sign (“RAI”) casts a soft, warm glow on the subject. Slight overexposure on the upper half of the frame gives a raw, unprocessed look. No auto‑beauty or filter enhancement – just natural contrast and slight color cast from the neon.

- **Camera & Lens**  
  35 mm wide‑angle lens (short focal length), handheld, slightly shaky. Everything in focus – no bokeh, no portrait‑mode depth‑of‑field blur. The background is fully sharp, including the textured concrete wall and the leather couch’s tufted surface.

- **Composition & Framing**  
  Candid, unposed, spontaneous capture. Slight tilt and off‑center framing; the shot is partially cropped on the right side by a cut‑off neon letter. No rule‑of‑thirds intentionality – it’s an accidental, natural composition that feels “in the moment.”

- **Environment**  
  Location: a dimly lit, industrial‑style brewery with exposed brick and concrete walls. Time of day: late evening (night). Ambient conditions: cool air, faint hum of background music, slight moisture in the air that gives a subtle grain to the image.

- **Technical Details**  
  - Focal length: 35 mm  
  - Aperture: f/4.0 (wide, all‑in‑focus)  
  - Shutter speed: 1/60 s (slight motion blur from handheld)  
  - ISO: 800 (raw, unprocessed)  
  - No post‑processing color grading – just auto‑HDR off, natural contrast.
