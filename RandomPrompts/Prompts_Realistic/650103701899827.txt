**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

- **Subject & Pose**  
  A young woman with long, wavy brown hair that catches the light as she turns her head slightly to the side. She’s bare‑shouldered, wearing a simple black skirt that hangs loosely at the hips. Her posture is natural, as if she’re mid‑walk or mid‑turn, with a faint tilt in her shoulders and a slight off‑center crop that gives the shot a spontaneous feel.

- **Lighting & Exposure**  
  Warm, golden hour sunlight from the west casts a soft overexposure on her face and upper body, creating subtle highlights in her hair and skin. The exposure is slightly uneven—her chest is a little brighter than her shoulders—giving the image an authentic, handheld look. No auto‑beauty or filter enhancement; the raw, unprocessed feel comes from natural lens characteristics.

- **Lens & Technical Settings**  
  Use a wide‑angle lens (24–35 mm) with a short focal length to capture a broad view of the scene. Keep everything in focus—no bokeh or depth‑of‑field blur; the background, a lush green park with a tree and a faint path, remains sharp. Shoot at a high ISO to preserve detail in the ambient light, and use a tripod or handheld stability that’s almost imperceptible.

- **Environment**  
  The setting is a sunlit park in late afternoon, with a large tree on the right side of the frame and a paved pathway running along the left. The time of day is around 6 p.m., when the light is warm but not too harsh. The weather is clear, with a light breeze that ruffles her hair slightly.

- **Composition & Framing**  
  Capture the shot with no rule‑of‑thirds intentionality—frame the subject so that her head sits just above the center of the frame, and her left shoulder extends near the edge, creating a casual, candid feel. The background is fully sharp, and the composition is accidental, as if she were mid‑action and the photographer was in motion.

- **Post‑Processing**  
  Keep the image raw: no color grading or filters. Slight overexposure on the subject’s chest gives it a natural glow. The final look should feel like a genuine, spontaneous photograph taken with a wide‑angle lens in natural light.
