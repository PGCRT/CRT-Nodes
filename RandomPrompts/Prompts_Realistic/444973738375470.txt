**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

- **Subject & Pose**  
  A young woman with a high ponytail, wearing a light gray racerback tank and frayed denim shorts, stands by a large window, holding a glass beer bottle in her right hand. Her back is slightly turned toward the camera, head tilted to the left as if she’s about to say something or look at a reflection in the glass. The tank shows a subtle black strap on the left side, and a faint green lens flare sits near her shoulder.

- **Motion & Focus**  
  Slight motion blur around the hands and bottle, indicating handheld instability. Everything is fully sharp—no portrait‑mode blur, no depth‑of‑field isolation. The image has a raw, unprocessed look: no filters, no color grading, no post‑processing aesthetic. A tiny overexposure at the top right corner from the window light.

- **Lens & Technical**  
  Wide‑angle lens (24–35 mm), short focal length. No bokeh; background is fully sharp. The composition is accidental—no rule‑of‑thirds intentionality, slight tilt and off‑center framing, partial crop that cuts off the left side of the window frame.

- **Environment**  
  Location: a bright apartment balcony with a large glass window that reflects another woman in a light‑colored dress holding a bottle. The day is mid‑afternoon, the sun high, creating strong natural illumination and a small green flare near the subject’s shoulder. Light is slightly overexposed at the top right. Weather is clear, no wind.

- **Candid Elements**  
  The woman’s left hand rests on her hip, her right hand gripping the bottle as if she’s about to take a sip. Her hair is tied up in a messy ponytail, a few strands escaping. A small black strap of a tank top peeks from under the back of her shirt. The denim shorts have frayed edges, adding texture.

- **Details & Imperfections**  
  Slight tilt of the camera, off‑center framing that cuts the left side of the window frame. Partial crop at the bottom right corner where a small wooden stool and a pink cloth are visible. A faint green lens flare is present near the subject’s shoulder. The image has natural motion blur from handheld shooting.
