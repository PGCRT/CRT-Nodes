**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

- **Subject & Pose**  
  A young woman with long, wavy brown hair tied back with a black clip, wearing an off‑white hooded sweatshirt and ripped blue jeans. She is seated on a concrete sidewalk, leaning forward slightly while holding a smartphone in both hands, her face turned toward the phone’s screen as if she’s texting or taking a photo. Her expression is mid‑smile, eyes focused on the device, giving a spontaneous, “in‑the‑moment” feel.

- **Motion & Focus**  
  Slight motion blur at the fingertips and hair, indicating handheld instability. Everything in the frame — subject, phone, background — is fully sharp with no portrait‑mode blur. The blur is subtle, a gentle “breathing” effect that keeps the image raw and unprocessed.

- **Lighting & Exposure**  
  Natural daylight from a high‑angle, mid‑afternoon sun. A slight overexposure on the subject’s face, with a warm, golden hue that suggests the sun is slightly above the horizon. No color grading or filters — just the raw tones of the scene.

- **Lens & Technical**  
  Wide‑angle lens (24–35 mm), short focal length, no bokeh. The background is fully sharp, with no depth‑of‑field blur. The composition is accidental: a slight tilt to the left, off‑center framing that captures the woman and part of the street behind her.

- **Environment**  
  A busy city sidewalk in an urban neighborhood. A silver sedan is parked on the street behind her, a black motorcycle is partially visible further back, and a passerby in a black jacket walks by on the right. The scene includes a concrete curb, a metal trash can, a blue soda can lying on the ground near the woman’s right foot, and a white piece of paper or napkin next to her left knee. The time of day is late afternoon, with long shadows cast across the sidewalk.

- **Details & Imperfections**  
  The prompt emphasizes candidness: the woman’s hands are slightly curled around the phone, her fingers barely touching the screen. A faint tilt in the frame gives a natural, unposed feel. The background includes a traffic light and a green tree, adding depth without any intentional rule‑of‑thirds placement.
