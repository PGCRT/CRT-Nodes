**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

- **Subject & Pose**  
  A young woman with long, wavy blonde hair lying on a white bed, wearing a sheer gray bra and matching underwear that reads “Tusty.com” on the waistband. Her left hand rests near her hip, fingers slightly curled, while her right hand supports her head in a relaxed, casual pose. She looks directly at the camera with a soft, confident expression, eyes wide and lips parted slightly.

- **Lighting & Exposure**  
  Natural daylight from a window on the left, creating a gentle side‑light that produces slight overexposure on the face and upper body. The exposure is slightly uneven—warm highlights on the skin and cool shadows in the background—giving a raw, unprocessed look. No auto‑HDR or beauty filters; just a single shutter click.

- **Camera & Lens Settings**  
  - Lens: 24 mm wide‑angle (short focal length) for a broad field of view.  
  - Aperture: f/5.6 – everything from the subject to the background stays fully sharp, no bokeh.  
  - Shutter speed: 1/125 s – enough to freeze the motion but with a hint of handheld shake.  
  - ISO: 400 – for balanced noise and detail.

- **Composition & Framing**  
  The shot is intentionally off‑center, with the subject’s head slightly above the left edge of the frame and her legs extending toward the right corner. The composition is spontaneous—no rule‑of‑thirds intentionality; the framing feels accidental, as if the photographer caught a candid moment. The bed takes up most of the lower half of the frame, while a white lamp and a small side table with a mug appear in the upper right corner.

- **Environment**  
  A bright, airy bedroom in the late morning, with soft natural light streaming through a window. The walls are painted a neutral off‑white, and a white pillow is tucked behind the subject’s head. A small wooden nightstand holds a white lamp and a pink mug; a cup of tea or coffee is visible on the table. The room appears lived‑in, not staged—slight dust motes float in the air, adding to the candid feel.

- **Technical Details**  
  - No depth‑of‑field blur: background fully sharp.  
  - Slight motion blur on the subject’s hand and hair, indicating handheld instability.  
  - All elements are in focus—subject, pillow, lamp, nightstand—captured with a single exposure.

- **Prompt Summary**  
  “A candid, wide‑angle shot of a blonde woman in a sheer gray bra and underwear lying on a white bed, captured in natural daylight with slight overexposure. The 24 mm lens keeps everything sharp while a handheld shake adds subtle motion blur. The frame is off‑center and spontaneous, featuring a white lamp and a side table with a mug in the background. No filters or post‑processing—just raw, realistic photography.”
