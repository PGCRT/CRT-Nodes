**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

- **Subject & Pose**  
  A young woman with long, dark hair, wearing a light‑pink, short‑sleeved crop top and matching high‑waist shorts. A faint motion blur at the wrists and hips suggests natural movement.

- **Lighting & Exposure**  
  Ambient daylight from a high‑angle, midday sun. Slight overexposure on the upper torso, creating a warm, soft glow. No auto‑beauty or filter enhancement—raw, unprocessed look. The exposure is uneven: the left side of the image is slightly brighter than the right, giving an organic feel.

- **Camera Settings**  
  Wide‑angle lens (24 mm), f/3.5, 1/125 s shutter speed, ISO 400. Handheld, slight instability causing a gentle blur on the edges. Everything is in focus—no portrait‑mode depth of field blur; background remains sharp.

  Candid, unposed framing: the subject is off‑center, slightly tilted. The crop cuts off part of her left arm and the right side of her shorts, giving a spontaneous feel. No rule‑of‑thirds intentionality—accidental composition. The background shows a plain concrete wall with a faint texture, a small green plant on the right, and a bright window on the left.

  Location: an urban loft space in a residential building. Time of day: early afternoon, sun high but not directly overhead. Weather: clear, mild humidity. A small potted plant sits in the corner; a faint reflection of a ceiling light is visible.

- **Lens & Technical Details**  
  Short focal length (24 mm) for wide coverage. No bokeh—background fully sharp. All elements are in focus, from the subject’s face to the distant wall. No “portrait mode” isolation; the entire scene is crisp.

- **Final Prompt**  
  *“A young woman with long dark hair, wearing a light‑pink crop top and high‑waist shorts, captured mid‑step in a candid, unposed shot.
