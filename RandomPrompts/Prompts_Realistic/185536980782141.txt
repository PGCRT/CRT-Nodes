  Wide‑angle lens (24–35 mm) captured at a short focal length; everything in focus from foreground to background. Slight handheld shake produces a natural motion blur on the edges, while the center remains crisp. No bokeh or depth‑of‑field blur—background is fully sharp.

  Imperfect, off‑center framing: the subject is slightly tilted, and the crop cuts off part of the doorframe on the left, giving a spontaneous, candid feel. No rule‑of‑thirds intentionality—accidental composition. The shot captures a real‑world location (a bedroom with a wooden floor, a small side table with a vase of flowers, and a white door), time of day mid‑afternoon, ambient warmth.

  The room has a pale yellow wall, a wooden floor, and a small side table holding a vase of colorful flowers. A white doorframe is visible on the left; a light switch is seen on the right wall. The background is fully sharp, no studio lighting, no professional composition, no editorial tone.
