**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

- **Subject & Pose**  
  A young woman with deep red, half‑tied ponytail and bangs, wearing a black spaghetti‑strap mini dress with a cut‑out at the waist. She’s holding a gray smartphone in both hands, looking down at the screen as if texting or browsing. Her left wrist is adorned with a gold bracelet and a thin gold necklace; a ring sits on her right hand. The pose is candid—she’s mid‑action, slightly tilted, off‑center, as if caught in a spontaneous moment.

- **Lighting & Exposure**  
  Natural daylight from a bright, diffused window (mid‑afternoon). Slight overexposure on the face and upper body, giving a warm, raw feel. No auto‑HDR or auto‑beauty—just a straightforward exposure that keeps everything in focus. The background is a plain off‑white wall with a subtle texture, no bokeh, no depth‑of‑field blur.

- **Camera & Lens**  
  Wide‑angle lens (24–35 mm). Short focal length to keep the entire scene sharp. Handheld shot with slight instability, producing a natural motion blur on the subject’s hands and phone. All elements—subject, phone, jewelry, background—are fully in focus.

- **Composition & Framing**  
  No rule‑of‑thirds intentionality; the frame is accidental, with the subject slightly cropped at the top and right. The shot captures a “real” moment: the woman’s fingers are mid‑touch on the phone, her head is tilted downwards, and her left shoulder is slightly turned toward the camera.

- **Environment**  
  Location: A bright, uncluttered living room with a white wall and a light wooden floor. Time of day: late afternoon, soft natural light from a large window. Weather: clear, no wind—only the subtle motion blur from the subject’s movement.

- **Technical Details**  
  - Focal length: 35 mm  
  - Aperture: f/4 (wide, shallow depth but all elements stay sharp)  
  - Shutter speed: 1/60 s (captures slight motion blur)  
  - ISO: 400 (for natural contrast)  
  - No filters, no color grading, no post‑processing.  
  - Auto‑beauty mode off; raw image with minimal noise.

- **Final Prompt**  
  “A candid, unposed photograph of a woman with deep red half‑tied ponytail and bangs, wearing a black spaghetti‑strap mini dress with a waist cut‑out, holding a gray smartphone in both hands while looking down at the screen. She wears a gold bracelet on her left wrist, a thin gold necklace, and a ring on her right hand. The shot is taken with a 35 mm wide‑angle lens at f/4 and 1/60 s shutter speed, capturing slight motion blur from handheld instability.
