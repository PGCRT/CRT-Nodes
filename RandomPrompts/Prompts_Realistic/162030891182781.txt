**Candid, Unposed Photography Prompt Framework**

- **Subject & Pose**  
  A woman with deep‑brown, voluminous curls cascading over her left shoulder, wearing a sleeveless, high‑waist jumpsuit in a soft beige tone. The jumpsuit features delicate gold embroidery around the V‑neck and a subtle seam at the waist. Her right hand rests lightly on her hip, fingers slightly curled, while her left arm hangs relaxed by her side. She is mid‑step, one foot forward, capturing a natural, spontaneous motion.

- **Motion & Focus**  
  Slight handheld shake with a 35 mm wide‑angle lens (f/2.8). The image has a subtle motion blur on the subject’s hand and background, indicating a 1/60 s shutter speed. Everything remains in focus—no portrait‑mode depth of field blur; the background is fully sharp.

- **Lighting & Exposure**  
  Natural daylight from a high‑angle, late‑afternoon sun. The light hits the subject’s left side, creating a gentle highlight on the fabric’s front and a slight overexposure on the upper chest. No auto‑HDR or beauty filters—raw, unprocessed look.

- **Environment**  
  Outdoor setting in a modern residential courtyard: a white stucco wall on the left, a glass door, and a patch of green shrubbery on the right. A palm tree’s fronds peek from the top right corner. The ground is a light concrete slab with faint cracks, adding texture.

- **Composition & Framing**  
  No rule‑of‑thirds intentionality—framing is accidental, slightly off‑center. The shot is cropped tight around the subject’s torso to mid‑calf, with the top of her head cut off at the upper edge. The camera is held at a slight tilt, giving an informal, candid feel.

- **Technical Specs**  
  - Lens: 35 mm, wide‑angle, f/2.8  
  - Shutter speed: 1/60 s (slight motion blur)  
  - Aperture: f/2.8 (wide, shallow depth but full sharpness)  
  - ISO: 400 (natural contrast)  
  - No bokeh; background fully sharp.

- **Post‑Processing**  
  Zero filters, zero color grading, no auto‑beauty mode. Raw file with minimal editing—only basic white‑balance correction and a slight contrast boost.
