**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

- **Subject & Pose**  
  A tall, curvy woman with long, wavy dark hair and golden hoop earrings, mid‑step in a casual, spontaneous pose. Her right hand is slightly raised near her shoulder, fingers curled as if she’s about to grasp something. She wears tight, dark‑brown faux‑leather pants that cling to her hips and thighs, and a bare‑shoulder top that shows off her large, rounded breasts. Her face is turned toward the camera with a subtle smile, eyes bright and engaged.

- **Capture Style**  
  Candid, unposed, spontaneous — the subject appears unaware of the camera or mid‑action. Slight tilt and off‑center framing give an accidental, handheld feel. The shot has a raw, unprocessed look: no filters, no color grading, no post‑processing aesthetic. A mild overexposure in the highlights gives a natural, ambient‑light glow.

- **Motion & Focus**  
  Slight motion blur on the subject’s hand and background to simulate handheld instability. Everything remains fully sharp — no portrait‑mode depth‑of‑field blur, no bokeh. The focus is broad, capturing the entire scene in one go.

- **Lens & Technical**  
  Wide‑angle lens (24–35 mm) with a short focal length. No bokeh or subject isolation; background stays fully sharp. Auto‑HDR or auto‑beauty mode off. Slight overexposure or underexposure depending on ambient light — here, a gentle warm tint from early‑afternoon sunlight.

- **Environment**  
  Location: A sunlit hallway in a modern residential building, with a textured beige wall and a framed picture hanging on the right. The time of day is late morning, around 10:30 AM, with soft, diffused natural light. Ambient conditions are mild, with a slight breeze that creates a faint motion blur on the subject’s hair.

- **Composition Notes**  
  No rule‑of‑thirds intentionality — the framing is accidental and slightly off‑center. The shot captures a partial crop of the woman’s lower body and the background wall, giving a casual, spontaneous vibe. All elements — the woman, her clothing, the wall, the framed picture, and a small portion of a wooden bench — are in focus.
