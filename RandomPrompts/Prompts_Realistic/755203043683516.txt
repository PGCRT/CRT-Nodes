**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

- **Subject & Pose**  
  A tall, curvy woman with long, dark wavy hair standing in a casual, unposed stance. She’s wearing black bikini bottoms, her hands resting lightly on her hips, body slightly angled toward the camera but not perfectly centered. Her face is turned to the camera with a natural, unforced smile, eyes bright and relaxed. The shot should feel spontaneous, as if she’re mid‑pose or just finished a movement—slight motion blur at the edges to emphasize handheld capture.

- **Lighting & Exposure**  
  Use a wide‑angle lens (20–35 mm) with a short focal length to keep the background fully sharp and the subject in full frame. Shoot in natural daylight, perhaps late afternoon, so the light is warm but not harsh—slight overexposure or underexposure depending on ambient light. Turn off any auto‑HDR or auto‑beauty modes; aim for a raw, unprocessed look with no filters, color grading, or post‑processing aesthetic.

- **Composition & Framing**  
  Capture in a candid, unposed manner—no rule‑of‑thirds intentionality. Let the framing be accidental: slight tilt, off‑center cropping, and a partial crop that gives a sense of spontaneity. Keep everything in focus; no portrait mode or subject isolation. The background should be a specific real‑world location (e.g., a sunlit urban plaza or a quiet beach promenade), with the time of day (mid‑afternoon or early evening) and weather/ambient conditions (clear, mild breeze, soft wind) adding depth.

- **Technical Settings**  
  - Lens: 24 mm wide‑angle, f/4.0, ISO 400, shutter speed 1/125 s (slight motion blur).  
  - Focus: everything in focus—no bokeh or depth‑of‑field blur.  
  - Exposure: natural daylight, slight overexposure to keep the scene bright and lively.  
  - Post‑processing: none—raw, unprocessed, no auto‑HDR or color grading.

- **Environment**  
  The setting is a quiet urban plaza at mid‑afternoon; the background includes a concrete wall and a small fountain, with a faint hint of greenery on the side. The weather is clear with a gentle breeze, giving a soft, natural ambience. The time of day gives a warm, golden hue to the scene.

- **Final Prompt**  
  “A tall woman with long dark wavy hair, wearing black bikini bottoms, stands casually in a sunlit urban plaza at mid‑afternoon. She’s mid‑pose, hands on hips, slightly angled toward the camera with a natural smile. Capture with a 24 mm wide‑angle lens at f/4.0, ISO 400, 1/125 s shutter speed for slight motion blur. Shoot in natural daylight with a slight overexposure, no filters or auto‑HDR, everything in focus. Frame accidentally—slight tilt and off‑center crop—keeping the background fully sharp. The scene includes a concrete wall and a small fountain, with a faint greenery on the side, under clear weather conditions.”
