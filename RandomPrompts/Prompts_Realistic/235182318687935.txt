**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

- **Subject & Pose**  
  A tall, athletic woman with long, sun‑kissed blonde hair standing on a concrete rooftop at golden hour. She wears a light‑washed denim jacket with distressed seams and a black Calvin Klein bikini bottom; her hands rest lightly at her sides, slightly angled to one side. Her face is turned toward the camera but not directly aimed—mid‑expression, as if she’s just finished a workout or is about to step into a new rhythm.

- **Capture Style**  
  Candid, unposed, spontaneous — subject unaware or mid‑action. Slight tilt and off‑center framing; partial crop that gives a raw, handheld feel. No rule‑of‑thirds intentionality—everything is framed by the natural lines of the rooftop and the city behind.

- **Environment**  
  Specific real‑world location: an urban rooftop with a white railing, a low‑rise residential building in the background, and a small patch of green lawn on the side. Time of day: late afternoon, ambient light warm and soft, slight overexposure on the subject’s face to emphasize the golden hue. Weather: clear, no wind, but a faint breeze is suggested by the slight motion blur on the hair.

- **Lens & Technical**  
  Short focal length (24‑35 mm), wide‑angle lens. No bokeh—background fully sharp, everything in focus. No portrait mode or subject isolation; depth of field is uniform. Slight motion blur from handheld instability, giving a natural, live‑capture feel. Auto‑HDR or auto‑beauty mode off — raw, unprocessed look. No filters, no color grading, no post‑processing aesthetic.

- **Composition Notes**  
  The composition is accidental: the subject’s left side is slightly cut off by the frame edge, creating a dynamic, imperfect crop. The railing runs horizontally across the mid‑background, but it’s not perfectly centered. The lighting comes from the upper right, casting soft shadows on her torso and face.

- **Prompt for Text‑to‑Image Generation**
