**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework – “Summer Evening on the Balcony”**

- **Subject & Pose**  
  A young woman with a slender, athletic build, wearing a light‑colored, thin‑strap bikini top, standing on a wooden balcony. Her left leg is bent at the knee, foot tucked into a relaxed, mid‑step position, while her right arm hangs naturally by her side. She’s turning slightly toward the camera, capturing a candid, unposed moment as if she’re about to tie a scarf or adjust her hair.

- **Lighting & Exposure**  
  Warm, golden hour light from the west—slight overexposure on the upper body, giving a soft glow to the skin. The exposure is raw, no auto‑beauty mode: a handheld shot with a slight instability that gives a natural motion blur around the edges.

- **Camera & Lens**  
  35 mm wide‑angle lens (short focal length), fully sharp background—no bokeh. Everything in focus from foreground to background; no portrait‑mode depth‑of‑field blur. Slight tilt and off‑center framing, accidental composition.

- **Environment**  
  A rustic wooden balcony with a brick wall on the right side, a sheer white curtain hanging on the left. The time of day is late afternoon (golden hour), with a faint breeze moving the curtains. Ambient noise from the city outside is visible through the window, adding to the candid feel.

- **Details & Imperfections**  
  Slight motion blur at the knees and feet, indicating a mid‑action freeze. A tiny smudge on her left shoulder strap, a slight tilt of the camera, and a partial crop that cuts off the top of her head—makes it look spontaneous. The background brickwork is fully sharp, no rule‑of‑thirds intentionality.

- **Post‑Processing**  
  Zero filters, zero color grading, zero studio lighting. Raw, unprocessed look with natural contrast. A subtle auto‑HDR tweak only on the high‑dynamic‑range of the skin tones.
