**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

- **Subject & Pose**  
  A young woman with long, wind‑blown dark hair standing on a sunlit beach dune. She’s wearing a black bikini top and frayed denim shorts, accessorized with gold hoop earrings, a chunky gold chain necklace, and several gold bracelets. Her hands are gently pulling her top slightly upward, as if she’s adjusting it mid‑step.  

- **Motion & Focus**  
  Slight motion blur on the hair and hands to convey movement; everything in the frame is fully sharp—no bokeh, no depth‑of‑field blur. The image has a raw, unprocessed look: no filters, no color grading, no post‑processing aesthetic.  

- **Lighting & Exposure**  
  Natural daylight from a high‑angle, mid‑afternoon sun creates a soft overexposure on the subject’s face and a subtle shadow cast by the dune grass. The exposure is slightly warm, with a hint of ambient haze.  

- **Environment**  
  Specific real‑world location: a sandy beach with tall sea‑grass tufts on either side, a shallow water line in the background, and a clear blue sky. Time of day: late afternoon, around 4 pm, giving a golden‑hour glow. Weather: breezy, with light wind ruffling the grass and the woman’s hair.  

- **Lens & Technical**  
  Short focal length (20–35 mm) wide‑angle lens, handheld shot with a slightly tilted camera to give an accidental, off‑center framing. No rule‑of‑thirds intentionality—everything is in focus, background fully sharp.  

- **Composition Notes**  
  Candid, unposed, spontaneous — the subject appears unaware of the camera, mid‑action. The frame captures a partial crop of her lower body and the sand beneath her feet, with a slight tilt that gives a natural, informal feel.
