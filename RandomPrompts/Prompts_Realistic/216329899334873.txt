**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

- **Subject & Pose**  
  A woman with long, dark, wavy hair standing on a sandy beach, wearing a sheer black bikini. She is mid‑turn, slightly angled toward the camera, with one hand resting near her hip and the other hanging by her side. Her left arm bears a rose tattoo that is partially visible. She wears gold hoop earrings and a ring on her left ring finger. Her expression is neutral, almost candid, as if she’s caught in a natural moment.

- **Lighting & Exposure**  
  Slight overexposure from the mid‑afternoon sun, creating soft highlights on her skin and a subtle halo around her hair. No auto‑beauty or filter enhancement—raw, unprocessed look. The exposure is slightly warm, with a gentle haze that gives an ambient, natural feel.

- **Motion & Focus**  
  Slight motion blur from handheld shooting, giving a sense of movement. Everything in the frame—from her face to the waves behind—is fully sharp, no portrait‑mode depth‑of‑field blur. The focus is intentional but not staged; the composition feels accidental, with a slight tilt and off‑center framing.

- **Lens & Technical**  
  Wide‑angle lens (24–35 mm), short focal length. No bokeh—background (the ocean and sky) remains fully sharp. No rule‑of‑thirds intentionality; the frame is a spontaneous, casual crop that captures the subject’s full figure and part of the beach environment.

- **Environment**  
  Specific real‑world location: a sunny beach with gentle waves breaking in the background. Time of day: mid‑afternoon, around 3 pm, when the light is warm but not harsh. Weather: clear, with a few scattered clouds; the sky is a pale blue with a hint of white. The sand is light brown, slightly textured, and the beach is unpaved, giving an authentic feel.

- **Capture Style**  
  Candid, unposed, spontaneous—subject unaware or mid‑action. Imperfections encouraged: slight tilt, off‑center framing, partial crop. No rule‑of‑thirds intentionality — accidental framing. Zero studio lighting, zero professional composition, zero editorial tone. Natural motion freeze or slight blur from handheld instability.

- **Post‑Processing**  
  Auto‑HDR or auto‑beauty mode off—raw, unprocessed look. Slight overexposure or underexposure depending on ambient light. No filters, no color grading, no post‑processing aesthetic.
