**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

- **Subject & Action**  
  A young woman with long, sun‑kissed blonde hair, wearing a tight black long‑sleeved V‑neck top and light denim shorts, is mid‑gesture while holding a small object (a piece of food or a toy) between her fingers. Her head is tilted slightly downward, eyes focused on the object, mouth slightly open as if she’s about to speak or laugh. She wears a small stud earring in her left ear.  

- **Pose & Composition**  
  Candid, unposed, spontaneous — the subject appears unaware of the camera. The frame is off‑center and slightly tilted, with a partial crop that cuts off the left side of her torso, giving an accidental, handheld feel. No rule‑of‑thirds intentionality; the composition is driven by the natural line of the subject’s body and the background elements.  

- **Motion & Focus**  
  Slight motion blur on the hands and object, indicating a handheld shot captured at a fast shutter speed but with a little instability. Everything in the frame — subject, background, and foreground — is fully sharp; no portrait‑mode depth‑of‑field blur.  

- **Lighting & Exposure**  
  Natural daylight from a high‑angle window, creating a soft, warm glow on the subject’s face and body. A slight overexposure on the upper chest area gives a raw, unprocessed look. No auto‑HDR or beauty filters; the image retains the genuine ambience of the moment.  

- **Environment**  
  The setting is a bright, lived‑in bedroom or studio: a white dresser with drawers is visible to the left, a small potted plant sits on a shelf, and a stack of colorful books lines the back wall. A framed picture hangs on the wall behind her, slightly out of focus but still visible. The time of day is late afternoon, with warm, golden light streaming through a window, casting soft shadows.  

- **Lens & Technical**  
  Short focal length (24‑35 mm) wide‑angle lens, no bokeh. All elements — subject, background, foreground — are in focus. The lens is set to a wide aperture but the depth of field is shallow enough that the entire scene remains crisp.  

- **Style & Aesthetic**  
  Zero studio lighting, zero professional composition, zero editorial tone. The image feels spontaneous and real, with slight imperfections (a tiny smudge on the shirt, a faint dust particle in the air) that add to its authenticity.
