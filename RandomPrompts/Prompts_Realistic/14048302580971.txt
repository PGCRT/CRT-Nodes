**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

- **Subject & Pose**  
  A woman lying on a white bed, mid‑stretch, wearing a thin white thong and black leggings. Her hips are slightly turned, knees bent, legs spread wide, capturing a natural, unposed moment of relaxed movement. The camera is positioned low, almost at the level of her buttocks, creating an intimate, close‑up view that feels spontaneous and candid.

- **Lighting & Exposure**  
  Slight overexposure from natural daylight coming from a south‑west window; the exposure is a little bright but not blown out. No auto‑beauty or filter processing—just raw, unedited light. The scene has a warm, slightly hazy ambience with subtle ambient glare.

- **Motion & Focus**  
  A gentle motion blur from handheld shooting: the subject’s skin and the white thong strings have a slight streaking effect, indicating a quick shutter speed but still capturing the full frame in focus. Everything—from her hips to the edges of the bed—remains sharp, with no portrait‑mode depth‑of‑field blur.

- **Lens & Technical**  
  24 mm wide‑angle lens (short focal length), no bokeh. The background—the white sheets and a faint gray blanket in the corner—is fully sharp, giving a “everything in focus” feel. No rule‑of‑thirds intentionality; the frame is slightly tilted and off‑center, as if captured by a casual photographer on the go.

- **Environment**  
  A bright, sunlit bedroom in the late afternoon. The time of day is around 4:30 pm, with warm natural light casting soft shadows. A gray blanket drapes loosely at the top right corner of the bed, adding texture and a subtle contrast to the white sheets.

- **Composition & Details**  
  The composition is accidental and spontaneous: the shot is a close‑up, partial crop that cuts off the top of the woman’s torso and the lower part of her legs, emphasizing the mid‑body and the white thong’s straps. Slight tilt and off‑center framing give the image a dynamic, informal feel. The white thong’s straps create a subtle “Y” shape at the center of the frame, while the black leggings’ zipper is visible at the bottom right, adding a small detail that anchors the composition.

- **Post‑Processing**  
  Zero filters, zero color grading, zero studio lighting. The image retains its raw, unprocessed look, with only minimal adjustments to exposure and contrast in post‑production.  

**Prompt Summary**  
> “A candid, low‑angle shot of a woman’s hips and lower torso on a white bed, captured with a 24 mm wide‑angle lens. The scene is lit by warm late‑afternoon sunlight, producing slight overexposure and motion blur from handheld shooting. The subject is mid‑stretch, wearing a white thong and black leggings, with a gray blanket peeking from the corner. Everything is in focus, the frame is slightly tilted and off‑center, creating an accidental, spontaneous composition without rule‑of‑thirds.”
