**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

- **Subject & Pose**  
  A woman with long, wavy brown hair, wearing a bright orange bikini, crouching low on the sand. Her left hand rests on her knee, right hand clasped near her wrist, and she wears a gold watch on her left ankle. A delicate tattoo is visible on her left forearm. She smiles slightly, looking toward the camera with a natural, candid expression.

- **Motion & Focus**  
  Slight motion blur from handheld shooting; everything in focus—no portrait‑mode depth of field. The image has a raw, unprocessed look: no filters, no color grading, no post‑processing aesthetic. A hint of overexposure from the bright midday sun.

- **Lens & Technical**  
  Wide‑angle lens (24–35 mm), short focal length. No bokeh—background fully sharp. Slight tilt and off‑center framing; partial crop to give a spontaneous, unposed feel.

- **Environment**  
  Beach at midday, the sand is light beige with small pebbles. The ocean is visible in the background with gentle white waves breaking. A second woman in a patterned bikini stands further back, slightly out of focus but still sharp. The sky is pale blue with a faint haze from the sun’s intensity.

- **Lighting & Exposure**  
  Natural daylight, slight overexposure on the subject’s face and upper body; ambient light creates soft highlights on the sand and skin. No studio lighting, no professional composition—just a casual, spontaneous snapshot.

- **Composition Notes**  
  The frame is slightly tilted, the subject is positioned off‑center to the right, the lower part of the image cuts off near the bottom edge, giving an accidental, candid feel. The background is fully sharp, with the second woman and ocean visible but not dominating the composition.

- **Final Prompt**  
  “A woman in an orange bikini crouching on a sunny beach, mid‑day sun creating slight overexposure, captured with a wide‑angle short focal length lens. Her left hand rests on her knee, right hand near her wrist, gold watch on ankle, tattoo on forearm visible. Slight motion blur from handheld shot, everything in focus, no filters or color grading. Background shows another woman in a patterned bikini standing further back near the water, ocean waves and pale blue sky. The frame is slightly tilted and off‑center, partial crop at bottom, natural candid composition.”
