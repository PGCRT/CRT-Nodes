**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

- **Subject & Pose**  
  A young woman with a high ponytail and bangs, wearing a white crop‑top and a short leather jacket over a white mini‑skirt, walking confidently down a city street. Her hands are relaxed at her sides, slightly bent, as if she’s mid‑step.  

- **Motion & Focus**  
  Slight motion blur on the subject’s legs and the background to convey movement, but everything remains fully sharp—no portrait‑mode softening. The lens is a short focal‑length wide‑angle (24–35 mm) with a wide aperture that keeps the entire scene in focus.  

- **Lighting & Exposure**  
  Natural daylight from a low‑angle sunrise or sunset; the light is slightly overexposed on the subject’s face, giving a warm golden hue, while the background retains subtle contrast. No auto‑HDR or beauty filters—just raw, unprocessed imagery.  

- **Environment**  
  A bustling urban alleyway lined with brick buildings, a concrete sidewalk, and a parked white car in the background. The time of day is early evening, with long shadows and a soft ambient glow. Light rain or drizzle may be present, adding a subtle sheen to the pavement.  

- **Composition & Framing**  
  No rule‑of‑thirds intentionality—frame the shot loosely, letting the subject occupy the central‑left portion of the frame with a slight tilt. The composition is accidental and candid; the edges cut off the street’s corner, giving a partial crop feel.  

- **Technical Settings**  
  - Focal length: 30 mm (wide angle)  
  - Aperture: f/2.8 (wide, but not too wide—background stays sharp)  
  - Shutter speed: 1/60 s (enough to capture slight motion blur)  
  - ISO: 400–800 (depending on ambient light)  
  - No bokeh or depth‑of‑field blur—everything from foreground to background is in focus.  

- **Post‑Processing**  
  Zero filters, zero color grading, no post‑processing aesthetic—just a raw, natural look with slight overexposure to the subject’s face and a gentle warmth to the entire scene.
