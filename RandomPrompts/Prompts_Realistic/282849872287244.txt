**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

- **Subject & Pose**  
  A tall, athletic woman with long, wavy brown hair standing in a casual, unposed stance. She is wearing white lace underwear and a thin, sheer top that drapes over her shoulders. Her arms are raised above her head, hands clasped together, as if she’s about to tie a scarf or adjust a shirt. Her face is turned slightly toward the camera, with a soft, natural expression. The pose captures a moment of spontaneous movement, with a slight motion blur at the wrists and fingertips to emphasize the handheld feel.

- **Lighting & Exposure**  
  Ambient daylight from a western‑side window creates a warm, slightly overexposed highlight on her chest and shoulder. The exposure is uneven—the background is a bit brighter than the foreground, giving a raw, unprocessed look. No auto‑beauty mode; no filters, no color grading, no post‑processing aesthetic. The lighting gives a subtle halo effect around her hair, with a faint vignette from the wide lens.

- **Lens & Technical**  
  24 mm focal length, wide‑angle lens (wide‑angle “wide‑body” mode). Everything is in focus—no bokeh, no depth‑of‑field blur. The background is fully sharp, a plain light‑blue wall with two white plastic chairs on either side. No rule‑of‑thirds intentionality; the frame is slightly tilted and off‑center, capturing an accidental, candid composition.

- **Environment**  
  A simple, unadorned room with a light‑blue textured wall. The time of day is late morning, with soft, diffused sunlight coming from the left. The ambient light is slightly hazy, as if the window has a thin curtain or a light veil. Two white plastic chairs sit against the wall—one on the left, one on the right—creating a balanced but not symmetrical backdrop.

- **Capture Style**  
  Candid, unposed, spontaneous—the subject appears unaware of the camera mid‑action. Slight tilt and partial crop give an imperfect, natural feel. The image has a raw, in‑camera look with no studio lighting or professional composition. A slight motion blur at the wrists conveys the handheld instability.

- **Detailed Prompt**  
  “A tall woman with long wavy brown hair, wearing white lace underwear and a sheer top, stands casually in a room with a light‑blue wall. Her arms are raised above her head, hands clasped together, as if she’s about to tie a scarf. The scene is lit by late‑morning daylight from the left, creating a warm overexposure on her chest. Shot on a 24 mm wide‑angle lens, everything is in focus—no bokeh. Two white plastic chairs frame the background. The composition is slightly tilted and off‑center, capturing a candid, spontaneous moment with a hint of motion blur at the wrists.”
