**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

- **Subject & Pose**  
  A young woman with long, wavy brown hair walking confidently down a city street. She wears a black strap‑top and distressed gray jeans, her hands relaxed at her sides, mid‑step as if caught in an unplanned moment. Her face is turned slightly toward the camera, a natural smile on her lips, with a faint motion blur around her shoulders to suggest movement.

- **Lighting & Exposure**  
  Warm, golden hour light from the setting sun casts soft highlights on her hair and face. Slight overexposure on the upper part of the frame gives a raw, unprocessed look. No auto‑beauty or filter enhancement—just natural ambient illumination with a hint of warmth and slight color cast.

- **Camera & Lens Settings**  
  Wide‑angle lens (24‑35 mm) with a short focal length to capture a broad view of the scene. Everything is in focus—from the subject to the background—no bokeh or depth‑of‑field blur. Handheld shot with slight tilt and off‑center framing, no rule‑of‑thirds intentionality.

- **Environment**  
  A bustling urban street lined with brick buildings, storefronts, and parked cars. The street is paved, with a white line marking the curb. A traffic light glows amber in the background, and a red awning adds a pop of color. The time of day is late afternoon, just before dusk, giving a soft, diffused glow. Weather is clear, with a light breeze that ruffles the subject’s hair.

- **Composition & Details**  
  The composition is spontaneous: the subject is slightly off‑center to the left, her body angled toward the camera but not perfectly centered. The background includes a row of parked cars, a traffic signal, and a sidewalk with pedestrians in the distance. The shot captures a full‑body view, from head to toe, with the lower part of the frame cutting off just above the knees of the subject’s jeans.

- **Post‑Processing & Finish**  
  No filters or color grading—just raw, natural tones. A subtle auto‑HDR effect is applied only to the highlights, enhancing brightness without losing detail. The final image has a slight overexposure on the upper half, giving it an energetic, candid feel. All elements—the subject, the street, the buildings—are fully sharp, as if captured with a wide‑angle lens that keeps everything in focus.

**Prompt Summary**  
> “A young woman with long wavy brown hair, wearing a black strap‑top and distressed gray jeans, walks confidently down a brick city street at golden hour. The shot is taken with a 24‑35 mm wide‑angle lens, handheld, slightly tilted, off‑center framing, everything in focus. Warm light from the setting sun creates a subtle overexposure on the upper half. The background shows parked cars, a traffic light, and a red awning, with pedestrians in the distance. No filters or color grading—just raw, natural tones with a faint auto‑HDR boost on the highlights.”
