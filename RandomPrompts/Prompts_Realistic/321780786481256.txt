**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

- **Subject & Pose**  
  A young woman with long, wavy blonde hair sitting on a white bed, wearing a sheer white lace bra and matching underwear. Her pose is casual and unposed, as if she just finished a photo session and is still catching her breath.

- **Lighting & Exposure**  
  Natural daylight from a south‑west window, mid‑afternoon (≈ 4 pm). The light is soft but warm, creating a gentle highlight on her left shoulder and a subtle warmth in the background wallpaper. Slight overexposure on the high‑lights gives a raw, unprocessed feel. No auto‑HDR or beauty mode—just a straight RAW capture.

- **Camera & Lens**  
  DSLR or mirrorless with a 24 mm wide‑angle lens (f/3.5). Everything is in focus—from her face to the bed’s edge—no bokeh, no depth‑of‑field blur. The background wallpaper and pillows are fully sharp.

- **Composition & Framing**  
  Handheld shot with a slight tilt and off‑center framing; the composition is accidental, not rule‑of‑thirds. The crop cuts off a small portion of the bed’s headboard on the right, giving a casual, spontaneous feel. The background shows a light‑colored damask wallpaper and a white pillow, adding texture without distracting.

- **Environment**  
  A bright, airy bedroom in a modern apartment. The time of day is late afternoon, with warm natural light. The weather outside is clear, which keeps the ambient light steady. The room’s decor includes a subtle patterned wallpaper and a white bed frame, creating a clean, neutral backdrop.

  - Focal length: 24 mm (wide‑angle)
