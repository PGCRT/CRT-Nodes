**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

- **Subject & Pose**  
  A young woman with long, dark brown hair, wearing a bright yellow bikini, sitting on a white bed. She is mid‑adjustment, hands pulling the straps of her top upward, a subtle motion blur around her fingers. Her expression is natural, slightly surprised, as if caught in an unplanned moment.

- **Lighting & Exposure**  
  Natural daylight from a western window, late afternoon. Slight overexposure on the face and upper body, giving a warm, soft glow. No auto‑beauty or filter enhancement—raw, unprocessed look. The exposure is uneven: the background is fully sharp, while the subject has a gentle motion blur indicating handheld shake.

- **Lens & Technical**  
  24 mm wide‑angle lens, short focal length. Everything in focus—from the bed to the wall—no bokeh. No portrait‑mode depth‑of‑field blur; the background is crisp. Slight tilt and off‑center framing, a partial crop that gives a candid feel.

- **Environment**  
  A simple bedroom with a light‑colored wall and a wooden headboard. White pillows, a white duvet, and a small bedside table visible. Time of day: late afternoon, soft natural light with a hint of warm amber. Weather: clear, no wind.

- **Composition & Style**  
  Candid, unposed, spontaneous—subject unaware or mid‑action. Imperfections encouraged: slight tilt, off‑center framing, partial crop. No rule‑of‑thirds intentionality—accidental framing. Zero studio lighting, zero professional composition, zero editorial tone. Natural motion freeze or slight blur from handheld instability.

- **Post‑Processing**  
  No filters, no color grading, no post‑processing aesthetic. Only raw capture data, with a hint of auto‑HDR to preserve detail in highlights and shadows.
