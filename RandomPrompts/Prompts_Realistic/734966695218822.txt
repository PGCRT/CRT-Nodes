**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

  Short focal length (24–35 mm), wide‑angle lens. No bokeh; background is fully sharp. The shot uses a handheld, “in‑the‑moment” approach, with natural instability that gives the image a candid feel. All elements—subject, background, floor tiles, people in the distance—are in focus.

  A bright, modern office hallway with white walls, a large window on the right, and a tiled floor. The time of day is mid‑afternoon, as indicated by the warm, slightly harsh sunlight. - **Composition & Style**  
  Candid, unposed, spontaneous—subject unaware or mid‑action. Imperfections encouraged: slight tilt, off‑center framing, partial crop. Zero studio lighting, zero professional composition, zero editorial tone.   No filters, no color grading, no post‑processing aesthetic. Only raw, natural details are preserved. Slight overexposure at the top gives a subtle glow; the rest of the image remains true to the original capture.
