**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

- **Subject & Pose**  
  A young woman with a sleek, high‑ponytail hairstyle standing mid‑walk on a sunlit rooftop. She wears an ivory knotted camisole that drapes softly over her torso and a light‑blue denim mini skirt that sits snugly at the hips. Her left hand hangs loosely by her side, while her right arm is slightly bent as if she’re about to adjust her hair or grip a railing. The pose captures a natural, unposed moment—her face is turned toward the camera but not directly at it, giving a candid, “in‑motion” feel.

- **Lighting & Exposure**  
  Ambient midday sunlight from the west casts a warm, slightly overexposed glow on her front. The light hits her left shoulder first, creating a subtle highlight and a faint shadow under the knot of her top. Slight handheld shake is present—tiny motion blur around the edges that gives the image a raw, unprocessed look. No auto‑HDR or beauty filters; the exposure is natural, with a slight warmth to the tones.

- **Lens & Technical**  
  24 mm wide‑angle lens (short focal length), f/3.5 aperture, 1/60 s shutter speed, ISO 400. Everything in focus from foreground to background; no bokeh or depth‑of‑field blur. The framing is off‑center and slightly tilted, as if the photographer was walking and captured the shot spontaneously. No rule‑of‑thirds intentionality—just a natural, accidental composition.

- **Environment**  
  Location: a modern, white‑stucco rooftop with a low wall on the back side; a few palm fronds peek over the edge in the far background. Time of day: early afternoon, about 12:30 pm, with bright, high‑angle sun. Weather: clear, lightly breezy, with a faint haze that adds a subtle ambient glow. The background is fully sharp—no blur or depth‑of‑field separation.

- **Capture Style**  
  Candid, unposed, spontaneous—subject unaware or mid‑action. Slight tilt and partial crop give the image an informal vibe. No studio lighting, no professional composition, no editorial tone. Natural motion freeze or slight blur from handheld instability adds realism.

- **Post‑Processing**  
  Zero filters, zero color grading, zero post‑processing aesthetic. The final image retains a raw, natural look with only minimal adjustments to exposure and contrast.

**Prompt Summary for Text‑to‑Image Generation**

Candid, unposed, spontaneous capture with slight tilt and off‑center framing; raw, unprocessed look.”
