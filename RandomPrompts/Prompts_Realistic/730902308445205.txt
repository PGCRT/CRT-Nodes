**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

- **Subject & Action**  
  A woman in a glossy black latex bodysuit, mid‑swing on a rope, hanging from the side of a glass‑faced skyscraper. She holds a knife in her right hand, her left hand gripping the rope, body angled toward the camera as if she’s about to perform a dramatic heist or acrobatic move. Her hair is loose and slightly messy, catching the rain. The shot should feel spontaneous—she’s unaware of the camera, caught in a natural “mid‑action” moment.

- **Capture Style**  
  Candid, unposed, spontaneous — subject unaware or mid‑action.  
  Slight tilt and off‑center framing; partial crop that gives a raw, handheld feel.  
  No rule‑of‑thirds intentionality — accidental framing.  
  Natural motion freeze or slight blur from handheld instability.

- **Environment**  
  Specific real‑world location: a modern city street in Tokyo’s Shibuya district at dusk. The building is a tall glass office tower with a reflective façade; behind the woman, a busy intersection is visible with cars and neon signs (blue “Sushi” sign, red “Kyo” sign). Weather is rainy, with light drizzle creating a subtle sheen on the glass and wet pavement. Time of day: late evening, ambient light is dim but the streetlights and signage provide warm glow.

- **Lens & Technical**  
  Short focal length (24‑35 mm) wide‑angle lens.  
  No bokeh — background fully sharp; everything in focus.  
  Auto‑HDR or auto‑beauty mode off — raw, unprocessed look.  
  Slight overexposure or underexposure depending on ambient light (choose a slight underexposure to keep the dark tones rich).  
  Wide lens, handheld, natural motion blur.

- **Composition Notes**  
  - Frame the woman’s body in a diagonal line that cuts across the vertical glass façade.  
  - Keep the rope and knife in a dynamic angle; the knife should slightly overhang the edge of the building.  
  - Capture the reflection of the woman in the glass to add depth.  
  - Include a few street‑level details (cars, signs, wet pavement) in the background for context.

- **Lighting & Color**  
  Natural lighting from the city’s neon signs and street lamps; slight underexposure to keep the dark tones rich.  
  Warm cool‑tone mix: cool blues from the night sky and warm reds from the signage.  
  No filters, no color grading, no post‑processing aesthetic.

- **Final Prompt**  
  “A woman in a glossy black latex bodysuit, mid‑swing on a rope, hanging from the side of a glass‑faced skyscraper in Tokyo’s Shibuya district at dusk. She holds a knife in her right hand, her left hand gripping the rope, body angled toward the camera as if she’s about to perform a dramatic heist or acrobatic move.
