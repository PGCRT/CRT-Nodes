**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

- **Subject & Pose**  
  A tall, curvy woman with long, wavy brown hair and gold hoop earrings stands in a casual, off‑shoulder white sheer bodysuit. Her pose is mid‑turn, left hand lightly gripping the side of her hip, right arm relaxed by her thigh. She faces slightly toward the camera but isn’t posing—her expression is natural, almost candid.

- **Lighting & Exposure**  
  Ambient daylight from a western‑facing window creates a soft, warm overexposure on the front of her body. Slight underexposure on the background gives a raw, unprocessed look. No auto‑beauty mode—just a straightforward ISO 400, f/2.8, 1/125 s shutter speed with a 35 mm wide‑angle lens.

- **Camera & Technical Settings**  
  - Lens: 35 mm (wide‑angle, short focal length)  
  - Aperture: f/2.8 – everything in focus, no bokeh, background fully sharp  
  - Shutter speed: 1/125 s – natural motion freeze with a hint of handheld blur  
  - ISO: 400 – balanced exposure without excessive grain

- **Composition & Framing**  
  The shot is slightly tilted, off‑center, and partially cropped on the left side. No rule‑of‑thirds intentionality—just accidental framing that gives a spontaneous feel. The background is a plain beige wall with faint texture, no studio lighting, no post‑processing filters.

- **Environment**  
  Location: A bright, unadorned apartment hallway with a textured beige wall and a small potted plant in the corner. Time of day: late morning, natural light coming from the west, creating soft shadows. Weather: clear, mild—no wind, no rain.

- **Details & Imperfections**  
  Slight motion blur at the edges of her hands and hair, indicating handheld instability. The bodysuit’s sheer fabric shows subtle wrinkles and a slight overexposed highlight on her chest. Her nails are painted a pale pink; her earrings catch the light. The background plant’s leaves are in focus, no portrait‑mode isolation.
