**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

- **Subject & Pose**  
  A plus‑size woman with long, wavy brown hair, wearing a cream‑colored halter dress with a blue sash tied in a bow at the waist. She stands on a marble‑tiled terrace of a classical pavilion, her left hand lightly touching her hip and her right arm relaxed by her side. Her face is turned slightly toward the camera, a candid smile on her lips, as if she’s mid‑conversation or just finished a pose. A small gold heart‑shaped pendant hangs from her neckline.

- **Lighting & Exposure**  
  Natural daylight from a high‑angle, late‑afternoon sun creates a soft overexposure on the upper part of the frame; the exposure is slightly uneven, giving a raw, unprocessed look. The lighting is warm, with a gentle golden hue that highlights the texture of her dress and the marble columns.

- **Camera & Lens Settings**  
  - Lens: 24‑mm wide‑angle prime (short focal length).  
  - Aperture: f/4.0 (wide opening).  
  - Shutter speed: 1/60 s (slight motion blur from handheld shake).  
  - ISO: 400 (for natural grain).  
  - Focus: everything in focus – subject and background.  
  - No bokeh; depth of field is fully sharp.

- **Composition & Framing**  
  The shot is intentionally off‑center, with the subject’s left side slightly cut off by the frame edge. The camera is tilted a few degrees to the right, giving a spontaneous, candid feel. The background shows a series of classical marble columns, a white balustrade, and a distant hillside with scattered trees. The sky is bright blue with fluffy white clouds.

- **Environment**  
  Location: A sunlit terrace of a neoclassical building in a rural setting. The time of day is late afternoon (around 5 pm), giving warm, golden light. Weather is clear with a few high‑altitude clouds. The ground is a polished marble floor, the columns have fluted detailing, and a small balcony railing is visible behind the subject.

- **Details & Imperfections**  
  Slight motion blur on the subject’s right arm, indicating handheld shake. The image has no filters or color grading; it retains the raw, natural look of a candid snapshot. A faint overexposure at the top of the frame gives a subtle halo effect. The background columns and tiles are fully sharp, no portrait‑mode isolation.

- **Prompt for Text‑to‑Image Generation**  
  “A plus‑size woman with long wavy brown hair, wearing a cream halter dress with a blue sash tied in a bow at the waist, standing on a marble‑tiled terrace of a classical pavilion.
