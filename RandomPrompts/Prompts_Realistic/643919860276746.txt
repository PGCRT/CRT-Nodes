**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

- **Subject & Pose**  
  A tall, athletic woman with long, sun‑kissed blonde hair standing mid‑step in a meadow. She wears a shimmering gold bikini that catches the light as she turns her head to the side, one hand on her hip, a subtle smile on her face. Her left foot is slightly ahead of her right, creating a natural diagonal line that gives the image a dynamic feel.

- **Motion & Focus**  
  Slight motion blur at the edges from handheld shake; the subject’s face and upper body are in sharp focus while the background remains fully crisp—no portrait‑mode softening. The lens is a 24‑mm wide‑angle that captures a broad field of view, keeping everything from foreground grass to distant trees in focus.

- **Lighting & Exposure**  
  Warm, golden hour lighting from the east, with a mild overexposure on the upper left corner that lets the sunlight spill through the leaves. No auto‑HDR or beauty filters—just raw, unprocessed natural light. The exposure is slightly open, letting a hint of brightness bleed into the background.

- **Environment**  
  A sunlit meadow at late afternoon, with tall wild grasses swaying around her. Autumn foliage clings to the trees in the background, creating a tapestry of orange, yellow, and green hues. The time of day is just before sunset, giving a soft, diffused glow that enhances the gold tones of her bikini.

- **Lens & Technical**  
  Short focal length (24 mm) for wide coverage; no bokeh or depth‑of‑field blur—everything from the foreground to the background is sharp. The composition is accidental: the frame is slightly tilted, the subject is off‑center, and the rule of thirds is ignored for a more spontaneous feel.

- **Capture Style**  
  Candid, unposed, spontaneous—an instant captured mid‑action as if she’s just finished a dance or a jog. Slight tilt and partial crop give the image a raw, documentary vibe. No studio lighting, no professional composition, no editorial tone—just a genuine moment frozen in time with natural motion blur from a handheld camera.
