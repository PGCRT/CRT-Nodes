**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

- **Subject & Pose**  
  A young woman with tousled, wind‑blown brown hair standing mid‑step in a cluttered warehouse. She’s bare‑chested, wearing loose gray sweat shorts and a black knee‑sleeve; her hands are clasped over her chest as if she’re about to adjust something. Her expression is neutral, slightly dazed, caught between a spontaneous movement and an unposed moment.

- **Motion & Focus**  
  Slight motion blur on the subject’s hands and hair, indicating handheld instability. Everything in the frame — from her face to the back wall — is fully sharp; no portrait‑mode bokeh. The lens is a short focal‑length wide‑angle (24‑35 mm), giving a “wide‑lens” feel with minimal distortion but still crisp background detail.

- **Lighting & Exposure**  
  Natural daylight coming from a tall, multi‑paned window on the left; the light is slightly overexposed at the top right, creating a warm halo around her head. No auto‑HDR or beauty filters — raw, unprocessed look. The exposure is a little bright at the top, giving a subtle flare and a hint of lens glare.

- **Environment**  
  A real‑world industrial space: a warehouse with exposed wooden beams, corrugated metal roof, and a fluorescent tube light hanging from the ceiling. Cardboard boxes stack along the right wall, a large canvas bag rests on a pallet near the window, and a small plastic bin sits on the floor. The time of day is late morning; the sun is high but not directly overhead, casting soft shadows.

- **Composition & Framing**  
  No rule‑of‑thirds intentionality — the frame is slightly tilted, off‑center, capturing the subject’s left side more prominently. The crop is partial: the top of her head cuts off a bit at the upper edge, and the lower leg is partially out of frame at the bottom right. The background is fully sharp; no depth‑of‑field blur.

- **Technical Specs**  
  - Lens: 35 mm, wide‑angle, f/2.8 (or f/4)  
  - Aperture: f/4 (wide, but everything stays in focus)  
  - Shutter speed: 1/60 s (enough to capture slight blur)  
  - ISO: 400–600 (for natural grain and dynamic range)  
  - No bokeh, no portrait‑mode isolation — all elements from foreground to background are in focus.

- **Style Notes**  
  Candid, unposed, spontaneous — the subject appears unaware of the camera, mid‑action. Imperfections are encouraged: slight tilt, off‑center framing, partial crop. Zero studio lighting, zero professional composition, zero editorial tone. The image should feel like a genuine snapshot captured in a real environment.
