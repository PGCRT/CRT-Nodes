**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

The pose is candid—she appears to be mid‑movement, with a faint motion blur around her hand and shoulder, suggesting a handheld shot captured in real time.

- **Facial Features & Expression**  
  Her face shows a natural, unposed expression: a soft smile, eyes slightly tilted toward the camera, with a gentle gaze that conveys confidence and ease. The lighting is ambient, coming from the west‑southwest, creating a warm, slightly overexposed highlight on her forehead and cheek, while a subtle shadow falls under her jawline. No auto‑beauty or filter enhancement—just raw, unprocessed detail.

- **Lighting & Exposure**  
  The scene is lit by natural daylight at late afternoon, giving a golden‑hour warmth. The exposure is slightly overexposed on the subject’s face and upper body, with a faint halo effect around her hair. No post‑processing color grading; the tones remain natural, with a slight contrast boost from the camera’s in‑camera HDR.

  Shot on a wide‑angle lens (24 mm), short focal length, capturing a broad view of the subject and her surroundings. The composition is accidental: the frame is slightly tilted, the subject is off‑center, and the crop is partial, giving an unposed, spontaneous feel.

- **Environment**  
  The location is an urban alleyway in a modern residential complex, with a concrete wall and a small window that lets in the ambient light. The time of day is late afternoon, around 5:30 pm, when the sun is low but still high enough to keep the background bright. Weather is mild, with a light breeze that creates a subtle motion blur on the subject’s hair and shirt.

- **Capture Style**  
  Candid, unposed, spontaneous—subject unaware or mid‑action. Imperfections are encouraged: slight tilt, off‑center framing, partial crop. No rule‑of‑thirds intentionality—accidental framing that gives the image a natural, documentary feel. Natural motion freeze or slight blur from handheld instability adds life to the shot.
