**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

- **Subject & Pose**  
  A young woman with long, dark, slightly wavy hair standing on a white metal railing in an urban park. She’s wearing a delicate white lace bra and matching panties, with a soft beige faux‑fur coat draped over her shoulders. Her left hand rests on the rail, right hand holding the coat, body angled 30° toward the camera, head turned slightly to the side, eyes focused off‑camera, a natural, unposed expression.

- **Lighting & Exposure**  
  Warm golden hour sunlight from the west, slight overexposure on the upper body, ambient light creating soft highlights on her skin and fabric. No auto‑HDR or beauty filters—raw, unprocessed look. Slight handheld shake giving a subtle motion blur around the edges.

- **Lens & Technical Settings**  
  24 mm wide‑angle lens, f/3.5, 1/60 s shutter speed, ISO 400. Everything in focus—background fully sharp, no bokeh or depth‑of‑field blur. No portrait mode or subject isolation.

- **Framing & Composition**  
  Casual, off‑center framing: the subject is slightly tilted, the top of her head cuts off near the upper edge, creating a partial crop. No rule‑of‑thirds intentionality—accidental composition. The railing runs horizontally across the lower third of the frame, but not perfectly aligned.

- **Environment**  
  Location: a concrete park bench with a white metal railing, behind her a line of tall green trees and a city skyline in the distance. Time of day: late afternoon (golden hour). Weather: clear, mild breeze, slight haze from the sun’s angle.

- **Details & Imperfections**  
  Slight motion blur on the coat’s fur, natural texture visible. The woman’s left ear is partially hidden by her hair, adding candid authenticity. Her fingernails are painted pale pink, a small detail catching the light. The background trees are fully sharp, no depth‑of‑field blur.

- **Post‑Processing**  
  Zero filters, zero color grading, zero post‑processing aesthetic—just raw capture. Slight overexposure on the upper body gives a warm glow.  

**Prompt Summary**  
“Candid photo of a woman in white lace lingerie and beige faux‑fur coat standing on a white railing in an urban park at golden hour. 24 mm wide‑angle, f/3.5, 1/60 s, ISO 400, everything in focus, slight handheld blur, warm overexposure, off‑center framing, partial crop, natural motion freeze, no filters—raw, unprocessed look.”
