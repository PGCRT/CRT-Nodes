**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

- **Subject & Pose**  
  A young woman with long, straight brown hair and black-rimmed glasses, seated in a black office chair. She wears a sheer black corset-style top with front laces, her hands resting on her thighs, legs slightly apart, a natural, relaxed posture as if caught mid‑gesture. Her expression is a bright, candid smile, eyes slightly tilted toward the camera, giving an informal, spontaneous feel.

- **Lighting & Exposure**  
  Natural daylight from a western window, mid‑afternoon (≈ 4 pm). Slight overexposure on the face and upper body, creating a warm, soft glow; subtle underexposed shadows in the lower legs. No auto‑beauty or filter processing—raw, unedited look.

- **Camera & Lens Settings**  
  Wide‑angle lens (24 mm), handheld, slightly shaky. F‑stop 5.6, shutter speed 1/125 s, ISO 400. Everything in focus—from the subject’s face to the background plant—no bokeh, no depth‑of‑field blur.

- **Composition & Framing**  
  Off‑center, accidental framing: the chair is slightly tilted, the left side of the frame cuts off a bit of the wall. The composition is not rule‑of‑thirds; the subject’s head is a little above the center line, her hands extend near the bottom edge. Background includes a small potted palm plant to the right and a framed horse painting on the left wall, both fully sharp.

- **Environment**  
  A bright, casual office or home setting. Light comes from a west‑facing window, creating soft shadows. The room has light beige walls, a wooden floor visible at the bottom, and a small white side table on the left. Time of day is mid‑afternoon, giving warm, slightly harsh natural light. Weather is clear, no wind, ambient noise is minimal.

- **Technical Details**  
  - Lens: 24 mm wide‑angle, f/5.6, 1/125 s, ISO 400.  
  - Focus: everything in focus—subject and background.  
  - No portrait mode or subject isolation.  
  - Slight motion blur on the hands and legs due to handheld shake.  
  - No color grading or filters; raw JPEG output.
