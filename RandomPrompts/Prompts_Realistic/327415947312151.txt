**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

- **Subject & Pose**  
  A young woman with long, dark brown hair tied in a loose ponytail, wearing a plain white crew‑neck t‑shirt and gold hoop earrings. She is captured mid‑smile, head slightly tilted, eyes focused on something just off‑camera, giving a candid, spontaneous feel. Her face shows natural freckles and a faint blemish near the left cheek, adding realism.

- **Lighting & Exposure**  
  Ambient daylight from a bright, overcast sky; slight overexposure on the upper half of the frame to keep the look raw. No auto‑beauty or filter enhancement—just natural auto‑HDR. The lighting creates soft highlights on her forehead and nose, with subtle shadows in the neck and under‑chin area.

- **Camera Settings**  
  - Lens: 24 mm wide‑angle (short focal length), handheld.  
  - Aperture: f/5.6 (mid‑range) for a fully sharp background.  
  - Shutter speed: 1/125 s, producing a slight motion blur on the subject’s hair and a tiny shake in the background.  
  - ISO: 400 – balanced for natural contrast.

- **Composition & Framing**  
  The shot is intentionally off‑center; the subject’s left eye sits slightly above the horizontal midline, and the top of her head cuts close to the upper edge of the frame. No rule‑of‑thirds intentionality—just a casual, spontaneous crop that includes a small portion of the background wall and a hint of a nearby tree branch. The background is fully in focus, with no bokeh or depth‑of‑field blur.

- **Environment**  
  Location: A sunlit park bench in an urban suburb; the background shows a light beige stucco wall and a thin, green leafy branch on the right side. Time of day: mid‑afternoon (14:30–15:00), with diffused sunlight and a slight haze from moisture in the air. Weather: lightly cloudy but bright, giving a soft, even illumination.

- **Technical Details**  
  - No portrait‑mode subject isolation; everything from foreground to background is sharp.  
  - Slight tilt of the camera body adds a natural, handheld feel.  
  - The image has a raw, unprocessed look—no color grading or filters, just an auto‑HDR lift on the highlights and shadows.

- **Final Prompt**  
  “A candid, wide‑angle shot of a young woman with dark brown hair in a loose ponytail, wearing a white crew‑neck t‑shirt and gold hoop earrings, captured mid‑smile with a slight tilt and off‑center framing. The scene is set on a bright mid‑afternoon day in an urban park, with a beige stucco wall and a green branch in the background. Use a 24 mm lens at f/5.6 and 1/125 s shutter speed for a slight motion blur, auto‑HDR for natural highlight lift, and keep everything fully in focus—no bokeh or portrait isolation.”
