**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

- **Subject & Pose**  
  A fit woman with long dark hair, wearing a black triangle bikini, captured mid‑movement as she leans against a large glass sliding door. Her arms are raised, hands gripping the frame, head tilted to the side, eyes looking upward, conveying a spontaneous, energetic vibe. A small tattoo is visible on her left ribcage. She stands barefoot on a light‑colored tile floor.

- **Lighting & Exposure**  
  Natural daylight from a bright morning window; slight overexposure on the upper body and background to emphasize the airy feel. No auto‑beauty or filter processing—raw, unprocessed look. Slight handheld shake gives a natural motion blur around the edges.

- **Environment**  
  Location: A modern home with an arched glass door that opens onto a lush garden. Outside, tropical plants (banana leaves, palm fronds) and a terracotta pot are visible through the glass. The time of day is early afternoon, with warm, soft sunlight casting gentle shadows. Weather is clear, slightly humid.

- **Lens & Technical**  
  Wide‑angle lens (24‑35 mm), short focal length to capture a broad view. Everything in focus—no bokeh, no depth‑of‑field blur. The background is fully sharp, from the glass door to the garden beyond. No rule‑of‑thirds intentionality; framing is accidental, slightly tilted and off‑center.

- **Composition & Style**  
  Candid, unposed, spontaneous—subject appears unaware of the camera. Slight tilt and partial crop give a raw, documentary feel. The composition is not deliberately structured; the subject’s pose is mid‑action, with her arms reaching upward and her body slightly angled. All elements (subject, door, plants, pot) are in frame, creating a rich, layered scene.

- **Post‑Processing**  
  Zero studio lighting, zero professional composition, zero editorial tone. Only natural motion freeze or slight blur from handheld instability. No color grading or filters—just the raw capture.
