**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

- **Subject & Action**  
  A young woman with long, sun‑kissed blonde hair, wearing an oversized black t‑shirt, captured mid‑gesture as she holds a silver smartphone in front of a large window. Her face is turned slightly to the side, eyes focused on the screen, fingers curled around the device as if taking a photo or video. The pose is spontaneous and unposed; her head is tilted a few degrees off‑center, giving a natural, handheld feel.

- **Lighting & Exposure**  
  Warm, natural daylight from a high‑angle window creates a soft overexposure on the right side of the frame. Slight ambient glare hints at a bright, sunny afternoon. The exposure is slightly uneven—the left side of the image is a bit brighter than the right, giving an authentic, raw look. No auto‑beauty or filter enhancement; the image remains unprocessed.

- **Camera & Lens Settings**  
  - Lens: 24 mm wide‑angle (short focal length) for a broad field of view.  
  - Focal depth: everything from foreground to background is fully sharp—no portrait‑mode blur.  
  - Aperture: f/5.6, giving a moderate depth that keeps the window and surrounding foliage crisp.  
  - Shutter speed: 1/125 s, producing a slight motion blur on the subject’s hands and phone, indicating handheld instability.  
  - ISO: 400 for clean detail without noise.

- **Composition & Framing**  
  The frame is intentionally imperfect: the subject’s left shoulder cuts off near the top edge, and the right side of her shirt extends beyond the bottom border. The composition follows no rule‑of‑thirds; it’s a casual, spontaneous snapshot. The window’s vertical mullion runs along the left edge, partially framing the woman’s head. A small portion of a green plant leaf peeks into the lower right corner, adding a natural accent.

- **Environment & Time**  
  Location: A bright, modern apartment balcony with a large, clear glass window. The time of day is late morning, around 10:30 AM, when the sun is high but still casts soft shadows. Outside the window, a blurred garden scene with green foliage and a hint of a blue sky is visible, fully in focus.

- **Technical Details**  
  - No bokeh; background remains sharp.  
  - Slight tilt of the camera adds to the candid feel.  
  - The image has a natural color palette: warm golds on the hair, cool blues in the window frame, and muted greens outside.  
  - No post‑processing or color grading—just raw, unfiltered capture.

- **Final Prompt**  
  “A young woman with long, sun‑kissed blonde hair, wearing an oversized black t‑shirt, captured mid‑gesture as she holds a silver smartphone in front of a large window. Warm, natural daylight from a high‑angle window creates a soft overexposure on the right side. Shot on a 24 mm wide‑angle lens at f/5.6 and 1/125 s, with everything fully sharp.
