**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

- **Subject & Pose**  
  A young woman with shoulder‑length brown hair and a bright, candid smile stands bare in a sunlit forest clearing. Her arms are raised above her head, hands clasped together, creating a dynamic, mid‑motion pose that feels spontaneous and unposed. She wears a single hoop earring on each ear, a small belly button piercing, and a faint, natural tan that catches the light.  

- **Lighting & Exposure**  
  Slight overexposure from ambient daylight, giving a warm, soft glow to the subject’s skin. The exposure is uneven—slightly brighter at the top of her head and shoulders, with subtle vignetting at the edges. No auto‑beauty or filter enhancement; the look remains raw and unprocessed.  

- **Camera Settings**  
  Wide‑angle lens (24 mm), short focal length, handheld. Slight motion blur from the subject’s movement and the camera’s instability. Everything is in focus—no bokeh or depth‑of‑field blur; the background is fully sharp.  

- **Composition & Framing**  
  No rule‑of‑thirds intentionality: the frame is slightly tilted, off‑center, and partially cropped at the bottom left and top right. The subject occupies the central vertical space, with her body angled toward the camera but not directly facing it.  

- **Environment**  
  A forest clearing in late morning, with tall trees and large, moss‑covered rocks scattered around. Sunlight filters through the canopy, creating dappled light and soft shadows on the ground. The time of day is mid‑morning, about 10:30 AM, with a warm, golden hue. Weather is clear, with a light breeze that ruffles the leaves.  

- **Technical Details**  
  - Lens: 24 mm wide‑angle, handheld.  
  - Focal length: short, giving a broad view of the scene.  
  - Depth of field: everything in focus—subject and background.  
  - No portrait mode or subject isolation.  
  - Slight motion blur on the subject’s arms and hands, indicating mid‑action.  

- **Prompt for Text‑to‑Image Generation**  
  “A young woman with shoulder‑length brown hair and a bright candid smile stands bare in a sunlit forest clearing, arms raised above her head with hands clasped, wearing hoop earrings and a belly button piercing. The image has slight overexposure from ambient daylight, a warm soft glow on her skin. Shot on a 24 mm wide‑angle lens, handheld, with slight motion blur from the subject’s movement and camera instability. Everything is in focus—no bokeh. The frame is slightly tilted and off‑center, partially cropped at the bottom left and top right. Background shows tall trees and large moss‑covered rocks, dappled sunlight filtering through the canopy, late morning, clear weather with a light breeze. Raw, unprocessed look, no filters or color grading.”  

Provide only the structured prompt.
