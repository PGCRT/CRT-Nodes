**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

- **Subject & Pose**  
  A woman with long, wavy brown hair, wearing a black lace bra and matching black bikini bottoms, is captured mid‑action on a white quilted bed. She’s pulling a floral‑printed kimono‑style shirt open with both hands, her fingers slightly blurred as if she’s just finished adjusting the fabric. Her gaze is downward, focused on the shirt, giving a candid, unposed feel.

- **Lighting & Exposure**  
  Natural daylight from a western window, slightly overexposed to keep the scene bright and airy. No auto‑HDR or beauty filters—just raw, unprocessed light. The exposure is a little high, letting soft highlights linger on her skin and the fabric.

- **Camera Settings**  
  - Lens: 24‑35 mm wide‑angle, short focal length for a “handheld” feel.  
  - Aperture: f/4–f/5.6 (wide enough that everything—from subject to background—remains sharp).  
  - Shutter speed: 1/60–1/100 s, producing slight motion blur on the hands and shirt movement.  
  - ISO: 400–800, depending on ambient light; keep noise low but natural.

- **Composition & Framing**  
  - No rule‑of‑thirds intentionality—frame the subject slightly off‑center, with the left side of her body cutting close to the left edge of the frame.  
  - The shot is a “partial crop” that cuts off a bit of the bed’s corner, adding spontaneity.  
  - Slight tilt (5–10°) to mimic handheld instability.  
  - Background: a plain white quilted bed with subtle texture; no bokeh—everything in focus from foreground to background.

- **Environment**  
  Location: a bright, airy bedroom with a white quilted bed and a small window on the left. Time of day: late morning, soft natural light. Weather: clear, no wind, ambient temperature around 22 °C.

- **Technical Details**  
  - No portrait‑mode depth‑of‑field blur—keep the entire frame sharp.  
  - Slight overexposure on the subject’s skin and shirt to keep the image luminous.  
  - Capture “mid‑action” with a natural freeze or slight blur from handheld instability.

- **Prompt Summary**  
  *“A woman in black lace lingerie pulling a floral kimono shirt open on a white quilted bed, captured with a 24‑35 mm wide‑angle lens at f/5, 1/80 s shutter speed, slight motion blur on the hands, natural daylight from a western window, slightly overexposed, handheld tilt, off‑center framing, everything in focus, raw unprocessed look.”*
