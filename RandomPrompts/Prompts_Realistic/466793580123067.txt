**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

- **Subject & Pose**  
  A young woman with a high bun, wearing an off‑white long‑sleeve sweater, captured mid‑gesture as she points toward her wrist with both hands. Her face is turned slightly to the right, a bright, candid smile on her lips. The shot should feel spontaneous—she’s unaware of the camera, caught in a natural moment.

- **Motion & Focus**  
  Apply a subtle motion blur to convey slight handheld instability. Keep everything in focus—no depth‑of‑field blur; background must be fully sharp. Avoid any “portrait mode” or subject isolation.

- **Lighting & Exposure**  
  Use wide‑angle, short‑focal‑length lens (e.g., 24‑35 mm). Capture with a slight overexposure or underexposure depending on ambient light—aim for a raw, unprocessed look. No auto‑HDR or auto‑beauty enhancements; no filters, color grading, or post‑processing aesthetics.

- **Framing & Composition**  
  Frame the shot casually: slight tilt and off‑center composition, partial crop that doesn’t follow the rule of thirds. The background should be a real office space with a large window, visible fluorescent lights, and a glass partition that reflects the ceiling lights. Keep the background fully sharp—no bokeh.

- **Environment**  
  Location: A modern office with a large window, visible ceiling fluorescents, and a glass partition that reflects the interior lighting. Time of day: mid‑afternoon, natural daylight with a warm, slightly hazy tone. Weather or ambient conditions: clear, mild, with a light breeze that may be captured in the subject’s hair movement.

- **Lens & Technical**  
  Short focal length (24–35 mm), wide‑angle lens. No bokeh, no depth‑of‑field blur—everything in focus. Zero studio lighting, zero professional composition, zero editorial tone. Capture a candid, unposed, spontaneous moment with slight tilt, off‑center framing, and partial crop.
