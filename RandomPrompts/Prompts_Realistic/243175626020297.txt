**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

- **Subject & Pose**  
  A woman with long, dark hair kneeling on a cluster of dark volcanic rocks, her body slightly tilted to the left, mid‑gesture as she reaches for a succulent plant beside her. Her left arm is bent, hand resting on a rock, while her right arm supports her weight. She’s bare‑headed, no clothing, with a visible tattoo on her left upper arm. Her face is turned toward the camera but not directly at it—slight off‑center framing.

- **Lighting & Exposure**  
  Natural daylight from a high‑angle, late‑afternoon sun; slight overexposure in the highlights, giving a warm, golden hue. No auto‑HDR or auto‑beauty mode—raw, unprocessed look. The exposure is slightly uneven, with the top of the subject’s head a bit brighter than the lower body.

- **Motion & Focus**  
  Slight motion blur on the subject’s right hand and the nearby succulent, indicating handheld instability. Everything in the frame—from the rocks to the background plants—is fully sharp; no bokeh or depth‑of‑field blur.  

- **Lens & Technical**  
  Wide‑angle lens (24 mm), short focal length. No rule‑of‑thirds intentionality—framing is accidental, with a slight tilt and partial crop that cuts off the top of the subject’s head and the lower left corner of the rock cluster.  

- **Environment**  
  Location: A sunny, rocky outcrop in a coastal or mountain meadow. The ground is covered in dark, porous volcanic stones, interspersed with pale green succulents (likely an agave or yucca variety). In the background, a few palm fronds peek from above, and a faint line of trees can be seen at the far edge. Time of day: late afternoon, around 5 pm, with warm, golden light. Weather: clear, no wind, but the sun’s angle creates soft shadows.  

- **Composition & Style**  
  Candid, unposed, spontaneous—subject appears unaware of the camera. Imperfections are encouraged: slight tilt, off‑center framing, partial crop. No studio lighting, no professional composition, no editorial tone. The image should feel like a captured moment in real life, with natural motion freeze or slight blur from handheld instability.  

- **Post‑Processing**  
  Zero filters, zero color grading, zero post‑processing aesthetic. Keep the raw look; only minor adjustments to exposure and contrast if needed.
