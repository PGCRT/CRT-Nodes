**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

- **Subject & Pose**  
  A fit woman with long, wavy brown hair standing on a sunlit beach, wearing a navy blue “bell” bikini. Her hands rest on her hips, body slightly angled toward the camera, mid‑pose as if she just finished a swim or a dance move. The bikini straps are visible, the “bell” logo printed in white on each cup. She’s smiling softly, eyes bright, a natural candid expression.

- **Lighting & Exposure**  
  Slight overexposure from ambient daylight; the image has a raw, unprocessed look with no filters or color grading. The exposure is a little warm, as if the sun is high but not midday—late afternoon, golden hour. A faint halo of light wraps around her shoulders and head.

- **Motion & Focus**  
  Slight motion blur from handheld shooting; the subject’s hair has a gentle smear indicating movement. Everything in focus—from her face to the background—no portrait‑mode depth of field blur. The background is fully sharp, no bokeh.

- **Lens & Technical**  
  Wide‑angle lens (24–35 mm), short focal length. No rule‑of‑thirds intentionality; framing is accidental, slightly off‑center. The composition feels spontaneous, as if the photographer caught her in a candid moment.

- **Environment**  
  Specific real‑world location: a sandy beach with a calm ocean behind her, a few palm fronds in the foreground, a white wall or building on the left side. Time of day: late afternoon, soft golden light. Weather: clear, slightly humid, a light breeze.

- **Details & Imperfections**  
  Slight tilt in the camera angle; partial crop that cuts off a bit of the ocean horizon on the right. The image has a natural “raw” feel—no studio lighting, no professional composition, no editorial tone. The subject’s skin has a slight overexposed glow, and there is a faint underexposure in the lower part of the frame, giving it an authentic, unpolished vibe.

- **Prompt for Generation**  
  *“A fit woman with long wavy brown hair stands on a sunlit beach in a navy blue bell bikini, hands on hips, mid‑pose as if just after a swim. Slight motion blur on her hair, soft overexposure from late afternoon sunlight, everything fully sharp with wide‑angle lens. The background shows a calm ocean and a white wall, a few palm fronds in the foreground. Capture candid, spontaneous, accidental framing, slight tilt, partial crop, no filters or color grading.”*
