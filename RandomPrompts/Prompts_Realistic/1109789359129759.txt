**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

- **Subject & Pose**  
  A young woman with long, dark hair, wearing a black lace lingerie set (bra and thong), leaning casually against a modern kitchen counter. She holds a wine glass in her right hand, slightly tilted as if mid‑sip, with a subtle motion blur around the glass to convey movement. Her left hand rests on the countertop, creating a natural, unposed stance.

- **Appearance & Details**  
  - Skin tone: warm, natural with a slight overexposure in the highlights.  
  - Makeup: soft pink lipstick, subtle eyeliner.  
  - Accessories: thin gold necklace, small pendant.  
  - Clothing: black lace with delicate floral patterns, thin straps, tie‑front bra and matching thong with side ties.  
  - Expression: confident, slightly upward gaze toward the camera.

- **Environment**  
  - Location: a contemporary kitchen with light‑wood cabinets, stainless‑steel refrigerator, marble countertop, and a visible backsplash.  
  - Time of day: late afternoon, soft natural light from the west, creating warm ambient glow.  
  - Weather: clear, mild; slight wind causing gentle motion in the subject’s hair.

- **Lens & Technical**  
  - Focal length: 24‑35 mm (wide‑angle).  
  - Aperture: f/4–f/5.6 (handheld, slightly shallow depth of field but everything remains sharp).  
  - Shutter speed: 1/60–1/100 s (producing slight motion blur on the wine glass).  
  - ISO: 400–800 (for natural grain).  
  - Auto‑HDR or auto‑beauty mode off.  
  - No filters, no color grading, no post‑processing aesthetic.  
  - Slight tilt and off‑center framing; partial crop to give a candid feel.

- **Capture Style**  
  - Candid, unposed, spontaneous — subject unaware or mid‑action.  
  - Imperfections encouraged: slight tilt, off‑center framing, partial crop.  
  - No rule‑of‑thirds intentionality — accidental framing.  
  - Zero studio lighting, zero professional composition, zero editorial tone.  
  - Natural motion freeze or slight blur from handheld instability.

- **Background & Focus**  
  - Background fully sharp: kitchen appliances (stove, range hood), visible utensils in a red container, stainless‑steel refrigerator, and a small window with natural light.  
  - Everything in focus — no portrait mode or subject isolation.

- **Prompt Summary for Generation**  
  “A young woman with long dark hair, wearing a black lace lingerie set, leans against a modern kitchen counter, holding a wine glass mid‑sip.
