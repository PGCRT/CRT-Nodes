**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

- **Subject & Pose**  
  A young woman with long, sun‑kissed blonde hair, wearing a black quilted Chanel‑style handbag on her left shoulder, standing at the edge of a sunny outdoor café. She is turned slightly away from the camera, head tilted to the side, mouth open as if mid‑speech or laugh, capturing a spontaneous, “in‑the‑moment” vibe. Her body is angled toward a wooden table where two other patrons are seated, creating a natural depth layering.

- **Clothing & Accessories**  
  She wears a black leather backless top with a thin waist belt, paired with denim shorts. The handbag has a classic quilted pattern, gold‑tone chain strap, and a double‑C logo clasp. A subtle, natural shadow falls across her back from the umbrella above.

- **Environment**  
  The scene is set on a bright mid‑afternoon day at an outdoor café in a suburban town. Yellow and orange umbrellas are scattered around, one directly above the woman’s head casting a soft shade. A red “STOP” sign stands on a metal pole to the left, slightly out of alignment with the frame. In the background, a paved street runs parallel to the café, with a parked white car and a green tree line further back. The sky is clear, with a faint haze from the sun’s heat.

- **Time of Day & Lighting**  
  Captured at 3:30 p.m., when the sun is high but not directly overhead, producing warm, slightly harsh natural light. The lighting has a slight overexposure on the upper part of the frame, giving a raw, unprocessed look. No auto‑HDR or beauty filters are applied; the image retains a natural, handheld feel.

- **Lens & Technical Settings**  
  - Focal length: 24 mm (wide‑angle, short focal length)  
  - Aperture: f/5.6 (mid‑range, keeping background fully sharp)  
  - Shutter speed: 1/125 s (slight motion blur from handheld instability)  
  - ISO: 400 (for balanced exposure)  
  Everything is in focus—no portrait mode or subject isolation. The composition is accidental: the frame is slightly tilted, the subject is off‑center, and the “STOP” sign is not aligned with the rule of thirds.

- **Capture Style**  
  Candid, unposed, spontaneous—the woman appears unaware of the camera, mid‑action. Slight tilt and partial crop create a raw, documentary feel. No intentional rule‑of‑thirds; the framing feels natural and imperfect.

- **Post‑Processing**  
  No filters, no color grading, no post‑processing aesthetic. The image retains its raw, in‑camera look with only minimal white‑balance adjustment.

- **Final Prompt Summary**  
  “A candid, wide‑angle shot of a blonde woman in a black quilted handbag, standing at an outdoor café on a bright mid‑afternoon. Yellow umbrellas, a red STOP sign, and a wooden table create a layered background. The image is captured with a 24 mm lens at f/5.6 and 1/125 s, producing slight motion blur and a slight overexposure. Everything is in focus, the frame is slightly tilted, and the composition feels accidental and spontaneous.”
