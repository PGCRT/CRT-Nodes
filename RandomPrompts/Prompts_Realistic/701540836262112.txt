**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

- **Subject & Pose**  
  A woman with long, wavy brown hair, wearing a pink mesh bra and matching pink thong, is mid‑kneel on a beige sofa. Her back is to the camera; she’s balancing on her toes with both hands resting on the armrests, one foot slightly ahead of the other. Her left hand grips the left armrest, right hand on the right armrest, and her head is tilted down as if she’s about to pick something up or adjust her clothing.  

- **Motion & Focus**  
  Slight motion blur at the knees and feet to suggest a natural, handheld capture. Everything in focus—no portrait‑mode blur; background fully sharp.  

- **Lighting & Exposure**  
  Natural daylight from a large window behind the sofa creates a soft, slightly overexposed highlight on her back. No auto‑HDR or beauty filters—raw, unprocessed look.  

- **Environment**  
  A bright, airy living room with a tall, leafy orchid plant to the left, a beige curtain, and a large glass window that lets in natural light. The sofa is a plush, neutral‑tone fabric; a small side table with a vase of white flowers sits next to the sofa.  

- **Lens & Technical**  
  Short focal length (24 mm) wide‑angle lens, no bokeh. All elements—subject, sofa, plant, window—are in focus. Slight tilt and off‑center framing; partial crop that cuts off a bit of the left side of the room for a candid feel.  

- **Composition Notes**  
  No rule‑of‑thirds intentionality—the frame is accidental, as if captured by a photographer on the move. The shot has a slight handheld shake, giving it a spontaneous, “mid‑action” vibe.  

- **Final Prompt**  
  *“A woman in pink lingerie kneeling on a beige sofa in a sunlit living room, with an orchid plant and a tall window behind her. Capture with a 24 mm wide‑angle lens, everything in focus, slight motion blur at the knees and feet, natural daylight overexposure, handheld tilt, off‑center framing—raw, candid, unposed.”*
