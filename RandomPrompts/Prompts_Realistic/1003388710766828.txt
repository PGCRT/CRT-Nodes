**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

- **Subject & Pose**  
  A young woman with long, dark hair sitting casually on a stone step outside a sunlit building. She’s wearing a rainbow‑striped off‑the‑shoulder bikini top with a bow, matching bottoms, and tall white boots. Her hands rest on her knees, one slightly forward, as if she just finished a playful pose or is mid‑movement. A subtle smile shows a natural, unposed vibe.

- **Clothing & Accessories**  
  Rainbow‑striped fabric (red, orange, yellow, green, blue, purple) with a front bow; off‑the‑shoulder cut; high‑waist bikini bottom; white heeled boots that reach mid‑calf. A thin gold necklace with a small pendant hangs around her neck, and hoop earrings add a subtle shine.

- **Pose & Body Language**  
  Seated on a rough stone step, knees bent, one leg slightly forward. Right hand touches the left knee; left hand rests on the right thigh. Head tilted slightly to the right, eyes looking toward the camera with a casual smile. Shoulders relaxed, slight tilt of the whole frame for an accidental, unposed feel.

- **Expression & Mood**  
  Warm, sunny mid‑afternoon light casts soft shadows. Her expression is relaxed and candid, as if caught in a spontaneous moment. Slight motion blur on her boots and hands to emphasize handheld shake.

- **Environment**  
  A beige stucco building with white window frames behind her; a small potted plant sits on a ledge to the left. A metal handrail runs along the right side of the step. The scene is set in an outdoor courtyard, with a clear blue sky above and a few scattered clouds.

- **Time of Day & Lighting**  
  Late afternoon (around 4 pm), warm ambient light with slight overexposure on the face and upper body. Natural sunlight creates soft highlights on her skin and clothing; no post‑processing color grading.

- **Lens & Technical Settings**  
  24‑mm wide‑angle lens, f/4.0 aperture, 1/125 s shutter speed, ISO 400. Everything in focus from foreground to background—no bokeh. Slight handheld shake captured as a subtle motion blur on the boots and hands. No rule‑of‑thirds; framing is off‑center and accidental.

- **Composition Notes**  
  The shot is slightly tilted, with the top of the frame cutting off a bit of the building’s roof. The step she sits on occupies the lower third of the image, but it’s not perfectly centered. The background is fully sharp—no depth‑of‑field blur. The overall look is raw and unprocessed, as if taken with a smartphone or entry‑level DSLR in “auto‑HDR” off mode.

- **Final Prompt**  
  *“A young woman with long dark hair sits casually on a stone step outside a sunlit beige building, wearing a rainbow‑striped off‑the‑shoulder bikini top with a bow, matching bottoms, and tall white boots. Her hands rest on her knees as she smiles softly, one leg slightly forward. A gold necklace and hoop earrings add subtle shine. The scene includes a small potted plant on a ledge to the left and a metal handrail along the right side of the step. Shot with a 24‑mm wide‑angle lens at f/4, 1/125 s, ISO 400, capturing natural mid‑afternoon light with slight overexposure. All elements are in focus; a faint motion blur on the boots and hands conveys handheld shake. The frame is slightly tilted and off‑center, giving an accidental, candid feel.”*
