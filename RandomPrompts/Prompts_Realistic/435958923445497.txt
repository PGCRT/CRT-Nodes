**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

- **Subject & Pose**  
  A young woman in a brown leather jacket and ripped jeans, mid‑step on a stone floor, boots slightly off‑center. Her hair is loose, a few strands catching the light. She’s standing inside an old alchemy workshop, with a glowing magical circle projected on the ground beneath her feet.

- **Motion & Focus**  
  Slight motion blur from handheld shake; everything in focus—no portrait‑mode blur. The image has a raw, unprocessed look: no filters, no color grading, just natural exposure (slight overexposure on the light source).  

- **Lighting & Exposure**  
  Warm ambient light from a lantern hanging overhead, with a faint green flare from a furnace in the background. The exposure is slightly high, giving a soft glow to the entire scene.  

- **Environment**  
  Location: An ancient alchemy workshop inside a stone building; the walls are rough limestone, the ceiling has exposed beams and a single lantern. Shelves on either side hold bottles of colored liquids, tools, and a small clock. A man is working at a furnace on the left, another man is stirring a cauldron on the right, and an older figure stands on a balcony above. The time of day is late afternoon, the sky outside the window shows a warm golden hue. Weather: mild, slightly humid; the air carries a faint smoky scent.  

- **Lens & Technical**  
  Short focal length (24‑35 mm), wide‑angle lens. No bokeh—background fully sharp. All elements are in focus, from the woman’s boots to the distant clock.  

- **Composition Notes**  
  Candid, unposed, spontaneous framing: the shot is slightly tilted, the subject is off‑center, and the composition feels accidental. The rule of thirds is ignored; the frame captures a wide view of the workshop, with the magical circle on the floor anchoring the scene.  

- **Final Prompt**  
  “A young woman in a brown leather jacket and ripped jeans stands mid‑step on a stone floor inside an old alchemy workshop, boots slightly off‑center, with a glowing magical circle projected on the ground beneath her feet.
