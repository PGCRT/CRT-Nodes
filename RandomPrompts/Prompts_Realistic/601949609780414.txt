**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

- **Subject & Pose**  
  A young woman with long, sun‑kissed hair sitting on a beige sofa in a bright, lived‑in living room. She’s wearing a white bikini bottom with pink floral ties and a light pink strap; her left hand is raised near her ear as if she just received a call or is adjusting her hair. Her expression is mid‑speech, mouth slightly open, eyes bright, capturing a spontaneous, candid moment.

- **Lighting & Exposure**  
  Natural daylight from a large window on the left creates a soft overexposed highlight on her shoulder and a gentle warm glow across the scene. Slight underexposure on the lower part of the frame gives a raw, unprocessed feel. No auto‑beauty mode—just a straightforward camera capture.

- **Camera & Lens**  
  Wide‑angle lens (24 mm) with a short focal length, handheld. The shot has a slight motion blur from the subject’s movement and the photographer’s hand instability, but everything remains fully sharp—no bokeh, no portrait‑mode isolation. No rule‑of‑thirds intentionality; the framing is accidental, slightly tilted, and off‑center.

- **Environment**  
  The setting is a cozy, real‑world living room with a large window on the left, a potted plant on the windowsill, and a tall wooden bookshelf filled with colorful books behind the woman. The time of day is late morning, with warm sunlight streaming in, creating soft shadows and a slight haze. Weather is clear, ambient light is bright but not harsh.

- **Composition & Details**  
  The composition captures the woman’s full upper body and part of her legs, with her left leg bent and right leg extended. The sofa’s fabric shows a subtle texture, and the window curtain is slightly ruffled. In the background, the bookshelf’s spines display a variety of colors—red, blue, green—adding depth. A small plant in a white pot sits on the windowsill, adding a touch of green to the scene. The overall look is raw, unposed, and spontaneous, with imperfections like a slight tilt and partial crop that give it authenticity.

- **Technical Notes**  
  - Focal length: 24 mm (wide angle)  
  - Aperture: f/5.6 (mid‑range)  
  - Shutter speed: 1/125 s (slight motion blur)  
  - ISO: 400 (natural brightness)  
  - No post‑processing filters; raw, ungraded image.
