**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

- **Subject & Pose**  
  A young woman with a high ponytail, wearing an oversized white sweatshirt and tan shorts, standing at a city‑street window. Her back is to the camera; she’s mid‑turn, left hand on a small white book titled “SSIA” that rests on a wooden windowsill. A clear plastic cup sits beside the book. She’s holding a smartphone in her right hand, about to take a photo of the street outside.

- **Lighting & Exposure**  
  Slight overexposure from bright midday sun, creating soft highlights on her sweatshirt and the window glass. Auto‑HDR off – raw, unprocessed look. No color grading or post‑processing; only natural contrast.

- **Camera Settings**  
  Wide‑angle lens (24–35 mm), short focal length. Handheld, slight motion blur from instability. Everything in focus – no bokeh, no depth‑of‑field blur; background fully sharp. No portrait‑mode subject isolation.

- **Composition & Framing**  
  Candid, unposed, spontaneous – the shot is slightly tilted and off‑center. The frame captures the woman’s back, the window sill, a white taxi outside, a yellow city bus in the distance, and a street sign that reads “ONE WAY.” A small portion of a pedestrian’s leg is visible on the right edge. No rule‑of‑thirds intentionality – accidental framing.

- **Environment**  
  Specific real‑world location: a busy city street in New York City, at a coffee shop or office building with a large glass window. Time of day: mid‑afternoon (sun high, shadows short). Weather: clear, bright, slight haze from dust or pollen.

- **Lens & Technical**  
  Short focal length, wide angle. No bokeh, no depth‑of‑field blur – background fully sharp. Everything in focus. No portrait mode, no subject isolation.

- **Details to Include**  
  - The woman’s hair is tied up in a high ponytail with a black elastic band; a few strands escape the tie, adding motion.  
  - Her sweatshirt is slightly loose, revealing a hint of her waist.  
  - The white book on the sill has visible text “SSIA” and a small logo.  
  - A clear plastic cup sits next to the book, half‑filled with a white beverage.  
  - A smartphone is held in her right hand, screen facing the street.  
  - Outside the window: a white taxi (Toyota Corolla) parked at an intersection, a yellow bus further away, a traffic light showing green, and a “ONE WAY” sign.  
  - A pedestrian’s leg in jeans is partially visible on the far right.  
  - The window reflects the street, adding a subtle glare.  
  - The scene is captured through a large glass window that shows the city’s buildings and a concrete sidewalk.

  “A candid, unposed shot of a young woman with a high ponytail, wearing an oversized white sweatshirt and tan shorts, standing at a city‑street window.
