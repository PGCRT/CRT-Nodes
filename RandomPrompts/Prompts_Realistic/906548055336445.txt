**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

- **Subject & Pose**

- **Motion & Focus**  
  Slight motion blur from handheld instability; everything in focus—no portrait‑mode depth of field. The image has a raw, auto‑HDR feel with no filters or color grading, a hint of overexposure due to ambient daylight.

- **Lens & Technical**  
  Wide‑angle lens (24–35 mm), short focal length, no bokeh—background fully sharp. Slight tilt and off‑center framing; partial crop that gives an accidental, spontaneous feel.

- **Environment**  
  Specific real‑world location: a broad city avenue with a neoclassical building featuring columns and a dome in the background. A traffic light glows red on the left, a parked car is visible, pedestrians walk in the distance. Time of day: late afternoon, soft golden light with lingering daylight. Weather: clear, mild humidity, slight wind.

- **Composition**  
  No rule‑of‑thirds intentionality—framing is casual and imperfect. The shot is zero‑studio, zero‑professional‑composition, zero‑editorial tone. Background remains fully sharp, no subject isolation.
