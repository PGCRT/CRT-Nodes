**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

- **Subject & Pose**  
  A young woman with shoulder‑length wavy blonde hair, wearing a black long‑sleeved off‑shoulder crop top and matching high‑waist bottoms, sitting on a bed in a dimly lit bedroom. Her left leg is bent, foot tucked under the mattress, right leg extended forward; she leans slightly to her right, head tilted, eyes looking toward the camera with a soft, contemplative expression.  

- **Capture Style**  
  Candid, unposed, spontaneous — subject unaware or mid‑action. Slight tilt and off‑center framing, partial crop that cuts off a bit of the bed’s edge. No rule‑of‑thirds intentionality; the composition is accidental, as if captured by a handheld camera in a hurry.  

- **Motion & Focus**  
  Slight motion blur from handheld instability, natural freeze or mild blur on the subject’s hands and legs. Everything in focus — no portrait‑mode depth‑of‑field blur; background fully sharp.  

- **Lighting & Exposure**  
  Zero studio lighting, zero professional composition, zero editorial tone. Ambient light from a north‑west window creates a soft, slightly overexposed highlight on the subject’s face and shoulder, with a faint warm tint.  

- **Environment**  
  Specific real‑world location: a modest bedroom with concrete walls, a white pillow, a wooden nightstand, and a small orange object (maybe a bottle) on the bed. Time of day: late afternoon, low natural light. Weather or ambient conditions: calm, no wind.  

- **Lens & Technical**  
  Short focal length (24‑35 mm), wide‑angle lens. No bokeh; background fully sharp. All elements in focus.  

- **Post‑Processing**  
  Auto‑HDR or auto‑beauty mode off — raw, unprocessed look. No filters, no color grading, no post‑processing aesthetic. Slight overexposure on the subject’s upper body to emphasize the black top’s texture.
