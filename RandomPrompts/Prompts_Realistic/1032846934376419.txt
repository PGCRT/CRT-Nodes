**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

  A woman with long, dark brown hair, wearing a bright red bikini bottom, is captured mid‑movement on a white‑sheeted bed. She’s on all fours, back to the camera, knees bent, hands planted on the sheet, as if she’s about to stand or adjust her position. - **Motion & Focus**  
  Slight motion blur at the edges from a handheld shot; the subject’s back and hips are in sharp focus, with no portrait‑mode depth‑of‑field blur. The entire scene is fully sharp, no bokeh.

- **Lighting & Exposure**  
  Natural daylight coming from a window on the left, creating a soft, warm glow. Slight overexposure on the upper part of the frame gives a raw, unprocessed look. No auto‑HDR or auto‑beauty mode—just a straightforward capture.

- **Lens & Technical**  
  Wide‑angle lens (24‑35 mm), short focal length. Everything in focus, background fully sharp. No rule‑of‑thirds intentionality; the framing is accidental, slightly tilted, and off‑center.

- **Environment**  
  A simple hotel room or private bedroom. A light‑wood door with a silver handle is visible on the left, a white wall with a small square switch plate on the right, and a single white pillow at the corner of the bed. The time of day is mid‑afternoon, with bright natural light filtering in.

- **Capture Style**  
  Candid, unposed, spontaneous—subject unaware or mid‑action. Imperfections encouraged: slight tilt, off‑center framing, partial crop. Zero studio lighting, zero professional composition, zero editorial tone. Natural motion freeze or slight blur from handheld instability.
