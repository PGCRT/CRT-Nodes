**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

- **Subject & Pose**  
  A young woman with wavy, shoulder‑length brown hair, wearing a pink sequined halter dress, standing on a sandy beach. She is mid‑smile, head tilted slightly to the right, left arm relaxed by her side, right hand gently touching her hair. Her face is turned toward the camera but not directly aimed — a natural, candid angle.

- **Lighting & Exposure**  
  Warm, golden hour sunlight coming from the left, creating soft highlights on her dress and subtle overexposure in the upper left corner. Slight handheld shake gives a natural motion blur around the edges; no auto‑beauty or filter processing. The exposure is slightly warm, with a tiny hint of overexposure in the sky.

- **Environment**  
  Tropical beach at sunset, with gentle waves lapping at the shore behind her and a line of palm trees lining the background. The sky shows a soft gradient from pale blue to warm amber. The sand is light beige, slightly textured, and the sea is a calm, shimmering blue.

- **Lens & Technical**  
  24‑mm wide‑angle lens, short focal length, everything in focus — no bokeh or depth‑of‑field blur. No rule‑of‑thirds; framing is accidental, slightly off‑center, with a slight tilt. The composition is spontaneous and unposed.

- **Capture Style**  
  Candid, unposed, spontaneous — subject unaware or mid‑action. Slight tilt, partial crop, no intentional rule‑of‑thirds. Natural motion freeze or slight blur from handheld instability. No studio lighting, no professional composition, no editorial tone.

- **Post‑Processing**  
  Auto‑HDR off – raw, unprocessed look. No color grading or filters. Only natural ambient light and a hint of slight overexposure in the upper left.
