**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

- **Subject & Action**  
  A young woman with a high ponytail, wearing loose grey denim jeans and a thin‑strapped top, is mid‑step on a sunny city sidewalk. She holds a smartphone in her right hand, looking down at the screen, while a plastic grocery bag hangs from her left shoulder. Her sneakers have a subtle motion blur indicating movement.  

- **Capture Style**  
  Candid, unposed, spontaneous — the woman appears unaware of the camera, caught in an everyday moment. Slight tilt and off‑center framing give a raw, handheld feel. The shot is a partial crop that doesn’t follow the rule‑of‑thirds; everything remains fully sharp because a wide‑angle lens is used.  

- **Environment**  
  Location: A bustling commercial street with storefronts (“Shopping Mall”, “Silverline”) and parked cars lining the curb. Time of day: late afternoon, warm golden light with a slight overexposure that keeps the ambient brightness natural. Weather: clear, mild wind, no rain.  

- **Lens & Technical**  
  Short focal length (≈24 mm) wide‑angle lens; no bokeh, background fully sharp. No portrait‑mode depth‑of‑field blur. Slight motion blur on the subject’s feet and bag to emphasize movement. Auto‑HDR or auto‑beauty mode off — raw, unprocessed look. No filters, no color grading, no post‑processing aesthetic.  

- **Composition Notes**  
  The frame captures the woman from a slightly low angle, with her body occupying the central‑right portion of the image. A nearby passerby carrying a “Pomodyhill” bag is partially visible on the right edge, adding context. Parked cars and storefront signs line the left side; a red flag flaps in the background. The scene feels spontaneous and real, as if captured by a passerby with a handheld camera.  

**Prompt:**

> “A young woman with a high ponytail, wearing loose grey denim jeans and a thin‑strapped top, steps on a sunny city sidewalk while checking her phone. She carries a plastic grocery bag from her left shoulder. The shot is candid, slightly tilted, off‑center, captured with a 24 mm wide‑angle lens; everything is fully sharp but has a subtle motion blur on her feet and bag. The background shows parked cars, storefront signs that read ’Shopping Mall’ and ’Silverline’, and a red flag. Late afternoon golden light creates a slight overexposure. No filters, no auto‑HDR, raw look. The composition is accidental, partial crop, no rule‑of‑thirds.”
