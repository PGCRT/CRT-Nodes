**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

  Slight overexposure in the highlights, a raw, unprocessed look with no filters or color grading.   A quiet residential street at mid‑afternoon, with a concrete road stretching into the distance and a patch of green lawn on the right.   Short focal length (24 mm) wide‑angle lens, no bokeh, no subject isolation. Everything is in focus from foreground to background.
