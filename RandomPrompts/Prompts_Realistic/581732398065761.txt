- **Capture Style**  
  Candid, unposed, spontaneous — subject unaware or mid‑action. Slight motion blur from handheld instability, no filters, no color grading, no post‑processing aesthetic. A tiny overexposure in the highlights to keep the ambient light raw. Everything stays fully sharp; no bokeh or depth‑of‑field blur.

- **Lens & Technical**  
  Short focal length (≈20 mm) wide‑angle lens. No “portrait mode” isolation— background is completely in focus. Slight overexposure or underexposure depending on ambient light, auto‑HDR or auto‑beauty mode off.

- **Environment**

- **Composition Notes**
