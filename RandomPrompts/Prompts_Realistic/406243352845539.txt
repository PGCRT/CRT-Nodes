**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

- **Subject & Pose**: A tall, athletic woman with long, wavy blonde hair sits casually on a cream‑colored sofa in a bright, airy living room. She wears a black ribbed tank top and a red high‑waist skirt, with white and black striped knee‑socks that peek above the skirt. Her hands rest on her hips, one hand slightly forward, giving a subtle dynamic angle. She smiles softly at the camera, eyes bright, with natural makeup and a hint of blush.

- **Lighting & Exposure**: Natural daylight from a large window to the left creates a warm, slightly overexposed highlight on her face and upper body. The exposure is a little high, giving a soft glow, but no filters or color grading—just raw, unprocessed light. Slight handheld shake gives a natural motion blur around the edges.

- **Camera & Lens**: 24‑mm wide‑angle lens (short focal length), f/3.5 aperture, 1/60 s shutter speed, ISO 400. Everything in focus from foreground to background; no bokeh or depth‑of‑field blur. The composition is accidental—slightly off‑center, a little tilted, with a partial crop that cuts off the top of the sofa and a portion of the plant behind her.

- **Environment**: A modern living room with a large, leafy green plant in a ceramic pot on a side table, a white curtain hanging to the right, and a bright window that lets in soft natural light. The time of day is mid‑afternoon, around 3 pm, when the sun is high but not directly overhead, creating gentle shadows. The weather outside is clear, with a slight breeze that moves the plant’s leaves.

- **Details & Imperfections**: The image has a slight tilt and a partial crop that gives it a candid feel. There’s a faint motion blur on her hands and the plant’s leaves, indicating handheld instability. The background is fully sharp—no portrait‑mode isolation. A small ring on her left hand adds a subtle detail.
