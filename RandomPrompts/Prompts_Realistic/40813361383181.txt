**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

- **Subject & Pose**  
  A young woman with long, dark brown hair standing on a sunlit beach, mid‑step as if caught in a casual, spontaneous moment. She wears a white lace thong that peeks above her hips, and a delicate gold necklace rests on her collarbone. Her left hand hangs loosely by her side, fingers slightly curled, while her right arm bends at the elbow, giving a natural, unposed feel.

- **Facial Features & Expression**  
  Soft, natural complexion with a faint smile; eyes gaze slightly off‑camera, as if she’s just noticed the photographer. A subtle motion blur around her hair and shoulders conveys movement.

- **Lighting & Exposure**  
  Warm, golden hour light from the west, creating a slight overexposure on the upper body and a gentle halo around her head. The exposure is uneven—warm highlights in the foreground with cooler tones in the background—giving an auto‑HDR, raw look. No filters or color grading; the image stays unprocessed.

- **Background & Environment**  
  A wide‑angle view of a sandy beach at sunset. Palm trees line the horizon, a faint silhouette of a distant building is visible on the right, and the ocean’s surface shimmers in the background. The sand has a textured, uneven surface with small pebbles and footprints.

- **Lens & Technical**  
  24‑mm wide‑angle lens, short focal length. Everything is fully sharp—no bokeh or depth‑of‑field blur. Slight handheld shake gives a natural motion freeze/blur effect. The framing is accidental: the subject is slightly off‑center, and the composition has no rule‑of‑thirds intentionality.

- **Capture Style**  
  Candid, unposed, spontaneous—subject unaware or mid‑action. Imperfections are encouraged: slight tilt, partial crop, and a raw, unprocessed aesthetic. No studio lighting, no professional composition, no editorial tone.

- **Final Prompt**  
  “A young woman with long dark brown hair stands on a sunlit beach at golden hour, wearing a white lace thong and gold necklace, captured mid‑step with a slight motion blur. Her expression is natural and slightly off‑camera, her left hand relaxed by her side, right arm bent. Warm overexposure highlights her upper body while the background of palm trees, distant building, and shimmering ocean remains fully sharp. Shot on a 24‑mm wide‑angle lens, the image has accidental framing with slight tilt and partial crop, no filters or color grading, raw and unprocessed.”
