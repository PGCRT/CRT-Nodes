**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

- **Subject & Pose**  
  A young woman with long, straight blonde hair, wearing a white floral crop‑top and blue bikini bottoms, sitting in an office chair. Her hands are clasped together on her thighs, knees bent, and she is looking directly at the camera with a subtle smile. The chair has a black mesh back and blue cushion; it’s slightly tilted to the left, giving a casual, spontaneous feel.

- **Lighting & Exposure**  
  Natural daylight coming from a large window on the left side of the room. Slight overexposure in the upper‑right corner gives a warm, ambient glow. No filters or color grading—just raw, unprocessed tones. The exposure is slightly high, but the image remains crisp with no post‑processing aesthetic.

- **Environment**  
  A modern office space with a wooden desk on the left, a closed laptop, a tablet, and a small stack of books (blue, yellow, red) on the desk. Behind the woman, a tall window lets in bright daylight; a beige curtain hangs to the right. The wall is a light neutral color, and a patterned carpet covers the floor. The time of day is mid‑afternoon, around 3 pm, with soft, diffused sunlight.

- **Lens & Technical**  
  Wide‑angle lens (24 mm) with a short focal length. Everything is in focus—no bokeh or depth‑of‑field blur. The shot is handheld, with slight motion blur on the subject’s hands and a minor tilt that gives an accidental, candid feel. No rule‑of‑thirds intentionality; the framing is off‑center, with the subject slightly cut off at the bottom right.

- **Composition Notes**  
  The composition is spontaneous: the chair is angled, the desk extends out of frame on the left, and the window’s light creates a subtle highlight on the woman’s hair. The background is fully sharp, no portrait‑mode isolation. The overall look is raw, unposed, and natural, as if captured mid‑action by a photographer in a real‑world setting.
