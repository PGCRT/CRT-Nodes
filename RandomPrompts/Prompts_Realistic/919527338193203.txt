**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

- **Subject & Pose**  
  A young woman with long, sun‑kissed blonde hair standing in a modern kitchen. She’s bare‑headed, leaning casually against a black marble countertop, one hand on her hip, the other resting near her thigh. Her gaze is direct, slightly off‑center, giving a candid, unposed feel.

- **Lighting & Exposure**  
  Natural daylight from a large window behind the kitchen, mid‑afternoon. Slight overexposure on the upper part of the frame to capture the ambient glow; no auto‑HDR or beauty filters—raw, unprocessed look. The lighting is uneven, with a soft highlight on her face and a gentle shadow under her arm.

- **Camera Settings**  
  Wide‑angle lens (24 mm), handheld, slight motion blur from natural shake. Everything in focus—from the subject to the background—no portrait‑mode depth of field. The focal length is short, giving a broad view of the scene.

- **Composition & Framing**  
  No rule‑of‑thirds intentionality; the frame is slightly tilted, off‑center, with a partial crop that cuts off part of the countertop on the lower right. The background shows a stainless‑steel sink, a modern faucet, and dark marble backsplash with white veining. The kitchen cabinets are matte gray, and a small portion of an oven is visible on the right.

- **Environment Details**  
  Time of day: late afternoon, warm sunlight streaming in, creating subtle lens flare. Weather: clear, no wind. The kitchen is spacious, with a stainless‑steel range hood on the left and a black marble counter that reflects the subject’s body. A small green earring catches the light on her ear.

- **Technical Nuances**  
  Short focal length (24 mm) captures a wide field of view; no bokeh—background is fully sharp. Slight motion blur on the subject’s torso and hips to suggest movement. No post‑processing color grading; raw, natural tones. The image has a slight overexposure on the upper half, giving it an organic feel.
