**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

- **Subject & Pose**  
  A young woman with deep, warm brown curls that cascade over her shoulders, captured mid‑turn as she walks through a sunlit, cobblestone alley in an old European town. She wears a sleek black-and-white halter dress with a deep V‑neck, a pearl necklace, and crystal‑tipped earrings that catch the light. Her face is slightly turned toward the camera, a natural, unposed smile on her lips, as if she’s just finished a conversation or is about to start one.

- **Capture Style**  
  Candid, unposed, spontaneous — the subject is unaware of the camera, caught in a fleeting moment. Slight tilt and off‑center framing give an accidental, handheld feel. The shot has a tiny motion blur from the subject’s movement and the camera’s instability, but everything remains fully sharp—no portrait‑mode depth of field, just a wide‑angle, all‑in‑focus capture.

- **Environment**  
  Location: A narrow, historic cobblestone lane in a Mediterranean village; the alley is lined with aged stone walls and a small wooden archway. Time of day: late afternoon, around 4:30 p.m., when the sun is low but still warm, casting long, soft shadows. Weather: mild, slightly humid, with a light breeze that ruffles her hair. Ambient light is natural, with a slight overexposure on the face and a faint glow on the stone, giving an auto‑HDR feel without any post‑processing filters.

- **Lens & Technical**  
  Short focal length (24 mm) wide‑angle lens, no bokeh. The background is fully sharp—no depth‑of‑field blur. The composition is intentionally “accidental”: the frame cuts off the top of her head and the lower part of her dress, creating a partial crop that feels spontaneous. The image has a slight overexposure on the face to emphasize the natural light, but no color grading or filters are applied. All elements—subject, background, details—are in focus, captured with a handheld, natural motion freeze or slight blur from instability.
