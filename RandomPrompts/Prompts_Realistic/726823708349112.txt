**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework – “Summer Beach Breeze”**

- **Subject & Pose**  
  A young woman with wavy, sun‑kissed blonde hair, kneeling on a crumpled pink towel on a wooden deck. She’s bare‑headed, mid‑gesture, leaning slightly forward as if she just finished a swim or is about to cast a bead of water from her shoulder. Her left hand rests lightly on the towel, fingers curled, while her right arm hangs relaxed by her side. Her face is turned toward the left of the frame, eyes downcast, capturing a candid, unposed moment.

- **Lighting & Exposure**  
  Natural mid‑afternoon sunlight from the west, creating a soft, warm glow on her skin. Slight overexposure in the highlights gives a raw, unprocessed look. No auto‑HDR or beauty filters—just the native camera’s dynamic range. The exposure is slightly uneven, with the background a deep, almost black charcoal tone that contrasts the bright subject.

- **Motion & Focus**  
  Handheld shot with a short focal length (24 mm) wide‑angle lens. A subtle motion blur trails along her right arm, indicating slight movement at the moment of capture. Everything remains in focus—no bokeh or depth‑of‑field blur; the background is fully sharp, from the wooden planks to the black backdrop.

- **Composition & Framing**  
  The frame is intentionally off‑center: the subject’s left knee sits near the lower third, while her head leans toward the upper left corner. No rule‑of‑thirds intentionality—this is a spontaneous, accidental composition that feels natural and unposed. The crop is partial; the top of her head is slightly cut off by the frame edge, adding to the candid feel.

- **Environment**  
  Location: a rustic wooden deck at a beachside café. The time of day is late afternoon, around 4 pm, when the sun is high but starting to tilt. Ambient weather is mild breezy, with a faint hint of sea salt in the air. A single stray strand of hair blows across her forehead, caught mid‑motion.

- **Lens & Technical**  
  - Focal length: 24 mm (wide angle)  
  - Aperture: f/5.6 (mid‑range)  
  - Shutter speed: 1/125 s (producing slight motion blur)  
  - ISO: 400 (for clean, natural noise)  
  - No portrait mode or subject isolation—everything is in focus.

- **Post‑Processing**  
  Zero filters, zero color grading, zero studio lighting. The image retains its raw, unprocessed aesthetic, with only basic white‑balance adjustments and a slight contrast boost to enhance the natural tones.

**Prompt Summary for Text‑to‑Image Generation**

> “A candid, handheld photo of a bare‑headed woman with wavy blonde hair kneeling on a pink towel on a wooden deck, mid‑gesture as if just out of water. Natural mid‑afternoon sunlight from the west creates a warm glow and slight overexposure. Shot on a 24 mm wide‑angle lens at f/5.6, 1/125 s shutter speed, ISO 400—everything in focus with no bokeh. The composition is off‑center and accidental, capturing a spontaneous, unposed moment. Background is a dark charcoal tone, the scene is a rustic beachside café at late afternoon, breezy weather. Render in raw, unprocessed style with slight motion blur on the subject’s right arm, preserving natural imperfections.”
