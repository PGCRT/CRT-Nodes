**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

- **Subject & Pose**  
  A young woman with a high ponytail, wearing a bright yellow long‑sleeved shirt that reads “BOEWORA” above a graphic of a martial artist, and distressed denim shorts. She is mid‑step, head tilted slightly downward, a faint smile on her face, as if she just noticed something interesting on the floor. Her left hand hangs loosely by her side, right hand slightly curled.  

- **Motion & Focus**  
  Slight motion blur at the wrists and feet to suggest natural movement. Everything in focus—no portrait‑mode bokeh. The image has a raw, unprocessed look: no filters, no color grading, just ambient light with a hint of overexposure on the ceiling lights.  

- **Lens & Technical**  
  Wide‑angle lens (24–35 mm), short focal length. No depth‑of‑field blur; background fully sharp. Handheld shot with minor tilt and off‑center framing—no rule‑of‑thirds intentionality.  

- **Environment**  
  Location: an industrial warehouse or backstage area, with exposed concrete floor, stacked cardboard boxes, large black tires leaning against a wooden panel, and a row of fluorescent ceiling lights. In the background, two men in suits walk away from the camera, one holding a coffee mug. A plastic bottle and a few cigarette butts lie on the floor near the woman’s right foot.  

- **Time & Lighting**  
  Shot during mid‑afternoon, natural daylight mixed with artificial ceiling lights. Slight overexposure on the lights creates a warm glow; the scene has a slight ambient haze.  

- **Composition Notes**  
  The frame is slightly crooked: the left edge cuts off part of a tire, the right edge captures a portion of a box. The woman occupies the central‑left third of the frame, but the framing is accidental—she’s not posing for the camera. The background is fully sharp, giving a documentary feel.  

- **Post‑Processing**  
  Auto‑HDR off. No color grading or filters. Only raw capture data, with natural contrast and dynamic range.
