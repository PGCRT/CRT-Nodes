**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

- **Subject & Pose**  
  A tall, athletic woman with long, sun‑kissed blonde hair sitting on a white towel on a sandy beach. She is wearing tight white bikini bottoms that read “BONDS” in bold black letters. Her left hand rests on her thigh, while her right hand touches her cheek in a casual, mid‑pose gesture. Her head is slightly tilted, eyes closed as if she’s savoring the warm air.

- **Lighting & Exposure**  
  Slight overexposure from the midday sun, creating a soft halo around her face and body. No auto‑beauty or filter enhancement—raw, unprocessed look. The exposure is a little bright at the edges but still natural, with subtle highlights on her skin and a faint warm glow in the background.

- **Camera Settings**  
  Wide‑angle lens (24 mm) with a short focal length, handheld, giving a slight motion blur from the camera’s instability. Everything is fully sharp—no bokeh, no depth‑of‑field blur. No rule‑of‑thirds; framing is accidental and off‑center.

- **Environment**  
  Specific real‑world location: a sunlit beach with low, rugged rock formations in the background, sparse dry grasses on the dunes. Time of day: early afternoon, when the sun is high but still casts soft shadows. Weather: clear, breezy, with a few light clouds in the sky.

- **Composition & Style**  
  Candid, unposed, spontaneous—subject appears unaware of the camera. Slight tilt and partial crop give an informal feel. Background is fully sharp, no studio lighting, no professional composition, no editorial tone. Natural motion freeze or slight blur from handheld instability.

- **Technical Details**  
  Short focal length (24 mm), wide‑angle, all elements in focus. No portrait mode or subject isolation. Slight overexposure or underexposure depending on ambient light—here, a gentle overexposure that adds warmth.
