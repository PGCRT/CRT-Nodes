**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

- **Subject & Action**  
  A young woman with a high ponytail, wearing an oversized beige T‑shirt and gray cargo pants, is mid‑turn on a city sidewalk. Her right hand holds a smartphone to her ear, left hand adjusting her hair, capturing a spontaneous, candid moment as she walks past a shopping cart filled with boxes and a blue tote. She’s smiling, slightly tilted head, eyes focused on the phone screen.

- **Pose & Composition**  
  Capture a wide‑angle, handheld shot with a short focal length (e.g., 24–35 mm). Frame the scene from a low angle, letting the subject occupy the central‑right third of the frame while the shopping cart and a dark trash bin appear on the left‑back. The composition should feel accidental: slight tilt, off‑center framing, partial crop—no rule‑of‑thirds intentionality. Keep everything in focus; no bokeh or depth‑of‑field blur.

- **Lighting & Exposure**  
  Use natural daylight, mid‑afternoon (around 4 pm), with a slight overexposure that gives a warm, raw feel. No auto‑HDR or auto‑beauty mode—keep the look unprocessed. The lighting should be soft but not flat; ambient light creates subtle highlights on the subject’s shirt and hair.

- **Environment**  
  Location: a bustling urban street in a modern suburb. The background includes a concrete sidewalk, a glass‑front storefront, a metal shopping cart, a blue tote, and a black trash bin. A small patch of green grass is visible at the far left corner. Weather is clear, with a light breeze that creates slight motion blur on the subject’s hair.

- **Lens & Technical**  
  - Lens: 35 mm wide‑angle, handheld.  
  - Focal length: 35 mm (short, wide).  
  - Aperture: f/4–f/5.6 (wide opening for sharpness).  
  - Shutter speed: 1/125 s to capture slight motion blur.  
  - ISO: 400–800 (depending on ambient light).  
  - No portrait‑mode or subject isolation; everything in focus.

- **Capture Style**  
  Candid, unposed, spontaneous—subject unaware or mid‑action. Encourage imperfections: slight tilt, off‑center framing, partial crop. No rule‑of‑thirds intentionality—accidental framing. Natural motion freeze or slight blur from handheld instability.

- **Post‑Processing**  
  Zero filters, zero color grading, zero post‑processing aesthetic. Slight overexposure or underexposure depending on ambient light. Keep the image raw and natural.
