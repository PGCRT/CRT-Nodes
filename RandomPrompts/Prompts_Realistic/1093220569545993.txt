**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

- **Subject & Pose**  
  A young woman with a high ponytail and loose fringe, wearing an oversized faded denim jacket and dark jeans, seated on a bed in a dimly lit bedroom. She is mid‑smile, looking down at her hands as she fastens the jacket’s front button, fingers slightly curled, elbows bent. Her head tilts a little to the left, creating a subtle off‑center framing.

- **Motion & Focus**  
  Slight motion blur on the hands and jacket buttons from handheld shake; everything else in sharp focus—no bokeh, no portrait‑mode isolation. The image has a raw, unprocessed look: no filters, no auto‑beauty, no color grading. A tiny overexposure at the top of the frame captures the ambient light from a window, leaving a faint halo around her hair.

- **Lens & Technical**  
  Wide‑angle lens (24 mm), short focal length, everything in focus. The composition is accidental—no rule‑of‑thirds intentionality. The background shows a wooden nightstand with an open book, a blue cup, and a framed picture on the wall; a small portion of a patterned quilt is visible to the right.

- **Environment**  
  Location: a cozy, slightly cluttered bedroom in a suburban house. Time of day: late afternoon, soft natural light from a western window. Weather: mild, clear sky outside, gentle breeze. The room has a warm, muted tone palette with cool gray denim and brown wood tones.

- **Lighting & Exposure**  
  Natural lighting from a side window; slight overexposure at the top of the frame gives a subtle halo. No studio lights, no professional composition. The exposure is slightly high on the highlights but keeps the shadows detailed.

- **Details & Imperfections**  
  Slight tilt of the camera, partial crop that cuts off the top of the head and the lower part of the legs. A few stray strands of hair cling to her jacket collar. The denim has a worn, faded look with visible stitching and button holes. Her hands are in the foreground, slightly blurred, as if she’s just finished fastening the jacket.
