**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

- **Subject & Pose**  
  A young woman with shoulder‑length brown hair, wearing a black mesh crop‑top and no bottom, leaning casually against a kitchen countertop. Her left hand rests on the counter, her right hand near her thigh, body angled slightly toward the camera, head turned to face forward with a neutral, candid expression.  

- **Lighting & Exposure**  
  Slight overexposure from natural daylight coming from the west, creating soft highlights on her skin and a subtle warm tint. No auto‑beauty or filter processing—raw, unprocessed look.  

- **Camera Settings**  
  Wide‑angle lens (24 mm), short focal length, handheld. Slight motion blur from the subject’s movement and camera shake. Everything in focus—no portrait‑mode depth of field blur.  

- **Environment**  
  A modern kitchen with light‑colored cabinets, a granite countertop, a white ceramic mug, a plastic water bottle, and a red utensil holder on the left side. A small framed picture hangs on the back wall. Time of day: late afternoon, ambient light warm but not harsh.  

- **Composition**  
  No rule‑of‑thirds intentionality—accidental framing with a slight tilt. The shot is partially cropped on the right edge, giving a spontaneous, candid feel. Background remains fully sharp, no bokeh.  

- **Technical Details**  
  - Focal length: 24 mm  
  - Aperture: f/5.6 (wide, but all elements in focus)  
  - Shutter speed: 1/125 s (slight motion blur)  
  - ISO: 400  
  - No auto‑HDR or auto‑beauty mode—raw, unprocessed.  

- **Prompt for Text‑to‑Image Generation**  
  “A young woman with shoulder‑length brown hair, wearing a black mesh crop‑top, leaning against a kitchen countertop in a modern home. Her left hand rests on the counter, right hand near her thigh, body angled toward the camera, head turned forward with a neutral expression.
