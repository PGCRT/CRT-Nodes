**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

- **Subject & Pose**  
  A young woman with long, wavy brown hair lying on a neatly made bed, wearing black bikini bottoms. Her body is slightly tilted, arms spread out, legs bent at the knees, and her head resting on a beige pillow with a subtle floral pattern. She’s in mid‑motion, as if just waking up or posing spontaneously, with a faint handheld blur around her edges.

- **Lighting & Exposure**  
  Natural daylight from a side window creates a soft overexposed highlight on her torso and the pillow. The exposure is slightly uneven—warm tones dominate the upper part of the frame while the lower part remains a bit darker, giving an authentic, unprocessed look. No auto‑beauty or filter enhancements; the image retains raw, candid texture.

- **Camera & Lens Settings**  
  Shot on a DSLR or mirrorless with a 24‑mm wide‑angle lens (f/3.5, 1/60 s shutter speed). Everything is in focus—from her face to the far corner of the bed—no bokeh or depth‑of‑field blur. The background (a wooden nightstand and a white wall) stays sharp, reinforcing the “all‑in‑focus” requirement.

- **Composition & Framing**  
  The frame is intentionally off‑center: the bed’s headboard sits slightly left of center, the pillow leans toward the top right, and the woman’s body occupies the central third but is tilted 15° to the right. The composition feels spontaneous—no rule‑of‑thirds intentionality, just a casual, handheld capture.

- **Environment & Ambience**  
  The scene takes place in a bright, airy bedroom during late morning. A white sheet covers the bed, and a beige pillow with a delicate floral motif rests behind her head. A wooden nightstand is visible on the right side, holding a small white mug and a silver candle holder. The ambient light is warm, with a slight haze from the window’s curtains.

- **Details & Imperfections**  
  Slight motion blur around her arms and legs conveys the handheld shake. Her skin has natural imperfections—tiny blemishes, faint moles, and subtle shadows—that add realism. The pillow’s fabric shows a slight wrinkle where her head rests. The bedsheet has gentle folds that catch the light.

- **Post‑Processing**  
  No filters or color grading; the image is raw, with an auto‑HDR feel that enhances contrast without artificial polish. A subtle vignette is present at the corners, but it’s natural, not intentional.

**Prompt Summary (structured)**  
`Candid photo of a woman lying on a bed in black bikini bottoms, head on a beige floral pillow, arms spread, legs bent, captured with a 24‑mm wide‑angle lens at f/3.5 and 1/60 s. Natural daylight from a side window creates soft overexposure on the torso. Everything is in focus, no bokeh. Slight handheld blur, off‑center framing, spontaneous pose. Bedroom setting with a wooden nightstand and white sheet. Raw, unprocessed look, no filters.`
