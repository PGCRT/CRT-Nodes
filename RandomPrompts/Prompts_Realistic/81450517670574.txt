**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

- **Subject & Pose**  
  A woman with long, dark hair, wearing a red lace bodysuit, sitting on a white bed. She is turned to the side, leaning slightly forward with her left hand on the bed and right hand near her hip. Her head is tilted back, tongue sticking out, as if mid‑expression or playful pose. A rose tattoo is visible on her left thigh.  

- **Motion & Focus**  
  Slight motion blur from handheld shake; everything in focus—no portrait‑mode depth of field. The image has a raw, unprocessed look: no filters, no color grading, just natural exposure (slight overexposure due to bright window light).  

- **Lens & Technical**  
  Wide‑angle lens (24–35 mm), short focal length. No bokeh—background fully sharp.  

- **Environment**  
  A bright bedroom with a large window on the left, white curtains, a green potted plant on the right, and a white pillow behind the subject. The time of day is mid‑afternoon; natural daylight comes from the window, creating soft highlights and subtle shadows.  

- **Composition & Style**  
  Candid, unposed, spontaneous—subject appears unaware or mid‑action. Slight tilt and off‑center framing; partial crop at the edges. No rule‑of‑thirds intentionality. The frame captures the subject’s back, side profile, and a portion of the bed, with the plant and window forming a natural backdrop.  

- **Lighting**  
  Zero studio lighting—only ambient daylight. Slight overexposure on the window side, gentle contrast.  

- **Final Prompt**  
  “A woman in red lace bodysuit sitting on a white bed, turned to the side with her head tilted back and tongue out, long dark hair flowing, rose tattoo visible on thigh. Slight motion blur, wide‑angle lens, everything in focus, raw unprocessed look. Bright window on left, white curtains, green plant on right, mid‑afternoon daylight. Candid spontaneous pose, slight tilt, off‑center framing, partial crop. No bokeh, no filters.”
