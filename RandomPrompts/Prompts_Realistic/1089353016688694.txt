**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework – “Summer Meadow Portrait”**

- **Subject & Pose**  
  A young woman with long, loose brown hair tied loosely at the back, wearing a simple silver pendant necklace and a light‑colored, off‑shoulder top. She is seated on a beige outdoor chair, leaning slightly forward with her elbows on the armrests, looking directly at the camera with a soft, natural expression. Her shoulders are relaxed, and her arms hang loosely, giving a candid, unposed feel.

- **Lighting & Exposure**  
  Capture in mid‑afternoon sunlight (≈ 4 pm) with a slight overexposure that gives a warm, golden hue. Use a wide‑angle lens (20–35 mm) on a DSLR or mirrorless camera, handheld, with a slightly shaky hand to introduce natural motion blur. Keep the exposure time around 1/60 s to freeze the motion while allowing a subtle blur from the hand’s instability.

- **Focus & Sharpness**  
  Everything in focus – subject and background. Use a single focal plane (no portrait‑mode depth‑of‑field blur). The background is a lush green meadow with a few scattered trees, slightly out of focus only at the far edges, but overall sharp.

- **Composition & Framing**  
  Frame the shot “accidentally” off‑center: the left side of the frame cuts off a bit of the chair’s armrest, and the right edge captures a portion of a wooden fence post. The composition is not rule‑of‑thirds; it’s a spontaneous, candid crop that feels natural and unposed.

- **Environment**  
  Location: a sunny park in a suburban town, with a grassy field and a line of tall trees behind the subject. Time of day: mid‑afternoon (≈ 4 pm), with warm, slightly harsh sunlight coming from the west. Weather: clear, light breeze, no rain. The background is fully sharp, no bokeh.

- **Lens & Technical**  
  - Focal length: 24 mm (wide‑angle).  
  - Aperture: f/5.6 (mid‑range).  
  - Shutter speed: 1/60 s.  
  - ISO: 400.  
  - Handheld, slight motion blur.  
  - No auto‑HDR or auto‑beauty mode – raw, unprocessed look.  
  - No filters, no color grading, no post‑processing aesthetic.

- **Post‑Processing**  
  None – keep the image raw. Only minor crop to tighten the framing after capture. Preserve natural imperfections: slight tilt, off‑center cropping, and a hint of motion blur from the handheld shot.

**Prompt Summary (for AI image generation)**  
> “A candid, unposed portrait of a young woman with long brown hair, wearing a silver pendant necklace and a light off‑shoulder top, seated on a beige chair in a sunny park at mid‑afternoon. Everything is in focus, from the subject to the lush green meadow and trees behind her.
