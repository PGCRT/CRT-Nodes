**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

- **Subject & Pose**  
  A fit woman with long, wavy brown hair, wearing a black bikini, standing under an outdoor shower. Her arms are raised, hands clasped above her head, body slightly turned to the side, capturing a dynamic, mid‑motion pose. A small tattoo is visible on her left upper arm. She wears a thin gold necklace and hoop earring.  

- **Lighting & Exposure**  
  Natural daylight from a high‑angle, late‑afternoon sun; slight overexposure on the upper body to emphasize moisture and texture. No auto‑beauty or filter processing—raw, unprocessed look.  

- **Motion & Focus**  
  Slight motion blur on the water droplets and hair, indicating handheld instability. Everything in focus—no portrait‑mode depth of field.  

- **Lens & Technical Settings**  
  Wide‑angle lens (24–35 mm), short focal length. No bokeh; background fully sharp.  

- **Environment**  
  Outdoor shower area set in a rugged, natural stone wall. The wall is composed of irregular, dark gray rocks with light brown streaks. A metal showerhead is mounted on the upper left of the wall, water streaming down. Small patches of green foliage peek from the top right corner.  

- **Composition & Framing**  
  Candid, unposed, spontaneous framing—slight tilt and off‑center crop. No rule‑of‑thirds intentionality; the subject occupies the central‑right quadrant, with the showerhead and wall forming a natural vertical line.  

- **Post‑Processing**  
  Zero studio lighting, zero professional composition, zero editorial tone. Raw, in‑camera look only.
