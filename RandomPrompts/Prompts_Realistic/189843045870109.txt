- **Environment**  
  Specific real‑world location: a modern office with light wood desks, a coffee maker, a white mug, a stack of notebooks, and a window that lets in bright daylight. Time of day: mid‑afternoon, as indicated by the warm, slightly harsh sunlight streaming through the window. Weather or ambient conditions: clear, sunny, with a faint haze from the outside.

- **Lens & Technical**  
  Short focal length (24–35 mm), wide‑angle lens. No bokeh; background is fully sharp—everything in focus, no portrait‑mode subject isolation. The depth of field is flat, capturing the entire scene from foreground to background.
