**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

- **Subject & Action**  
  A young man with a neatly styled, dark brown haircut and a light beard, wearing an olive‑green short‑sleeve t‑shirt, holding a silver DSLR camera with a black strap. He is mid‑gesture, slightly tilting his head downward as if reviewing a photo or adjusting the lens, with a subtle smile. His hands grip the camera firmly, fingers curled around the body and lens, showing a natural, unposed moment.

- **Capture Style**  
  Candid, spontaneous, “in‑action” shot; the subject appears unaware of the camera capturing him. Slight handheld shake is present—tiny motion blur at the edges to emphasize the casual nature of the snap. No rule‑of‑thirds: the frame is off‑center, a partial crop that gives an informal feel. The image has a raw, unprocessed look: no filters, no color grading, no post‑processing aesthetic. A mild overexposure in the highlights keeps the ambient light warm and natural.

- **Environment**  
  Location: a sunlit park with tall, leafy trees and a grassy meadow in the background. Time of day: late afternoon, around 5 pm, where the sun is low but still bright, casting soft golden hues. Weather: clear, breezy, with a few scattered clouds overhead. The background is fully sharp—no bokeh or depth‑of‑field blur—so everything from foreground to background stays in focus.

- **Lens & Technical**  
  Lens: 24 mm wide‑angle, short focal length, giving a broad field of view. Aperture: f/5.6 (handheld, natural depth). Shutter speed: 1/60 s (slight motion blur). ISO: 400 (mid‑range for clean detail). No portrait‑mode subject isolation; the entire scene is crisp. The composition is accidental—no intentional rule‑of‑thirds, just a spontaneous, real‑life capture.

- **Post‑Processing**  
  Auto‑HDR or auto‑beauty mode off. No filters, no color grading, no post‑processing aesthetic. Keep the raw, natural feel of the original shot.

**Prompt Summary**  
> “A young man with a dark brown haircut and light beard, wearing an olive‑green t‑shirt, holding a silver DSLR camera with a black strap, mid‑gesture in a sunlit park at late afternoon. Candid, spontaneous capture with slight handheld shake, wide‑angle 24 mm lens, f/5.6 aperture, 1/60 s shutter speed, fully sharp background, raw unprocessed look, accidental off‑center framing.”
