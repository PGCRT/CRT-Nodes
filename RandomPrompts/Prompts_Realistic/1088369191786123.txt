**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

- **Subject & Pose**  
  A young woman with long, wavy brown hair, wearing a delicate pink lace lingerie set, is captured mid‑movement on a plush pink rug. She’s kneeling, slightly turned to the side, one hand raised to her head as if adjusting her hair or shielding her eyes from the sun. Her back is partially toward the camera, giving an intimate, candid feel.

- **Motion & Focus**  
  Slight motion blur at the edges from handheld shake; everything in focus—no portrait‑mode depth blur. The image has a raw, unprocessed look: no filters, no color grading, just natural lighting with a hint of overexposure to keep the ambient light warm.

- **Environment**  
  A bright, sunlit bedroom with pale pink walls and white trim. A vintage wooden chair with floral upholstery sits to the left, a small ornate table holds a vase of pink and white roses on the right, and a crystal chandelier hangs from the ceiling. The time of day is late morning, with soft natural daylight streaming through a window, creating gentle highlights and subtle shadows.

- **Lens & Technical**  
  Wide‑angle lens (24–35 mm), short focal length, no bokeh—background fully sharp. The composition is accidental: slight tilt, off‑center framing, partial crop. No rule‑of‑thirds intentionality; the frame captures the subject and surroundings as they naturally appear.

- **Capture Style**  
  Candid, unposed, spontaneous—the woman appears unaware of the camera, mid‑action. Imperfections are encouraged: a slight tilt to the left, a partial crop that cuts off a bit of the chair’s backrest. The image feels like a snapshot taken with a handheld camera in a real‑world setting.

- **Final Prompt**  
  “A young woman in pink lace lingerie kneeling on a pink rug in a sunlit bedroom, captured with a wide‑angle lens at late morning. She’s mid‑adjustment, hand near her head, back slightly turned to the camera. Slight motion blur from handheld shake, everything in focus, raw unprocessed look, no filters. The room features a vintage wooden chair, an ornate table with roses, and a crystal chandelier. The composition is accidental—slight tilt, off‑center framing, partial crop—creating a candid, spontaneous snapshot.”
