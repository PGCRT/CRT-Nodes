**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

- **Subject & Pose**  
  - A young woman in a relaxed, mid‑motion pose, captured mid‑breath or mid‑step, with a natural, unposed stance.  
  - Her body is slightly tilted to the left, creating an off‑center, accidental framing that gives a candid feel.  
  - The shot is a close‑up of her torso and upper thighs, cropped tightly so that only a small portion of her face is visible at the top left corner.  

- **Lighting & Exposure**  
  - Ambient daylight from a high‑angle window; slight overexposure on the right side of the frame, giving a warm, soft glow.  
  - No auto‑HDR or beauty filters—raw, unprocessed look with natural contrast.  
  - Slight handheld shake is present, creating a subtle motion blur that adds realism.  

- **Lens & Technical Settings**  
  - 24‑mm wide‑angle lens (short focal length) for a broad field of view.  
  - Everything in focus—no bokeh or depth‑of‑field blur; background remains sharp.  
  - ISO 400, f/4 aperture, 1/125 s shutter speed, 30 fps for a natural freeze/blur effect.  

- **Environment**  
  - Location: A bright, unadorned indoor space—a simple white wall with a faint texture, a small window on the right side.  
  - Time of day: Late morning, soft sunlight casting gentle shadows.  
  - Weather: Clear, dry air; no wind, but the subject’s hair moves slightly, adding a natural dynamic element.  

- **Composition & Style Notes**  
  - No rule‑of‑thirds intentionality—framing is accidental and spontaneous.  
  - Slight tilt and partial crop give an intimate, candid feel.  
  - Imperfections are encouraged: minor blemishes on the skin, a small water droplet on the left breast, and a faint reflection from the window.  

- **Prompt for Text‑to‑Image Generation**  
  *“A close‑up candid shot of a young woman’s torso and upper thighs, captured mid‑motion with a slight handheld shake. The frame is off‑center to the left, cropped tightly, with a small portion of her face peeking from the top left corner. Ambient daylight from a high‑angle window creates a warm, slightly overexposed glow on the right side. Shot on a 24‑mm wide‑angle lens, everything in focus with no bokeh. The background is a plain white wall with a faint texture and a small window on the right. Late morning sunlight, clear dry air, natural motion blur. No filters or post‑processing—raw, unprocessed realism.”*

Provide only the structured prompt.
