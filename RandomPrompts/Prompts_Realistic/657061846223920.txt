**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

- **Subject & Pose**  
  A tall, slender woman with long dark hair, wearing a black lace bodysuit and fishnet stockings, standing on a rugged cliff edge. She is mid‑step, one foot planted on the rock and the other slightly lifted, her hands raised behind her head in a casual, spontaneous pose. Her face is turned upward toward the sky, eyes closed as if she’s savoring the wind.

- **Lighting & Exposure**  
  Slight overexposure from a bright midday sun, creating a soft halo around the subject. The exposure is uneven—the upper part of the frame is slightly brighter than the lower part—giving a raw, unprocessed look. No auto‑beauty mode; no filters, no color grading, no post‑processing.

- **Camera Settings**  
  Wide‑angle lens (24 mm), short focal length, handheld. Slight motion blur from the subject’s movement and the camera’s instability. Everything is in focus—no bokeh, no depth‑of‑field blur; the background (a blue sky with fluffy white clouds and a textured rock face) remains sharp.

- **Composition**  
  Candid, unposed, spontaneous framing. The shot is slightly tilted, off‑center, and partially cropped on the right side by the cliff’s edge. No rule‑of‑thirds intentionality—just accidental, natural composition. The subject occupies the central‑right portion of the frame, with the sky and clouds filling the upper left.

- **Environment**  
  Location: a high, rocky outcrop on a coastal cliff. Time of day: mid‑afternoon, when the sun is high but not directly overhead. Weather: clear, bright, with scattered cumulus clouds. Ambient light creates a slight haze and a faint wind‑blown feel.

- **Lens & Technical**  
  Short focal length (24 mm) for wide coverage. No bokeh; background fully sharp. Zero studio lighting, zero professional composition, zero editorial tone. All elements—subject, rock, sky, clouds—are in focus.

- **Final Prompt**  
  “A tall woman with long dark hair, wearing a black lace bodysuit and fishnet stockings, stands on a rugged cliff edge at mid‑afternoon. She is mid‑step, one foot planted, the other slightly lifted, hands raised behind her head, face turned upward toward a bright blue sky with fluffy white clouds. The shot is taken with a 24 mm wide‑angle lens, handheld, with slight motion blur and a small overexposure. The frame is slightly tilted and off‑center, partially cropped on the right, capturing a candid, spontaneous moment. Everything—subject, rock, sky, clouds—is in focus, no filters or post‑processing, raw and natural.”
