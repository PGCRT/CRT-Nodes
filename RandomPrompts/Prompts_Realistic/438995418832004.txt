**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

- **Subject & Pose**  
  A tall, athletic woman in her late twenties, with a lean, toned build, standing mid‑stride in an office loft. She wears a dark, ribbed tank top and high‑waisted bike shorts that hug her hips and thighs. Her hair is tied back in a loose ponytail, a few strands framing her face. She’s wearing black-framed glasses, a gold watch on her left wrist, and a subtle smile. Her hands rest on her hips, elbows bent, knees slightly bent as if she just finished a quick stretch or is about to pick something up from a desk.

- **Facial Features & Expression**  
  Broad nose, defined cheekbones, and a warm, engaging smile. Her eyes are bright, looking slightly off‑camera, giving a candid feel. Slight motion blur on her face from a handheld shot, capturing the moment as she moves.

- **Clothing & Accessories**  
  The tank top is a dark charcoal with a subtle texture, a racerback style that shows off her shoulder muscles. The bike shorts are a matching dark tone, fitted at the waist with a visible button closure. A gold watch on her left wrist adds a small metallic accent. Her hair is a light brown, slightly frizzy at the ends, tied in a low ponytail that swings as she moves.

- **Pose & Body Language**  
  She’s in a dynamic stance: knees bent, hips slightly turned, hands on hips, elbows out, creating a sense of readiness or playful confidence. Her left foot is forward, right foot behind, giving a slight diagonal angle to the frame. The pose conveys motion and spontaneity.

- **Lighting & Exposure**  
  Natural daylight from a large window, mid‑afternoon, soft but warm. Slight overexposure on the upper body, creating a subtle halo effect. No auto‑HDR or beauty mode—raw, unprocessed look. The exposure is slightly uneven, with the background a bit brighter than the subject, adding to the candid feel.

- **Background & Setting**  
  A modern office loft with floor‑to‑ceiling windows that overlook a city skyline. Inside, a white desk with a black office chair and a small potted plant are visible. The floor is a light gray carpet. The window’s frame is white, and the daylight creates soft shadows on her face and body.

- **Lens & Technical**  
  24‑mm wide‑angle lens, short focal length, handheld. Everything is in focus—no bokeh or depth‑of‑field blur. Slight tilt and off‑center framing; the composition is accidental, not intentional rule‑of‑thirds. The shot has a slight motion blur from the subject’s movement and the photographer’s hand.

- **Mood & Atmosphere**  
  Casual, spontaneous, energetic. The image feels like a snapshot captured in the moment, as if the woman just finished a quick workout or is about to grab something from the desk. The overall tone is natural, unposed, and real.
