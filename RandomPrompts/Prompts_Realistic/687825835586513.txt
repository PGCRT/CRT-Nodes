**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

- **Subject & Pose**  
  A young woman with long, wavy brown hair that’s slightly blown by the wind, wearing a black, deep‑V, long‑sleeved top with a subtle zip‑front. She’s captured mid‑movement, leaning forward as if she’s about to pick something up from a shelf, her face turned toward the camera but not posing—her expression is natural and unposed, a faint smile on her lips.

- **Capture Style**  
  Candid, unposed, spontaneous — subject unaware or mid‑action. The shot has slight motion blur at the edges to suggest handheld instability, with a tiny overexposure in the upper right corner that gives it a raw, unprocessed look. No filters, no color grading, no post‑processing aesthetic. The framing is off‑center and slightly tilted, a partial crop that keeps the composition accidental and organic.

- **Environment**  
  A cluttered, lived‑in bedroom or living space. Behind the woman, a wooden shelf holds a bottle of water, a can of soup, a bag of rice, and a blue plastic bag on the floor. A framed photo sits on a small table to the left, and a green bucket is visible beneath the shelf. The time of day is late afternoon; the ambient light is warm and slightly hazy, creating a soft glow on the subject’s face and a faint halo around her hair.

- **Lens & Technical**  
  Short focal length (24‑35 mm) wide‑angle lens, no bokeh, no depth‑of‑field blur — everything from foreground to background is fully sharp. The image uses a wide‑angle “auto‑HDR” or “auto‑beauty” mode off; the exposure is slightly uneven, with a mild overexposure in the upper right and a tiny underexposed lower left corner. No portrait‑mode subject isolation—everything stays in focus.

- **Composition Notes**  
  The shot is centered on the woman’s torso and face, but the frame is tilted 15° to the right, giving an accidental, spontaneous feel. The background shelf and items are fully sharp, reinforcing the “no rule‑of‑thirds intentionality” principle. The slight motion blur is concentrated at the edges, especially around the hair, suggesting a quick, handheld snap.

- **Final Prompt**  
  *“A candid, unposed photograph of a young woman with long wavy brown hair, wearing a black deep‑V long‑sleeved top, captured mid‑movement in a cluttered bedroom. The image uses a short focal length wide‑angle lens, everything fully sharp with no bokeh, slight motion blur at the edges and a tiny overexposure in the upper right corner. The background shows a wooden shelf with a water bottle, soup can, rice bag, and a blue plastic bag on the floor, plus a framed photo on a table and a green bucket beneath the shelf. Late afternoon ambient light gives a warm haze. The frame is slightly tilted and off‑center, creating an accidental, organic composition.”*
