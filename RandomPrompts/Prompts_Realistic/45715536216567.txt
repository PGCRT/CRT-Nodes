**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

- **Subject & Pose**  
  A young woman with long, wavy dark hair, wearing a black strappy bodysuit and beige high‑waist trousers. She stands in a casual, unposed stance, slightly turned to the side, hands relaxed at her sides. Her face is turned toward the camera but she appears unaware of the lens—mid‑action, as if caught in a spontaneous moment.

- **Capture Style**  
  Candid, unposed, spontaneous — subject unaware or mid‑action.  
  Slight motion blur from handheld instability.  
  No filters, no color grading, no post‑processing aesthetic.  
  Slight overexposure on the upper torso to emphasize the raw feel of the shot.  
  Wide‑angle lens (24–35 mm) for a natural, wide capture.

- **Environment**  
  Location: A sunlit alleyway in an urban loft, with a concrete wall and a wooden beam visible in the background.  
  Time of day: Late afternoon, soft golden light from the west, creating gentle shadows.  
  Weather: Light breeze, clear sky, ambient noise of distant city.

- **Lens & Technical**  
  Short focal length (35 mm), wide‑angle.  
  No bokeh — background fully sharp, everything in focus.  
  No portrait mode or subject isolation; the entire frame is crisp.  
  Slight tilt and off‑center framing—no rule‑of‑thirds intentionality.  
  Slight partial crop on the right side to give a casual, spontaneous feel.

- **Lighting & Exposure**  
  Zero studio lighting — natural daylight only.  
  Slight overexposure on the upper torso to keep the image raw and unprocessed.  
  Auto‑HDR off; no color grading or post‑processing.  

- **Composition Notes**  
  The composition is accidental: the subject’s left shoulder is slightly higher than the right, creating a subtle diagonal line.  
  The background remains fully sharp, with the concrete wall and wooden beam providing context.  
  The frame cuts off just above the subject’s left hip, giving a partial crop that enhances candidness.

- **Final Prompt**  
  “Candid, unposed photograph of a woman in a black strappy bodysuit and beige high‑waist trousers, standing in a sunlit urban alleyway at late afternoon. Capture with a 35 mm wide‑angle lens, slight motion blur and overexposure on the torso, natural daylight, no filters or color grading. The shot is slightly tilted and off‑center, partially cropped on the right, with a fully sharp background of concrete wall and wooden beam, all in focus.”
