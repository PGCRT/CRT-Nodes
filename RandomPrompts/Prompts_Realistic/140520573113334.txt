**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

- **Subject & Pose**  
  A young woman with long, straight chest‑brown hair, wearing a translucent white button‑down shirt and no underwear, leaning forward on a gray sofa. Her hands rest on her knees, fingers slightly curled, nails painted pale pink. She’s mid‑movement, as if just finishing a stretch or a gentle dance move, with a subtle motion blur around her hands and knees to convey spontaneity.

- **Capture Style**  
  Candid, unposed, spontaneous — the subject is unaware of the camera, caught in a natural, “in‑the‑moment” pose. Slight tilt and off‑center framing give an accidental, handheld feel. The shot is partially cropped on the left side, leaving a small margin of empty wall space that adds to the informal vibe.

- **Environment**  
  Location: A cozy living room in a modern apartment. The background shows a light‑gray fabric sofa with a blue striped pillow on the left, a wooden floor visible at the bottom, and a beige wallpaper with subtle floral motifs on the wall. Time of day: late afternoon, soft natural light coming from the west, creating a warm, slightly overexposed highlight on the subject’s face and shirt. Ambient conditions: mild temperature, quiet, no wind.

- **Lens & Technical**  
  Short focal length (24‑35 mm) wide‑angle lens, handheld. Everything is in focus — background and subject — with a slight motion blur on the hands/knees area to suggest movement. No bokeh; depth of field is fully sharp. Auto‑HDR or auto‑beauty mode off — raw, unprocessed look. Slight overexposure on the subject’s shirt, giving a bright, natural glow. No color grading or post‑processing filters. The image has a zero‑studio‑lighting feel, as if captured by a casual photographer with a DSLR or mirrorless camera.

- **Composition Notes**  
  No rule‑of‑thirds intentionality — the framing is accidental, with the subject’s head slightly above the center of the frame and her left hand near the lower edge. The shot is a wide‑angle capture that includes the sofa, pillow, floor, and wall, creating an immersive, real‑world context. All elements (sofa, pillow, wallpaper, floor) are fully sharp.

- **Final Prompt**  
  “A candid, handheld shot of a young woman with long chest‑brown hair, wearing a translucent white button‑down shirt, leaning forward on a gray sofa in a modern apartment. Her hands rest on her knees, fingers slightly curled, nails painted pale pink. The image has slight motion blur around her hands and knees, capturing mid‑movement.
