**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

- **Subject & Pose**  
  A young woman with long, straight brown hair, wearing a light‑denim jacket that is slightly open at the front and white latex gloves on both hands. She stands in a relaxed, mid‑action pose, hands placed on her hips, head tilted back, eyes closed as if she’s just finished a movement or is about to do a dance move. Her expression is natural, unposed, with a subtle smile.

- **Lighting & Exposure**  
  Natural daylight from a western window, mid‑afternoon (around 4 pm). Slight overexposure on the upper body to keep the background bright but not washed out. No auto‑beauty mode; raw, unprocessed look. The exposure is uneven—slightly brighter at the top than at the bottom—to give a candid feel.

- **Camera & Lens**  
  DSLR or mirrorless with a wide‑angle lens (24 mm). Short focal length, no bokeh—everything from foreground to background is fully sharp. No portrait mode or subject isolation; the entire scene is in focus. Slight handheld shake results in a natural motion blur on the edges.

- **Composition & Framing**  
  Candid, unposed, spontaneous framing. The shot is slightly tilted and off‑center, with a partial crop that cuts off a bit of the background on the left side. No rule‑of‑thirds intentionality—just accidental, organic composition. The background is a plain, light‑colored wall with a faint texture; no decorative elements.

- **Environment**  
  A simple office or studio space with a beige wall and a small window to the west. Time of day: mid‑afternoon, soft natural light. Weather: clear, no wind. The ambient light creates a slight highlight on the subject’s left shoulder.

- **Technical Details**  
  - Focal length: 24 mm (wide angle)  
  - Aperture: f/5.6 (mid‑range)  
  - Shutter speed: 1/125 s (to capture slight motion blur)  
  - ISO: 400 (for natural grain)  
  - No filters, no color grading, no post‑processing aesthetic—just raw capture.

- **Final Prompt**  
  “A young woman with long brown hair, wearing a light denim jacket and white latex gloves, stands in a relaxed mid‑action pose with hands on hips and head tilted back, eyes closed. Natural daylight from a western window at mid‑afternoon creates slight overexposure on the upper body. Shot on a DSLR with a 24 mm wide‑angle lens, everything in focus with no bokeh. The frame is slightly tilted and off‑center, partially cropped, capturing a candid, spontaneous moment against a plain beige wall. No filters or post‑processing—raw, unprocessed realism.”
