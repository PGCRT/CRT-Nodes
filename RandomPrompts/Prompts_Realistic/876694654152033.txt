**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

- **Subject & Pose**  
  A young woman with long, wavy brown hair standing on a sunlit beach, wearing a black, long‑sleeved, high‑slit bodysuit that features a central zipper. She is mid‑turn, one hand on her hip, the other relaxed by her side, body slightly angled toward the camera, capturing a candid, unposed moment.

- **Motion & Focus**  
  Slight motion blur at the edges from handheld shake; everything in focus—no portrait‑mode depth of field. The image has a raw, auto‑HDR feel with no filters or color grading, a hint of overexposure from the ambient sunlight.

- **Lens & Technical**  
  Wide‑angle lens (24–35 mm), short focal length, no bokeh—background fully sharp. The shot is taken at a low angle to emphasize the subject’s silhouette against the rocks and sand.

- **Environment**  
  Specific real‑world location: a rocky beach with large boulders in the background, the sand textured and uneven. Time of day: late afternoon, golden hour light casting warm highlights and soft shadows. Weather: clear, breezy, with a slight wind blowing the hair.

- **Composition & Framing**  
  No rule‑of‑thirds intentionality—accidental framing with a slight tilt and off‑center crop. The composition feels spontaneous, as if the subject was caught mid‑action.

- **Lighting**  
  Zero studio lighting; natural sunlight creates a slight overexposure on the subject’s face and upper body, while the background remains fully sharp.

- **Post‑Processing**  
  Auto‑HDR or auto‑beauty mode off—raw, unprocessed look. No color grading or filters applied.
