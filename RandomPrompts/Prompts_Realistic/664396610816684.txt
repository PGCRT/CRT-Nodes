**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

- **Subject & Pose**  
  A young woman with long brown hair tied in a loose braid, wearing a light‑blue triangle bikini top and matching bottom, sitting on a white‑sheeted bed. She is leaning forward slightly, hands resting on the mattress, head tilted to the left, eyes looking directly at the camera with a soft, natural smile. Her glasses have thin metal frames that catch the light.

- **Lighting & Exposure**  
  Natural daylight from a western window, mid‑afternoon, creating a warm, slightly overexposed highlight on her chest and left shoulder. The exposure is uneven—soft highlights with a faint vignette at the corners. No auto‑beauty or filter processing; raw, unedited look.

- **Camera & Lens Settings**  
  35 mm wide‑angle lens, f/4.0 aperture, 1/125 s shutter speed, ISO 400. Slight handheld shake gives a subtle motion blur around the edges. Everything in focus—no portrait‑mode depth‑of‑field blur; background (a wooden headboard and two white pillows) is fully sharp.

- **Composition & Framing**  
  Candid, unposed framing: the shot is slightly off‑center, with the subject’s left side cutting close to the right edge. The composition has no intentional rule‑of‑thirds; the bed’s corner and headboard create a natural frame. The top of the frame captures the upper part of her head and the wooden headboard, while the bottom cuts just below her knees.

- **Environment**  
  A simple bedroom with a light‑wood headboard, white pillows, and a plain beige wall. The time of day is late afternoon, giving a warm, slightly hazy ambient light. A faint window glare is visible on the left side of the frame.

- **Technical Details**  
  Short focal length (35 mm) gives a wide‑angle view that keeps the background fully sharp but keeps the subject prominent. No bokeh—everything from foreground to background is in focus. Slight tilt to the right adds a casual, spontaneous feel. The image has a natural motion freeze or slight blur from handheld instability, giving it a candid vibe.
