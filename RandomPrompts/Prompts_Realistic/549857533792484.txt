**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

- **Subject & Pose**  
  A young woman with long, wavy brown hair standing mid‑step on a wooden park bench, wearing a black lace bra and matching lace panties. Her hands rest lightly at her sides, slightly tilted to the left, capturing an informal, spontaneous moment. She’s smiling softly, eyes bright, with a natural, unposed expression as if she’s just finished a conversation or is about to start one.

- **Lighting & Exposure**  
  Warm, golden hour sunlight from the east casts a slight overexposure on her face and upper body, creating a soft glow. The exposure is uneven—her torso is slightly brighter than the background—giving a raw, unprocessed look. No auto‑beauty or filter enhancement; just natural ambient light with a hint of handheld instability.

- **Camera & Lens Settings**  
  - Lens: 24mm wide‑angle (short focal length) for a broad field of view.  
  - Aperture: f/5.6 – moderate depth of field, keeping foreground and background mostly sharp.  
  - Shutter speed: 1/125 s – enough to freeze the motion but leave a subtle blur from slight handheld shake.  
  - ISO: 400 – balanced for the golden hour lighting.  

- **Composition & Framing**  
  The shot is intentionally off‑center, with the subject’s left side cutting close to the frame edge. The bench runs diagonally across the lower third of the image, creating a dynamic line that guides the eye. No rule‑of‑thirds intentionality—this is a candid, accidental framing. The background shows a park with tall trees, scattered fallen leaves, and a paved pathway; a few distant cars are visible on the road, adding depth.

- **Environment & Time**  
  Location: A sunny, late‑afternoon park in a suburban city. The time of day is early evening (around 5 pm), giving warm, soft light. Weather is clear with a light breeze; the trees have a few fallen leaves on the ground, adding texture. The background is fully sharp—no bokeh or depth‑of‑field blur.

- **Technical Details**  
  - No portrait mode or subject isolation.  
  - Slight motion blur on the woman’s hands and the bench, indicating handheld shake.  
  - All elements (subject, bench, trees, pathway) are in focus.  
  - Raw, unprocessed look—no color grading or filters.
