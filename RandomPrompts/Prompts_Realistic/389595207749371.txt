**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

- **Subject & Pose**  
  A tall, slender woman with long, wavy brown hair that cascades over her left shoulder. She stands in a relaxed, natural pose, hands hanging loosely by her sides, feet planted shoulder‑width apart. Her torso is bare, showcasing a smooth, unadorned body with subtle contouring of the abdomen and hips. The camera captures her from a slightly low angle, emphasizing the full length of her figure.

- **Cinematic Capture Style**  
  - Slight motion blur on the subject’s hands and hair to suggest spontaneous movement.  
  - Auto‑HDR or auto‑beauty mode turned off – the image retains a raw, unprocessed look.  
  - No filters, no color grading, no post‑processing aesthetic.  
  - Slight overexposure in the upper torso area to highlight ambient light.  
  - Wide‑angle lens (24 mm) for a broad field of view.

- **Capture Style – Continuation**  
  - Candid, unposed, spontaneous — subject unaware or mid‑action.  
  - Imperfections encouraged: slight tilt, off‑center framing, partial crop.  
  - No rule‑of‑thirds intentionality — accidental framing.  
  - Zero studio lighting, zero professional composition, zero editorial tone.  
  - Natural motion freeze or slight blur from handheld instability.

- **Environment**  
  - Location: A quiet, sunlit meadow at the edge of a small town; the background is a mix of green grass and a faint line of trees.  
  - Time of day: Late afternoon, around 5 pm, with warm, golden light hitting the subject’s left side.  
  - Weather or ambient conditions: Light breeze, clear sky, no clouds.

- **Lens & Technical**  
  - Short focal length (24 mm), wide‑angle.  
  - No bokeh, no depth‑of‑field blur – background fully sharp.  
  - Everything in focus.  
  - No portrait mode, no subject isolation.

- **Prompt Summary for Generation**  
  “A tall, slender woman with long wavy brown hair standing bare in a natural, candid pose on a sunlit meadow at late afternoon. Capture with a 24 mm wide‑angle lens, slight motion blur on the hands and hair, raw unprocessed look, slight overexposure on the torso, accidental off‑center framing, everything in focus, no bokeh. The background shows green grass and a line of trees, fully sharp. Shoot from a low angle, handheld, with natural lighting.”

Provide only the structured prompt.
