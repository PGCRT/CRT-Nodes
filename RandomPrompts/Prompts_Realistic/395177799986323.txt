**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

- **Subject & Pose**  
  A young woman with long, dark, wavy hair, wearing a simple off‑the‑shoulder white top, captured mid‑turn as she gently tilts her head to the side. Her eyes are closed, lips slightly parted, conveying a moment of quiet contemplation or surprise. The pose is spontaneous and unposed; she appears unaware of the camera, caught in a natural, candid gesture.

- **Capture Style**  
  • Candid, unposed, spontaneous — subject unaware or mid‑action.  
  • Slight tilt and off‑center framing; partial crop that gives an accidental, handheld feel.  
  • No rule‑of‑thirds intentionality — the composition is organic, as if captured by a quick, instinctive shot.  
  • Natural motion freeze or slight blur from handheld instability, giving a subtle sense of movement.

- **Auto‑HDR / Auto‑Beauty**  
  • Auto‑HDR off – raw, unprocessed look.  
  • No filters, no color grading, no post‑processing aesthetic.  
  • Slight overexposure or underexposure depending on ambient light (choose a warm, golden‑hour tone).  

- **Lens & Technical**  
  • Short focal length (e.g., 24 mm) wide‑angle lens.  
  • No bokeh – background fully sharp, everything in focus.  
  • No portrait‑mode subject isolation; the entire frame is crisp.

- **Environment**  
  • Location: A sunlit park bench at dusk, with a blurred city skyline in the distance.  
  • Time of day: Late afternoon, golden hour.  
  • Weather: Light breeze, clear sky, soft natural light.  

- **Composition Notes**  
  • Frame the subject slightly above the center, with her left shoulder peeking out of a white off‑the‑shoulder top.  
  • Keep the background fully sharp – no depth‑of‑field blur.  
  • Capture the subtle texture of her hair and the slight moisture on her skin from the wind.  

- **Prompt Summary**  
  “A candid, wide‑angle shot of a young woman with long dark wavy hair, wearing an off‑the‑shoulder white top, captured mid‑turn with her eyes closed and lips parted, in a park at golden hour. The image is raw, unprocessed, with slight tilt and accidental framing, all in focus, no bokeh, handheld motion blur, and a fully sharp background.”
