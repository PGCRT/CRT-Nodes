**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework – “Beach Biker”**

- **Subject & Pose**  
  A young woman with long, wind‑blown dark hair, wearing a black bikini top and frayed denim shorts, crouching low on a sandy dune. Her hands are gently clasped over her chest, fingers slightly blurred as if she’s just finished a quick stretch or is about to pick up a beach ball. She looks upward toward the camera with a casual, confident expression, eyes bright, lips parted slightly. A gold chain adorns her neck, and a thin bracelet rests on her right wrist; a small ring glints on her left hand. Her bare feet are planted in the sand, toes curling into the grains.

- **Motion & Focus**  
  Slight motion blur at the fingertips and hair, indicating a handheld shot captured mid‑action. The entire frame is in focus – no portrait‑mode bokeh; background remains sharp from the dune to the grassy ridge behind her.

- **Lighting & Exposure**  
  Natural daylight from a high‑angle, late‑afternoon sun creates a warm, slightly overexposed highlight on her shoulder and chest. The sky shows a soft white‑blue hue with a few scattered clouds; the sand reflects the light, giving a subtle glare. No auto‑beauty or filter processing – raw, unedited look.

- **Environment**  
  Location: A coastal dune field on a quiet beach, with tall sea‑grass tufts rising behind her and a low ridge of sand in the foreground. Time of day: early evening (golden hour), weather: light breeze, clear sky. The background includes a few scattered shells and a small patch of seaweed, adding texture.

- **Lens & Technical**  
  Wide‑angle lens (24 mm) with a short focal length, handheld, slightly tilted. No rule‑of‑thirds – the composition is accidental, off‑center, with the subject’s left side cutting close to the frame edge. Everything from foreground to background is fully sharp; no depth‑of‑field blur.

- **Capture Style**  
  Candid, unposed, spontaneous – the woman appears unaware of the camera, caught mid‑gesture. Slight tilt and partial crop give a raw, documentary feel. The shot has zero studio lighting, zero professional composition, zero editorial tone.

- **Post‑Processing**  
  Auto‑HDR off; no color grading or filters. Only natural exposure adjustments.
