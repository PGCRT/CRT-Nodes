**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

- **Subject & Pose**  
  A young woman with light brown hair tied in a loose ponytail, wearing an olive‑green V‑neck T‑shirt. Her hands are gently clasped near her chest, fingers slightly curled, as if she’re about to adjust something or simply hold a small object. She looks directly at the camera with a soft, engaged expression, eyes wide and bright, a hint of a smile on her lips.  

- **Motion & Focus**  
  Slight motion blur on the hands and background to convey handheld shake; everything remains fully sharp—no portrait‑mode bokeh. The focus is intentional but casual, capturing a candid moment as if she’s mid‑action.  

- **Lighting & Exposure**  
  Natural daylight from a high‑angle source (mid‑afternoon), with a slight overexposure that gives the image a warm, luminous feel. No auto‑HDR or beauty filters—just raw, unprocessed light.  

- **Lens & Technical**  
  Wide‑angle lens (24‑35 mm), short focal length, no depth‑of‑field blur. The background is fully in focus, with a real‑world location that appears to be an outdoor patio or garden area; a stone wall and a white flower are visible behind her.  

- **Environment**  
  A sunlit patio with a textured concrete wall on the left, a blurred green plant and a white rose on the right, and a small window frame in the upper left corner. The time of day is late afternoon, with warm golden light casting soft shadows.  

- **Composition & Framing**  
  No rule‑of‑thirds intentionality—framing is accidental and off‑center. The shot is slightly tilted, creating a dynamic, spontaneous feel. The crop is partial; the woman’s left shoulder extends beyond the frame, adding to the candid vibe.  

- **Style Notes**  
  Capture a realistic, unposed moment with natural motion freeze or slight blur from handheld instability. Keep zero studio lighting, zero professional composition, and zero editorial tone.
