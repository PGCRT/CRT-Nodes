**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

- **Subject & Pose**  
  A young woman with long, wavy brown hair that catches the wind, wearing a light‑pink off‑the‑shoulder blouse with puffed sleeves and high‑waisted denim jeans. She’s mid‑walk, slightly turned toward the camera, sunglasses perched on her head, a thin gold necklace dangling, and a black watch on her left wrist. Her expression is casual, almost unaware of the lens—mouth slightly open as if she’re about to speak or laugh.

- **Motion & Focus**  
  Slight motion blur at the edges from handheld instability; everything in focus—from her face to the street behind—no bokeh, no depth‑of‑field blur. The image has a raw, unprocessed look: no filters, no color grading, no post‑processing aesthetic. A hint of overexposure on the bright daylight, giving a natural, spontaneous feel.

- **Lens & Technical**  
  Wide‑angle lens (24–35 mm), short focal length. No rule‑of‑thirds intentionality—framing is accidental, slightly tilted and off‑center. Background fully sharp, no portrait‑mode isolation. The composition is candid, unposed, spontaneous; the subject appears unaware or mid‑action.

- **Environment**  
  A bustling city street in an old brick district: red‑brick buildings line both sides of a paved road, storefronts with green awnings on the left and a coffee shop on the right. The time of day is late morning, the sun high but not directly overhead, casting soft shadows. A few pedestrians are visible in the background, blurred slightly by motion. The weather is clear, a light breeze ruffles her hair.

- **Lighting & Color**  
  Natural daylight, slight overexposure on the subject’s front, ambient light from the street. Warm tones from the brick, cool tones from the denim, subtle contrast between the pink blouse and blue jeans. The color palette is natural—no artificial tone‑mapping.

- **Details & Imperfections**  
  Slight tilt of the camera, partial crop that cuts off a bit of the street on the right side. Her watch strap is slightly askew, her sunglasses slide a little to one side. The background buildings show a mix of modern and vintage architecture, with a green awning, a black storefront sign, and a white coffee‑shop sign. The pavement has faint cracks and a small patch of grass at the corner.

- **Final Prompt**  
  “A young woman with long wavy brown hair, wearing a light pink off‑the‑shoulder blouse, high‑waisted denim jeans, a gold necklace, and a black watch, walking down a bustling city street lined with red‑brick buildings. Capture with a wide‑angle lens, slight motion blur, everything in focus, accidental framing, raw unprocessed look, natural daylight overexposure, late morning, clear weather, background pedestrians, slight tilt, partial crop.”
