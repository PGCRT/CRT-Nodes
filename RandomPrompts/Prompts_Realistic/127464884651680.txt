  Natural daylight from a side window, mid‑afternoon; slight overexposure on the subject’s back and hips, creating a warm, soft glow. No auto‑HDR or beauty mode—raw, unprocessed look.   Candid, unposed, spontaneous—subject appears unaware of the camera. Slight tilt and partial crop give an informal feel. No filters, color grading, or post‑processing.
