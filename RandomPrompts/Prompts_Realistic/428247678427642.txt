**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework – “Bedroom Lounge”**

- **Subject & Pose**  
  A young woman with long, dark brown hair lying on her stomach on a white‑sheeted bed, back to the camera, head turned slightly toward the window. She’s wearing a black lace bodysuit with delicate scalloped edges and matching thigh‑high lace stockings. Her left leg is bent, foot tucked under the sheet, right leg extended, creating a soft, organic line. One hand rests near her chin, the other on her hip, as if she’s about to adjust her hair or a scarf.

- **Lighting & Exposure**  
  Natural daylight from a large window on the left, mid‑afternoon, warm amber tones with a slight overexposure in the window area. The lamp on the bedside table casts a gentle glow; the overall exposure is slightly high, giving a raw, unprocessed look. No auto‑beauty or filter enhancement—just a handheld shot with a wide‑angle lens.

- **Camera & Lens Settings**  
  - Lens: 24‑mm wide‑angle (short focal length)  
  - Aperture: f/5.6 (mid‑range, everything in focus)  
  - Shutter speed: 1/60 s (slight motion blur from handheld instability)  
  - ISO: 400 (for natural grain and depth)  

- **Composition & Framing**  
  - No rule‑of‑thirds intentionality; the frame is slightly tilted, off‑center, capturing the woman’s back, the bed, a portion of the window, and the lamp.  
  - Background fully sharp—no bokeh, no portrait‑mode isolation.  
  - The shot includes a partial crop of the bed’s corner, the window’s curtain, and a small portion of the wall, adding spontaneity.

- **Environment & Ambience**  
  - Location: A modern bedroom with a dark gray headboard, white sheets, and a tall window with sheer curtains.  
  - Time of day: Late afternoon, soft sunlight streaming in, creating warm highlights and subtle shadows.  
  - Weather: Clear, calm—no wind, but the handheld shake adds a natural motion blur.

- **Details & Imperfections**  
  - Slight tilt and off‑center framing give the image a candid, unposed feel.  
  - Minor motion blur on the woman’s hair and legs, indicating a handheld shot.  
  - The lace details are crisp, with visible texture, and the white sheet shows natural creases.  
  - No post‑processing color grading—just raw, in‑camera aesthetics.

- **Final Prompt**  
  “A young woman in black lace bodysuit and thigh‑high stockings lies on a white‑sheeted bed, back to the camera, head turned toward the window, one hand near her chin, the other on her hip. Natural daylight from a large window casts warm amber tones with slight overexposure; a bedside lamp adds soft glow. Shot on a 24‑mm wide‑angle lens at f/5.6 and 1/60 s, ISO 400, handheld shake producing mild motion blur. The frame is slightly tilted and off‑center, capturing the woman, the bed, a portion of the window, and the lamp—everything in focus, no bokeh. The scene is a modern bedroom with a dark gray headboard, white sheets, and sheer curtains; the time of day is late afternoon. The image has raw, unprocessed realism, candid spontaneity, and subtle imperfections.”
