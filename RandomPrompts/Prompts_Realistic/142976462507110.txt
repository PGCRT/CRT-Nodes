**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

- **Subject & Action**  
  A young woman in a light‑beige long‑sleeve top and high‑waisted light‑blue jeans, carrying a brown leather tote, is mid‑conversation on a bustling city street. Her hands are clasped together near her chest, head tilted slightly to the right, eyes focused on a man beside her. She’s wearing white sneakers; a subtle motion blur conveys movement.

- **Pose & Composition**  
  Candid, unposed, spontaneous — the woman is unaware of the camera, caught in an instant of natural interaction. Slight tilt and off‑center framing give an accidental, handheld feel. The shot uses a wide‑angle lens (24–35 mm) to capture a broad view of the scene; everything from foreground to background stays fully sharp, no bokeh or depth‑of‑field blur.

- **Lighting & Exposure**  
  Raw, unprocessed look: no filters, no color grading, no post‑processing aesthetic. A mild overexposure on the bright midday sun creates a slight halo around the subjects, with natural ambient light casting soft shadows. The exposure is uneven—some parts slightly brighter than others—to preserve authenticity.

- **Environment**  
  Specific real‑world location: a narrow commercial street lined with brick buildings, small shops, and a blue “open” sign on a storefront. A red “no entry” traffic sign is visible on the right side. The street is paved, with a concrete curb and a faint line of white paint marking the lane. The time of day is late afternoon, around 4 pm, when the sun is high but not directly overhead, producing warm, golden light.

- **Weather & Ambient Conditions**  
  Light wind, mild temperature, clear sky. A few pedestrians are walking in the background, some blurred due to motion. The ambient noise is low, allowing the main subject’s conversation to be the focal point.

- **Lens & Technical Settings**  
  Short focal length (24–35 mm), wide‑angle, handheld. Aperture set to f/5.6–f/8 for moderate sharpness across the frame. Shutter speed around 1/125 s to capture slight motion blur. ISO 400–600 for clean detail. No portrait mode or subject isolation—everything from foreground to background is in focus.

- **Details & Imperfections**  
  Slight tilt of the camera, partial crop that cuts off a bit of the left side of the street. The woman’s hair has a slight stray strand, and her sneakers show a faint scuff. The background shop signs include a blue “open” sign and a red “no entry” sign, both in focus. The scene has zero studio lighting, zero professional composition, zero editorial tone—just an authentic, spontaneous snapshot.

- **Final Prompt**  
  “A young woman in a light‑beige long‑sleeve top and high‑waisted light‑blue jeans, carrying a brown leather tote, is mid‑conversation on a bustling city street. Her hands are clasped near her chest, head tilted to the right, eyes focused on a man beside her. The shot uses a wide‑angle lens (24–35 mm) to capture a broad view of the scene; everything from foreground to background stays fully sharp.
