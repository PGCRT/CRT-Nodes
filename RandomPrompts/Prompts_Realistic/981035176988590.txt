**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

  A woman with long, sun‑kissed brown hair, wearing a bright orange Calvin Klein bikini, standing back to the camera at a chain‑link fence. - **Motion & Focus**  
  Slight motion blur at the edges of the hands and hair, indicating handheld instability. Everything is fully sharp—no bokeh, no portrait‑mode isolation. The focus covers the entire frame: subject, fence, sign, and background.

- **Lighting & Exposure**  
  Raw, auto‑HDR off, no color grading or post‑processing. A mild overexposure on the upper back and shoulders, creating a warm, natural glow. The ambient light suggests mid‑afternoon sun, with a slight haze that gives a soft edge to the chain‑link fence.

  Wide‑angle lens (24‑35 mm), short focal length. No rule‑of‑thirds intentionality—framing is accidental, slightly tilted, off‑center. The background (a green metal structure behind the fence) remains fully in focus.

Time of day: mid‑afternoon, around 3 pm, with warm, high‑angle sunlight. Weather: clear, slightly humid, with a faint wind that ruffles the woman’s hair.

- **Composition & Details**  
  The shot captures the woman’s back and left side, her hips turned toward the camera, with a visible “Calvin Klein” logo on the bikini’s waistband. Her right hand grips a horizontal bar, while her left hand is out of frame. A white “NO TRESPASSING” sign is partially visible in the upper left corner of the fence.
