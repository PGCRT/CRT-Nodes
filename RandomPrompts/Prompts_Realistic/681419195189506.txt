**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

- **Subject & Pose**  
  A young woman with long, wavy brown hair sits casually in a woven rattan chair, her body slightly tilted to the left. She wears a black lace bra and matching thigh‑highs that drape over her right leg, which is bent at the knee. Her left arm rests on the back of the chair, while her right hand grips the side. A large rose tattoo adorns her left upper arm, and a smaller floral tattoo is visible on her right thigh. She wears a thin gold necklace and a delicate bracelet on her left wrist.

- **Lighting & Exposure**  
  Natural daylight from a western window casts a warm, slightly overexposed glow on the subject. The light hits the front of her face and upper body, creating soft highlights on her forehead and clavicle. Slight ambient haze gives a raw, unprocessed look; no auto‑HDR or beauty filters are applied.

- **Camera Settings**  
  - Lens: 24 mm wide‑angle, short focal length.  
  - Aperture: f/5.6 (mid‑range).  
  - Shutter speed: 1/125 s (handheld, slight motion blur on the subject’s fabric).  
  - ISO: 400.  
  Everything is in focus – background and foreground equally sharp; no bokeh or depth‑of‑field blur.

- **Composition & Framing**  
  The shot is intentionally off‑center: the chair’s back leans slightly to the right, and the top of the frame cuts just above her head. The composition is spontaneous, as if she was caught mid‑pose. The background shows a plain concrete wall with a faint vertical seam; a small portion of a beige cushion is visible inside the chair.

- **Environment**  
  Location: A modern loft in an urban apartment building, 3 p.m. daylight. The room has a neutral gray tone, a single window on the west side, and a concrete floor. A small potted plant sits in the corner, partially out of frame.

- **Details & Imperfections**  
  Slight tilt of the camera, minor handheld shake, and a partial crop that cuts off the top left corner of the chair. The woman’s expression is relaxed, with a subtle smile. Her fingers are slightly curled on the chair’s armrest. The lace details on her bra and thigh‑highs show fine texture, and the rose tattoo has a slight shadow from the light.

- **Post‑Processing**  
  No filters or color grading; only a minimal crop and a slight vibrancy boost. The image retains its raw, candid feel.
