**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

- **Subject & Pose**  
  A young woman with long, sun‑kissed hair sitting on the ground in a wild meadow, wearing a teal lace bikini. She is mid‑smile, head tilted slightly to the right, left leg bent and right leg extended, hands resting on her thighs. Her left ear is adorned with a dark, textured earring. The camera captures her from a low, wide‑angle perspective (24 mm), with a slight handheld shake that gives a natural motion blur around the edges.

- **Lighting & Exposure**  
  Ambient mid‑afternoon sunlight, soft but warm, coming from the north‑west. Slight overexposure on the upper part of the frame to keep the background bright and airy. No auto‑HDR or beauty filters—raw, unprocessed look. The exposure is slightly uneven, with a subtle vignette caused by the wide lens.

- **Environment**  
  A sunlit meadow filled with tall, dry grasses and a scattering of wildflowers. In the background, a line of green trees lines the horizon, and a small white can of “Sunkist” sits on the ground near her right hip. A black backpack and a plastic bag are visible to the left, adding to the candid, spontaneous feel. The time of day is late afternoon, with long shadows and golden hues.

- **Lens & Technical**  
  Short focal length (24 mm), wide‑angle lens, no bokeh—everything from foreground to background is fully sharp. No portrait‑mode depth‑of‑field blur; the entire scene is in focus. Slight tilt and off‑center framing give an accidental, non‑rule‑of‑thirds composition. The image has a raw, unposed, spontaneous vibe, as if captured mid‑action by a handheld camera.

- **Post‑Processing**  
  Zero filters, zero color grading, no post‑processing aesthetic. Only natural motion freeze or slight blur from handheld instability. Keep the look realistic and candid.
