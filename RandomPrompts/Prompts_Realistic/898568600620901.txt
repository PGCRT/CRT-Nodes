**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

- **Subject & Pose**  
  A tall, slender woman with long, dark wavy hair standing in a sunlit forest clearing. She wears an olive‑green, deep‑V, spaghetti‑strap dress that falls to mid‑calf with a subtle high slit on the left side. Her hands hang loosely at her sides, slightly bent at the elbows; she is mid‑step, one foot forward, as if caught in a casual walk. Her face is turned toward the camera but not directly aimed—slight tilt to the right, giving an unposed, spontaneous feel.

- **Capture Style**  
  Candid, unposed, spontaneous — subject unaware or mid‑action.  
  Slight motion blur on the hands and lower dress, indicating handheld shake.  
  No filters, no color grading, no post‑processing aesthetic.  
  Slight overexposure in the upper torso area to emphasize ambient light.  
  Wide‑angle lens (24 mm) capturing a broad view of the scene; everything from foreground to background is fully sharp—no bokeh or depth‑of‑field blur.

- **Environment**  
  Specific real‑world location: a tropical forest clearing with tall, slender tree trunks on either side, large green ferns at the base of a small waterfall in the background.  
  Time of day: late morning, soft golden light filtering through the canopy.  
  Weather or ambient conditions: clear, slightly humid air; leaves are wet from recent rain, giving a subtle sheen to the foliage.

- **Lens & Technical**  
  Short focal length (24 mm), wide‑angle.  
  No bokeh, no depth‑of‑field blur — background fully sharp.  
  Everything in focus: subject, trees, ferns, waterfall.  
  No portrait mode, no subject isolation.  
  Slight tilt and off‑center framing; partial crop of the left side of her dress, giving a casual, imperfect feel.

- **Lighting & Composition**  
  Zero studio lighting, zero professional composition, zero editorial tone.  
  Natural motion freeze or slight blur from handheld instability.  
  The camera is held at eye level, slightly above the subject’s head, with the lens focused on the subject’s mid‑torso, capturing her full figure and a portion of the background.

- **Prompt Summary**  
  “A tall woman in an olive‑green deep‑V dress stands mid‑step in a sunlit forest clearing, with a small waterfall behind her. The shot is taken with a 24 mm wide‑angle lens at eye level, capturing everything in sharp focus from the subject to the background foliage. Slight motion blur on the hands and lower dress conveys handheld shake, while a slight tilt and off‑center framing give a candid, spontaneous feel. The scene is lit by late‑morning golden light, with wet leaves adding a subtle sheen. No filters or post‑processing—just raw, natural realism.”
