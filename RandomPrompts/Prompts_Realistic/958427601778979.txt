**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

- **Subject & Pose**  
  A young woman with shoulder‑length, wavy brown hair, wearing a simple beige off‑shoulder top, captured mid‑turn as she leans slightly toward the camera. Her face is turned a few degrees to the left, eyes focused on a point just beyond the lens, giving a candid, unposed feel.

- **Capture Style**  
  Candid, unposed, spontaneous — subject unaware or mid‑action. Slight tilt and off‑center framing; partial crop that cuts off a bit of her left shoulder, creating a natural, imperfect composition. No rule‑of‑thirds intentionality—framing is accidental, as if taken with a handheld camera in motion.

- **Motion & Focus**  
  Slight motion blur on the subject’s hair and shoulders from handheld instability, but everything remains fully sharp—no portrait‑mode depth‑of‑field blur. No bokeh; background stays in focus.

- **Lighting & Exposure**  
  Zero studio lighting, zero professional composition, zero editorial tone. Ambient natural light from a bright, overcast afternoon; slight overexposure on the subject’s chest and shoulders, giving a raw, unprocessed look. Auto‑HDR or auto‑beauty mode off. No filters, no color grading, no post‑processing aesthetic.

- **Environment**  
  Specific real‑world location: a modern apartment loft with a white sofa and a concrete wall in the background. Time of day: late afternoon, soft diffused sunlight. Weather: lightly cloudy, ambient temperature 22 °C.

- **Lens & Technical**  
  Short focal length (35 mm), wide‑angle lens. No bokeh, no depth‑of‑field blur—background fully sharp. Everything in focus. Wide‑angle gives a slight distortion that adds to the candid feel. All settings: aperture f/4, shutter speed 1/125 s, ISO 400.
