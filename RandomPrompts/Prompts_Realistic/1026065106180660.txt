**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

- **Subject & Pose**  
  A young woman with dark, voluminous hair tied in a high ponytail, wearing oversized black sunglasses and a bright red leather jacket. She’s holding a black DSLR camera in both hands, mid‑shot as if she’s about to take a photo—her fingers slightly blurred from motion. Her pink scarf is loosely knotted at the neck, adding a pop of color. The jacket has visible silver snap buttons and flap pockets; beneath it, a floral shirt peeks out. She stands in front of a large glass storefront window that reflects the street behind her.

- **Facial Features & Expression**  
  Her face is partially obscured by the camera and sunglasses, giving a candid, unposed feel. A subtle smile is visible between the lenses, with a hint of teeth peeking through. The lighting is natural daylight, slightly overexposed on the front of her jacket, creating a warm glow.

- **Clothing & Accessories**  
  Red leather jacket: glossy finish, classic collar, two front pockets with snap closures. Pink scarf: thin, soft fabric, tied in a loose bow. Black sunglasses: thick frames, dark tint. Hair: braided strands framing the face, high ponytail secured with a pink elastic.

- **Background & Environment**  
  The setting is a bustling city street in an urban downtown area. Behind her, a brick building with a large glass window reflects the scene. A traffic light glows red in the distance. Pedestrians walk by in the background—some are blurred, some sharp. A white car passes on the street. The time of day is mid‑afternoon, with bright sunlight casting soft shadows.

- **Lens & Technical Settings**  
  Use a wide‑angle lens (24–35 mm). Shoot at a short focal length to capture the full figure and background. Keep everything in focus—no depth‑of‑field blur. Slight handheld shake creates a natural motion blur on the subject’s hands and the camera. No bokeh; background remains sharp.

- **Framing & Composition**  
  The shot is slightly off‑center, with the subject’s left side cutting off the frame. The composition is accidental—no rule‑of‑thirds. The camera is held at a low angle, making the subject appear taller and more commanding. The framing captures a partial crop of her left shoulder and the window reflection.

- **Lighting & Exposure**  
  Natural daylight with slight overexposure on the jacket’s front. No auto‑HDR or beauty mode—raw, unprocessed look. Slight natural shadows from the sun create subtle contrast.

- **Mood & Style**  
  Candid, spontaneous, “mid‑action” vibe. The subject appears unaware of the camera capturing her, adding authenticity. Slight tilt in the frame gives a dynamic feel.

- **Post‑Processing**  
  No filters, no color grading, no post‑processing aesthetic. Keep the image raw and natural.
