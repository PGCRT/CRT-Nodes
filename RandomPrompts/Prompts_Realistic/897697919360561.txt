**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

- **Subject & Pose**  
  A young woman with dark hair tied in a messy high ponytail, wearing a light‑blue denim jacket over a black bralette and frayed denim shorts, sitting on an asphalt street at night. Her left knee is wrapped in a black sleeve. She’s mid‑laugh, head tilted slightly to the right, eyes closed, hands planted on the ground for balance.  

- **Motion & Focus**  
  Slight motion blur on the subject’s hands and the background, indicating handheld instability. Everything remains sharply in focus—no portrait‑mode bokeh.  

- **Lighting & Exposure**  
  Raw, unprocessed look with a mild overexposure of the street lamps and ambient city glow. No auto‑beauty or filter enhancements.  

- **Lens & Technical**  
  Wide‑angle lens (24–35 mm), short focal length. All elements—subject, cars, buildings, streetlights—are fully sharp; no depth‑of‑field blur.  

- **Environment**  
  Urban night scene on a busy city street. Tall residential and commercial buildings line both sides of the road. A white sedan is parked on the left, another on the right. Street lamps cast warm yellow light, creating a slight haze. The time is late evening (around 10:30 pm), with the sky still dark but the street illuminated.  

- **Composition & Framing**  
  Candid, unposed, spontaneous—subject appears unaware of the camera. Slight tilt and off‑center framing; partial crop that cuts off the edges of the street. No rule‑of‑thirds intentionality.  

- **Details to Emulate**  
  - Slight overexposure on the street lamps and the subject’s face.  
  - Natural motion blur from handheld shot.  
  - Imperfections: slight tilt, off‑center framing, partial crop.  
  - Zero studio lighting, zero professional composition, zero editorial tone.
