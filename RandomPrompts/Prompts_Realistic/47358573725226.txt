**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

- **Subject & Pose**  
  A young woman with long, dark hair, wearing a white off‑shoulder crop top and black thong‑style bikini bottoms, standing on a stone patio. She’s mid‑step, left leg bent, right foot planted, hand near her ear as if she’s about to speak or adjust a headset. Her expression is bright, casual, slightly smirking.  

- **Motion & Focus**  
  Slight motion blur at the knees and hands to convey candid movement. Everything in focus—no portrait‑mode bokeh. Handheld shot with a wide‑angle lens (24–35 mm).  

- **Lighting & Exposure**  
  Natural daylight, mid‑afternoon, soft but warm ambient light. Slight overexposure on the face and upper body to keep the scene lively. No auto‑HDR or beauty filters—raw, unprocessed look.  

- **Environment**  
  Location: a sunlit patio with blue tiled wall and stone columns, surrounded by tall agave plants and palm fronds. The background is fully sharp, no depth‑of‑field blur.  

- **Lens & Technical**  
  Short focal length (24–35 mm), wide‑angle. No bokeh, no portrait isolation. All elements—subject, background, details—fully sharp.  

- **Composition**  
  Candid, unposed, spontaneous framing. Slight tilt and off‑center crop; no rule‑of‑thirds intentionality. The shot feels “in the moment.”  

- **Post‑Processing**  
  Zero filters, zero color grading, zero studio lighting. Raw, natural look with only minimal white‑balance adjustment.
