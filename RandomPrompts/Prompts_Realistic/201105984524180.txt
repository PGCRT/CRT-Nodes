**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

- **Subject & Pose**  
  A tall, curvy woman with long brown hair tied in a loose ponytail stands on a city street, facing away from the camera and slightly turned to her right. She wears a black‑and‑white striped halter bikini top and matching high‑waist bottoms, paired with silver metallic ankle boots that have a slight heel. Her left hand rests lightly on her hip, while her right arm hangs by her side; her head is tilted upward as if she’s looking at the building’s façade.  

- **Motion & Focus**  
  Slight motion blur at the edges from handheld instability; the subject’s legs and boots have a subtle streak. Everything in the frame—from her boots to the distant building—is fully sharp, no portrait‑mode blur. No auto‑HDR or beauty filters—just raw, unprocessed light.  

- **Lighting & Exposure**  
  Natural daylight, mid‑afternoon; the sun is high but slightly to the west, casting soft shadows on the pavement and a gentle warm glow on the building’s white walls. A faint overexposure on the upper part of the frame gives an airy feel.  

- **Environment**  
  The scene is a wide‑angle shot of a European‑style city square. In the background stands a grand, white neoclassical building with a green dome and a small cross on top; its balcony and windows are visible. A tall street lamp, a traffic sign, and a green tree line the corner. The pavement is concrete with a white crosswalk marking. The sky is pale blue with a few wispy clouds.  

- **Lens & Technical**  
  24‑mm wide‑angle lens, short focal length. No bokeh—background fully sharp. No rule‑of‑thirds intentionality; the composition is accidental, slightly off‑center. The frame captures the woman’s full figure from head to toe, with a partial crop that cuts off a bit of the building’s left side.  

- **Capture Style**  
  Candid, unposed, spontaneous—subject unaware or mid‑action. Slight tilt in the camera gives a dynamic feel. No studio lighting, no professional composition, no editorial tone.  

- **Post‑Processing**  
  Zero filters, zero color grading, zero post‑processing aesthetic. Only raw, natural light with slight overexposure on the upper part of the image.
