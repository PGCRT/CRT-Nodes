**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

- **Subject & Pose**  
  A young woman with long, wavy brown hair sitting mid‑pose on a set of brick stairs. Her hands are behind her head, elbows out, creating a casual, spontaneous look. She wears a rainbow‑striped halter bodysuit that clings to her torso and hips, paired with tall white leather boots that reach just above the knee. Her knees are slightly apart, one foot forward, as if she’s about to step or adjust her boot—capturing a moment of motion.

- **Lighting & Exposure**  
  Natural daylight from a high‑angle, mid‑afternoon sun; slight overexposure on the upper body to emphasize the bright colors of the bodysuit. No auto‑HDR or beauty filters—raw, unprocessed look with a subtle handheld shake that gives a slight motion blur around the edges.

- **Lens & Technical**  
  Wide‑angle lens (24–35 mm) with a short focal length to keep the entire scene in focus. No bokeh; background (brick stairs, a small potted plant, and a black metal railing) is fully sharp. Handheld shot with minor tilt and off‑center framing—no rule‑of‑thirds intentionality.

- **Environment**  
  Outdoor setting on a residential staircase made of reddish-brown bricks. A green potted plant sits to the right, adding a natural accent. The time of day is late morning, with warm, diffused sunlight casting soft shadows. Weather is clear, with a light breeze that creates a faint motion blur on the subject’s hair and boots.

- **Composition & Details**  
  Capture a candid, unposed moment where the subject appears unaware of the camera. Slight tilt and partial crop give an accidental, spontaneous feel. All elements—subject, stairs, plant, railing—are in focus, creating a wide‑angle, documentary‑style image. No studio lighting or professional composition; everything feels natural and raw.
