**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

- **Subject & Pose**  
  A young woman with a high ponytail, wearing black-rimmed glasses and an unbuttoned light‑blue button‑down shirt, sitting on a beige armchair in a bright office. She is mid‑gesture, hand near her face as if adjusting the glasses or touching her cheek, head tilted slightly to the left, eyes downcast with a subtle smile. Her hair is dark brown, loosely tied, a few strands framing her face. The shirt’s front buttons are undone, revealing a hint of her chest, giving a casual, spontaneous vibe.

- **Capture Style**  
  Candid, unposed, spontaneous — subject unaware or mid‑action. Slight tilt and off‑center framing; the composition is accidental, not rule‑of‑thirds. The shot has a raw, unprocessed look: no filters, no color grading, no post‑processing aesthetic. A slight overexposure in the window’s light gives a warm, natural glow.

- **Motion & Focus**  
  Slight motion blur from handheld instability, but everything remains fully sharp — no bokeh, no depth‑of‑field blur. The background (a wooden desk, a black office chair, a small black trash bin, and a window with daylight) is in focus.

- **Lens & Technical**  
  Wide‑angle lens, short focal length (~24 mm). Auto‑HDR or auto‑beauty mode off. No studio lighting; all light comes from the window and ambient day. The image captures a real‑world location, midday, with bright natural daylight and a slight haze from the window’s glare.

- **Environment**  
  Office setting: a wooden desk with a pink notebook, a black office chair, a small black trash bin, and a window that lets in warm daylight. The time of day is late morning; the weather is clear but slightly hazy, giving a soft ambient light.
