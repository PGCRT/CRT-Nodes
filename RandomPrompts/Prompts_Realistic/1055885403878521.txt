**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

- **Subject & Pose**  
  A tall, slender woman with long, wavy blonde hair standing barefoot on a sandy beach at sunset. She is mid‑step, one foot slightly ahead of the other, with her left hand resting lightly on her thigh and right arm hanging by her side. Her head is tilted slightly to the left, eyes focused forward, capturing an unposed, spontaneous moment.

- **Lighting & Exposure**  
  Warm, golden hour light from the setting sun creates a soft overexposure on the subject’s upper body, with a slight flare in the sky. The exposure is uneven—the foreground is a bit brighter than the background, giving a raw, unprocessed look. No auto‑beauty mode; no filters, no color grading, no post‑processing aesthetic.

- **Environment**  
  A tropical beach with gentle waves rolling onto the shore behind her. Palm trees line the far side of the beach, and a small hut is visible in the distance. The sky is a gradient of soft orange and pink hues, with scattered clouds. The time of day is late afternoon, just before dusk. Weather is mild, with a light breeze creating slight motion on the water’s surface.

- **Lens & Technical**  
  Wide‑angle lens (24 mm) with a short focal length, handheld. Everything is in focus—no bokeh, no depth‑of‑field blur. Slight motion blur on the subject’s legs and the waves, indicating a natural freeze or slight instability. The framing is accidental: the composition does not follow the rule of thirds; the shot is slightly off‑center, with a partial crop that gives it a candid feel.

- **Capture Style**  
  Candid, unposed, spontaneous—subject unaware or mid‑action. Imperfections encouraged: slight tilt, off‑center framing, partial crop. No rule‑of‑thirds intentionality — accidental framing. Zero studio lighting, zero professional composition, zero editorial tone. Natural motion freeze or slight blur from handheld instability.

- **Post‑Processing**  
  None. Raw, in‑camera look with auto‑HDR off. Slight overexposure on the subject’s upper body or underexposure on the background depending on ambient light.  

**Prompt Summary**  
“Candid photo of a tall, slender woman with long wavy blonde hair standing barefoot on a sandy beach at sunset, mid‑step with one foot ahead, hand on thigh, slight tilt and off‑center framing. Warm golden hour light creates soft overexposure on the subject. Background shows gentle waves, palm trees, and a distant hut under an orange‑pink sky. Shot on 24 mm wide‑angle lens, handheld, everything in focus with slight motion blur on the subject’s legs and the waves. Raw, unprocessed, no filters, no auto‑HDR, accidental composition, natural freeze.”
