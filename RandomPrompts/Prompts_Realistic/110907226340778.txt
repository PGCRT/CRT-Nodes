**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

- **Subject & Pose**  
  A young woman with deep brown, slightly frizzy hair, wearing a white halter‑top, lying on a neatly made bed and holding a plain white mug with both hands. Her fingers are painted pink; she wears a gold ring on her left hand. She looks directly at the camera with a soft, natural smile.  

- **Lighting & Exposure**  
  Slight overexposure from ambient daylight, no auto‑HDR or auto‑beauty filters. The exposure is a little bright in the center but retains a raw, unprocessed look.  

- **Lens & Technical Settings**  
  Wide‑angle lens (24 mm), short focal length, handheld shot with slight motion blur and minor tilt. Everything in focus – no bokeh or depth‑of‑field blur; background is fully sharp.  

- **Composition & Framing**  
  Candid, unposed, spontaneous framing: the shot is slightly off‑center, a partial crop that gives an accidental, natural feel. No rule‑of‑thirds intentionality. The composition captures her mid‑action as she holds the mug, with a hint of the bed’s white sheets and a neutral wall behind her.  

- **Environment**  
  Location: a bright, airy bedroom in a modern apartment. Time of day: late morning, natural sunlight coming from the west, creating soft shadows. Weather: clear, mild, with a slight haze that adds a warm tone to the image.  

- **Details & Imperfections**  
  Slight motion blur from handheld instability, minor tilt, and a partial crop that keeps the scene feeling spontaneous. The image has zero studio lighting, zero professional composition, and zero editorial tone – it looks like a genuine, everyday snapshot.  

- **Post‑Processing**  
  No filters, no color grading, no post‑processing aesthetic – just raw, natural colors and an auto‑beauty mode off.
