**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

- **Subject & Pose**  
  A plus‑size woman with long, wavy brown hair, wearing a coral‑red triangle bikini, sitting on a rusted metal bench. She’s mid‑smile, head tilted slightly to the right, left hand resting on her thigh, right hand gripping the bench rail. A gold watch is visible on her left wrist. Her posture is casual and spontaneous, as if she’re caught in an unplanned moment during a beach‑side photoshoot.

- **Capture Style**  
  Candid, unposed, spontaneous — subject unaware or mid‑action. Slight tilt and off‑center framing; the composition is accidental, not rule‑of‑thirds. The shot has a raw, unprocessed look: no filters, no color grading, no post‑processing aesthetic. A hint of slight overexposure from natural daylight, with a wide‑angle lens that keeps the entire scene in focus (no bokeh).

- **Motion & Focus**  
  Slight motion blur from handheld instability, natural freeze or soft blur. Everything in focus — background fully sharp, no portrait‑mode depth‑of‑field blur. The focus is on the subject’s face and upper body, but the surrounding environment remains clear.

- **Environment**  
  Specific real‑world location: a concrete pier with a rusted metal bench, overlooking a calm ocean. Time of day: late afternoon, golden hour, ambient light warm and slightly hazy. Weather: mild breeze, light clouds, natural sunlight from the west. The background includes a faint silhouette of a distant lighthouse and a few scattered seagulls in the sky.

- **Lens & Technical**  
  Short focal length (24‑35 mm), wide‑angle lens. No bokeh, no depth‑of‑field blur — background fully sharp. All elements in focus. Zero studio lighting, zero professional composition, zero editorial tone. Auto‑HDR or auto‑beauty mode off. Slight overexposure or underexposure depending on ambient light (choose natural overexposure).

- **Details & Imperfections**  
  Slight tilt, partial crop, accidental framing. The bench’s rusted texture is visible, the woman’s skin has a natural glow, and her bikini straps are tied in a casual knot. A small watch on her wrist adds a subtle accessory detail. The image captures a fleeting, candid moment with a natural, unpolished feel.

**Prompt Summary**  
“Candid, wide‑angle shot of a plus‑size woman in a coral‑red bikini sitting on a rusted metal bench at a concrete pier, late afternoon golden hour. Slight motion blur and accidental framing, everything in focus, raw look, no filters. The scene includes a distant lighthouse and seagulls, captured with a 24‑35 mm lens, natural overexposure, handheld instability.”
