**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework – “Blonde Woman on Wooden Chest”**

- **Subject & Pose**  
  A young woman with long, wavy blonde hair sits atop a rustic wooden chest in a dimly lit bedroom. Her hands are gently clasped over her chest, fingers slightly blurred from motion. She wears a simple black choker; a floral tattoo adorns her left upper arm. Her gaze is downward, contemplative, as if she’s about to do something intimate or personal.

- **Lighting & Exposure**  
  Ambient indoor lighting from a window on the right creates a soft, natural overexposure on the subject’s face and torso. Slight underexposure in the background keeps the focus on her. No auto‑HDR or beauty filters – raw, unprocessed look. The exposure is uneven, giving a candid feel.

- **Camera Settings**  
  - Lens: 24‑35 mm wide‑angle, short focal length.  
  - Focal depth: everything in focus – subject and background.  
  - Shutter speed: 1/60 s (slight motion blur on the hands).  
  - Aperture: f/4.0 (wide opening, minimal bokeh).  
  - ISO: 800 (for low‑light sensitivity).  

- **Composition & Framing**  
  Handheld shot with accidental tilt; the frame is off‑center, capturing the woman’s upper body and the chest she sits on. The background shows a dimly lit room with a wall lamp and a door handle visible on the right. No rule‑of‑thirds – it’s a spontaneous, candid capture.

- **Environment**  
  A bedroom at dusk. The window lets in warm, diffused light that creates a subtle halo around her hair. A small table with a vase is visible in the left corner. The room’s walls are painted a muted gray, and a wooden door is partially seen on the right.

- **Details & Imperfections**  
  Slight motion blur on the hands, indicating mid‑movement. The wood grain of the chest is visible, adding texture. Her nails are painted white, contrasting with her skin tone. A faint shadow falls across her face from the lamp.

- **Post‑Processing**  
  No filters or color grading – just a raw, natural look. Slight vignetting from the wide‑angle lens is present but not exaggerated. The image retains all the imperfections of a real, unposed moment.
