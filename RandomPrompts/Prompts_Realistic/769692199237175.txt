**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

- **Subject & Pose**  
  A young woman with a bright pink bob haircut, wearing a white crop‑top and pink bikini bottoms, standing in front of a rustic wooden cabin. She is mid‑gesture, one hand raised as if waving or catching a bubble, the other slightly out to the side. Her face shows a natural, unposed smile, eyes bright, with a hint of motion blur around her hands.

- **Lighting & Exposure**  
  Slight overexposure from ambient daylight (mid‑afternoon), giving a warm, soft glow. No auto‑beauty or filter processing – raw, handheld shot. The exposure is slightly uneven, with the upper part of the frame a little brighter than the lower.

- **Camera Settings**  
  Wide‑angle lens (24‑35 mm), short focal length. Everything in focus – no bokeh, no depth‑of‑field blur. Slight tilt and off‑center framing; the composition is accidental, not rule‑of‑thirds. The shot has a natural motion freeze or slight blur from handheld instability.

- **Environment**  
  Location: A wooden cabin with a large window, white blinds, and a small blue fish decoration on the glass. A light‑colored wooden cabinet stands to the left. Outside the window, a hint of green foliage is visible. Time of day: mid‑afternoon, bright but not harsh sunlight. Weather: clear, a few floating soap bubbles drift in the air.

- **Details & Imperfections**  
  The image has a raw, unprocessed look – no color grading or filters. Slight tilt of the camera, partial crop that cuts off the top left corner of the cabin. The background is fully sharp, no portrait‑mode isolation. The subject’s hair has a slight motion blur, indicating movement. A few soap bubbles are captured mid‑flight around her head and shoulders.
