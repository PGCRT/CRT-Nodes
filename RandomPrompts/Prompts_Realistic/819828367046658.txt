  Auto‑HDR off. The scene is lit by natural daylight coming from the west, creating a soft overexposure on the upper body and a subtle underexposure on the lower legs. No color grading or filters—raw, unprocessed look.

  Wide‑angle lens (24 mm), short focal length. No depth‑of‑field blur; background is fully sharp. The composition is accidental: no rule‑of‑thirds, slight off‑center framing, partial crop that cuts off a bit of the left chair and the right wall.

  Candid, spontaneous—subject unaware of camera. Slight tilt in the camera’s grip, partial crop that includes the subject’s torso, legs, and feet, plus a portion of the chair and wall. No editorial tone; it looks like a real, unposed snapshot.
