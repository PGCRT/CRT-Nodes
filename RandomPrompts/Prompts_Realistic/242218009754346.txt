**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

- **Subject & Pose**  
  A young woman with dark, slightly frizzy hair, standing in a tiled bathroom. She’s bare‑headed, hands raised to the back of her head as if she’s just finished a shower or is styling her hair. Her face is turned toward the camera but not directly at it—there’s a subtle, candid tilt. Visible tattoos on both wrists and a small one on her left collarbone add texture. Her skin has a natural, unretouched warmth; slight overexposure gives a soft glow.

- **Lighting & Exposure**  
  Natural daylight from a window on the left, creating a gentle side‑light that slightly overexposes the upper part of the frame. No auto‑beauty or filter processing—just raw, handheld exposure. The lighting is uneven enough to keep the image feeling spontaneous.

- **Camera Settings**  
  - Lens: 24 mm wide‑angle (short focal length) – everything in focus, no bokeh.  
  - Aperture: f/5.6 – moderate depth of field, background fully sharp.  
  - Shutter speed: 1/125 s – slight motion blur on the subject’s hands, indicating handheld instability.  
  - ISO: 400 – natural grain and contrast.

- **Composition & Framing**  
  The shot is off‑center; the subject’s left side leans slightly to the right of the center line. The frame cuts off just above her waist, cropping out most of the bathroom’s lower tiles. No rule‑of‑thirds intentionality—just accidental, candid framing. The background shows blue square tiles with white grout, a decorative white molding near the top, and a portion of a white window frame on the left.

- **Environment**  
  Location: A modern bathroom with a white door frame, blue tiles, and a decorative molding. Time of day: mid‑afternoon, natural light coming from a window on the left side. Ambient conditions: slight humidity in the air, giving a subtle sheen to the skin.

- **Details & Imperfections**  
  Slight tilt of the camera, minor motion blur on the hands, a tiny smudge on the left wrist tattoo. The image has no post‑processing color grading—just raw, realistic capture. A faint reflection in the window glass adds a subtle highlight to the left side of her face.

- **Final Prompt**  
  *“A candid, wide‑angle shot of a bare‑headed woman in a blue‑tiled bathroom, captured with a 24 mm lens at f/5.6 and 1/125 s. Her hands are raised to the back of her head, slight motion blur on the hands, natural daylight from a left‑side window creating a soft overexposure. The frame is off‑center, cropping just above her waist, with a white door frame on the left and decorative molding near the top. The image retains raw, unprocessed realism—no filters, no auto‑beauty, everything in focus, slight handheld tilt, and subtle imperfections.”*
