**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework – “Beach Walk”**

- **Subject & Pose**  
  A plus‑size woman with long, sun‑kissed blonde hair walking barefoot along a tropical beach. She wears a dark‑green floral bikini that contrasts with the warm sand. Her left leg is slightly bent, foot mid‑step, toes gripping the wet sand; her right foot is just about to lift off. She’s looking upward and to the right, as if she’s just spotted something in the distance, giving a candid, “mid‑action” feel.

- **Motion & Focus**  
  Slight motion blur on the feet and lower legs to suggest movement, while the rest of the frame stays sharp. Handheld camera shake is visible in the edges. No portrait‑mode depth‑of‑field blur – everything from foreground to background is in focus.

- **Lighting & Exposure**  
  Natural daylight, mid‑afternoon, with a slight overexposure on the sky and water to keep the look raw. The sun is high but not directly overhead, casting soft shadows that tilt slightly to the left. No auto‑beauty or filter processing – just a natural, unpolished aesthetic.

- **Environment**  
  A wide‑angle shot of a sandy beach with gentle waves breaking in the background. Palm trees line the far side of the shore, a few resort buildings are visible behind them, and a blue umbrella peeks out near the left edge. The sky is partly cloudy with a pale gray‑blue tone. The sea is a light turquoise, with white foam trailing along the shoreline.

- **Lens & Technical**  
  24‑mm wide‑angle lens (short focal length). No bokeh – the background is fully sharp. The composition is accidental: the subject is slightly off‑center, the frame tilts a little to the right, and the crop is not rule‑of‑thirds intentional. Everything from the sand in the foreground to the buildings in the distance is in focus.

- **Capture Style**  
  Candid, unposed, spontaneous – the woman appears unaware of the camera, mid‑step. Slight tilt and partial crop give a raw, documentary feel. No studio lighting, no professional composition, no editorial tone. The image feels like a snapshot captured on a breezy afternoon at the beach.

- **Post‑Processing**  
  Auto‑HDR off – keep it natural. No color grading or filters. A subtle increase in contrast to enhance the texture of the sand and the fabric of the bikini. Slight vignette only on the corners for a subtle wide‑angle effect.
