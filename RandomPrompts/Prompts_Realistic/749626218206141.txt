**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

- **Subject & Pose**

  Warm, ambient candlelight mixed with natural daylight from a window behind the subject. Slight overexposure on the upper half of the frame gives a subtle halo effect. No auto‑beauty or filter processing—just raw, unprocessed capture. The exposure is slightly uneven, with the lower part of the image a bit darker than the top, as if the camera’s meter was off by a fraction of a stop.

- **Camera & Lens**  
  24‑mm wide‑angle lens, handheld, shot at f/4.8, 1/60 s shutter speed, ISO 400. The lens is set to a short focal length for a “wide” look that keeps the background fully sharp—no bokeh, no portrait‑mode isolation. Slight motion blur on the subject’s hands and the side table conveys a natural, spontaneous feel.

- **Composition & Framing**  
  Candid, unposed framing: the shot is slightly tilted to the right, off‑center, with a partial crop that cuts off the left edge of the room. No rule‑of‑thirds intentionality—just a casual, spontaneous composition. The side table with candles sits to the left, partially in view; a man’s head and shoulder are visible at the bottom right, lying on the floor, adding an element of everyday spontaneity.

- **Environment**  
  A quiet, dimly lit living room with beige walls and a small wooden side table holding three white candles. The time of day is late afternoon, as indicated by the warm, golden hue. The room’s tiles reflect the candlelight, and a curtain hangs in the background, slightly out of focus but still sharp.

- **Details & Imperfections**  
  Slight tilt and off‑center framing give the image a raw, candid feel. A faint motion blur on the woman’s hands suggests she is mid‑movement. The lighting is uneven—slight overexposure on the upper half, underexposure on the lower half—creating a natural contrast. No post‑processing or color grading; the image retains its original, unfiltered look.

  “A plus‑size woman in a light‑pink ribbed sports bra and high‑waist shorts stands mid‑step on a tiled floor, hands lightly on her thighs, near a small wooden side table with lit candles.
