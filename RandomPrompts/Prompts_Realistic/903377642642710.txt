**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

- **Subject & Pose**  
  A young woman with shoulder‑length brown hair, wearing a floral‑print bikini top and matching bottoms, holding a light‑blue denim jacket over her shoulders. She stands in a bright, sunlit bedroom, facing the camera with a relaxed, candid expression. Her left hand grips the jacket’s lapel, right hand slightly raised, creating a natural mid‑action pose.

- **Lighting & Exposure**  
  Natural daylight from a large window behind her creates a soft, slightly overexposed highlight on her face and torso. The exposure is uneven—her front is brighter than the background—giving a raw, unprocessed look. No auto‑beauty or filter enhancement; the image retains slight grain and subtle color warmth.

- **Camera Settings**  
  - Lens: 24 mm wide‑angle, short focal length, handheld.  
  - Focal depth: everything from her face to the back wall is fully sharp—no bokeh.  
  - Shutter speed: 1/60 s, producing a faint motion blur on her hands and jacket as she adjusts it.  
  - ISO: 400–600 for natural noise.

- **Composition & Framing**  
  The shot is intentionally off‑center; the subject’s left side leans slightly to the right of the frame’s center. The composition is spontaneous—no rule‑of‑thirds, just a casual, candid capture. The background shows a beige curtain, a small potted plant on a side table, and a wooden dresser, all in focus.

- **Environment**  
  Location: A bright, modern bedroom with light wood furniture, a large window letting in morning sunlight, and a visible green plant in the corner. Time of day: early afternoon, around 10 am, when the sun is high but not directly overhead. Weather: clear, slightly humid air.

- **Details & Imperfections**  
  Slight tilt to the camera, giving a natural, unposed feel. The jacket’s seams are partially out of alignment, adding realism. Her nails are painted red, visible as she holds the jacket. A small green pendant hangs from her necklace, catching the light. The image has no post‑processing color grading—just raw, in‑camera aesthetics.

- **Final Prompt**  
  “A young woman with shoulder‑length brown hair, wearing a floral‑print bikini top and matching bottoms, holding a light‑blue denim jacket over her shoulders, standing in a bright, sunlit bedroom. Natural daylight from a large window creates a soft, slightly overexposed highlight on her face and torso. Camera: 24 mm wide‑angle, handheld, 1/60 s shutter speed, ISO 500.
