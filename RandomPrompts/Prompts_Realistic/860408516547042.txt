**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

- **Subject & Pose**  
  A young woman with long, dark hair, wearing a shimmering gold, low‑cut gown adorned with red gemstones and gold jewelry (headpiece, necklace, bracelets). She holds a golden staff in her right hand, left hand raised to her head, as if adjusting her hair. Her expression is bright, mid‑smile, slightly turned toward the camera, capturing a candid, spontaneous moment.

- **Motion & Focus**  
  Slight motion blur on the subject’s hands and the staff, indicating handheld shake. Everything in frame is fully sharp—no bokeh, no portrait‑mode isolation. The image has a raw, unprocessed look: no filters, no color grading, no post‑processing aesthetic. A hint of overexposure in the upper left corner to emphasize ambient light.

- **Lens & Technical**  
  Wide‑angle lens (24 mm), short focal length. No depth‑of‑field blur; background is fully sharp. Slight tilt and off‑center framing—no rule‑of‑thirds intentionality. The composition feels accidental, as if the photographer was mid‑action.

- **Environment**  
  Location: a dimly lit indoor loft with exposed brick walls and a concrete floor. A small window on the left lets in natural daylight, creating soft shadows. Time of day: late afternoon, around 5 pm. Weather: mild, slightly humid. A faint smoky haze lingers in the air, adding texture.

- **Lighting**  
  Zero studio lighting; all light comes from the window and ambient interior lights. Slight overexposure in the upper left corner gives a warm glow. The lighting is natural, with soft contrast.

- **Post‑Processing**  
  Auto‑HDR or auto‑beauty mode off. No filters, no color grading, no post‑processing aesthetic—just raw, candid imagery.

- **Final Prompt**  
  “A young woman in a gold gown holding a golden staff, captured with a wide‑angle lens at a dimly lit loft during late afternoon. Slight motion blur on her hands, fully sharp background, accidental off‑center framing, natural lighting from a window, raw unprocessed look.”
