**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

- **Subject & Pose**  
  A tall, athletic woman with a rose tattoo on her left upper arm, wearing black fish‑net stockings and black fingerless gloves. She holds a wine glass in her right hand, mid‑sip, body slightly turned toward the camera, hips angled. Her hair is long, brown, and loosely brushed, falling over her shoulders.  

- **Lighting & Exposure**  
  Natural daylight from a western window, mid‑afternoon (16:30–17:00). Slight overexposure on the upper body, giving a warm, soft glow; minor vignette at the edges. No auto‑HDR or beauty filters—raw, unprocessed look.  

- **Camera & Lens**  
  DSLR or mirrorless with a 24‑mm wide‑angle lens (f/3.5, 1/60 s shutter speed). Everything in focus; background fully sharp, no bokeh. Slight handheld shake producing a subtle motion blur on the wine glass and the woman’s hand.  

- **Composition**  
  Candid, unposed, spontaneous framing—subject is mid‑action, slightly off‑center. The shot is not intentionally rule‑of‑thirds; the composition feels accidental, with a slight tilt to the left. The frame cuts off the lower left corner, cropping the woman’s left thigh slightly.  

- **Environment**  
  Location: A rustic wooden table in a sunlit loft, with a plain beige wall behind. The time of day is late afternoon, creating warm, directional light from the west. Ambient humidity is moderate; a faint breeze is visible in the subject’s hair.  

- **Details & Imperfections**  
  Slight tilt and partial crop on the lower right side; the woman’s left glove shows a tiny smudge. The wine glass has a slight reflection of the window. The tattoo’s edges are crisp, with a hint of natural shading.  

- **Post‑Processing**  
  Zero color grading, zero filters—only raw capture. A minimal crop is applied after capture to tighten the frame.
