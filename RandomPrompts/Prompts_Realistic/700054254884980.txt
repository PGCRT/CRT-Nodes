**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

- **Subject & Action**  
  A young woman with long, wavy brown hair sits on a white quilted bed, mid‑adjustment of a light‑colored floral shirt. Her hands are pulling the front of the shirt open, exposing her midriff and black thong underwear. She looks downward, eyes focused on the fabric, giving a candid, unposed feel.

- **Clothing & Accessories**  
  Shirt: off‑white with delicate pink‑red floral motifs, long sleeves rolled up at the cuffs, front buttons partially undone.  
  Underwear: thin black thong.  
  Background: a light‑wood nightstand with a beige lamp and a small black remote on top; a beige pillow rests against the headboard.

- **Lighting & Exposure**  
  Natural daylight from a window to the left, slight overexposure in the upper right corner. No auto‑HDR or beauty filters—raw, unprocessed look. Slight handheld shake gives a natural motion blur.

- **Lens & Technical Settings**  
  24‑mm wide‑angle lens, f/4.0 aperture, 1/125 s shutter speed, ISO 400. Everything in focus—no bokeh, no depth‑of‑field blur. Slight tilt and off‑center framing; partial crop of the bed’s edge.

- **Environment**  
  Location: a cozy, modern bedroom. Time of day: late morning, soft natural light. Ambient conditions: gentle warmth, slight dust motes in the air.

- **Composition Notes**  
  No rule‑of‑thirds intentionality—accidental framing with the subject’s left side slightly cut off. Background fully sharp, no portrait‑mode isolation. The shot feels spontaneous, as if captured mid‑movement.

- **Post‑Processing**  
  Zero color grading, zero filters. Slight natural contrast and a hint of warmth to keep the image realistic.
