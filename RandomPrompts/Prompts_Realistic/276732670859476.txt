**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

  - Focus: everything in focus – background and foreground equally sharp; no portrait‑mode depth‑of‑field blur.

  Candid, unposed, spontaneous framing. The shot is slightly tilted to the right, off‑center, with a partial crop that cuts off the top left corner of the bookshelf. No rule‑of‑thirds intentionality – it feels accidental, like a quick snapshot.

  Office setting: a light‑wood desk with a black office chair behind the woman. A tall wooden bookshelf stands to the left, filled with colorful spines (red, blue, green, yellow).   Slight motion blur on the woman’s left hand, indicating handheld instability. The image has a natural, raw aesthetic – no color grading or filters. A faint overexposure in the upper left corner softens the bookshelf spines. The background is fully sharp, no bokeh.
