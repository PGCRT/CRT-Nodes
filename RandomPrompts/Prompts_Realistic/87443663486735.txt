**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

- **Subject & Pose**  
  A young woman with long, wavy blonde hair standing mid‑stride on a city street. She wears a white long‑sleeved crop top and frayed denim shorts, arms crossed over her chest, head tilted slightly to the left, eyes focused forward. Her necklace has a small pendant that catches the light. The pose is casual, as if she’s just finished a conversation or is about to grab a coffee—no staged pose, just a candid moment.

- **Lighting & Exposure**  
  Natural daylight from a high‑angle, midday sun; slight overexposure on the upper body to keep the background bright but not washed out. No auto‑beauty filters—raw, unprocessed look. Slight handheld shake gives a natural motion blur around the edges.

- **Lens & Technical Settings**  
  24‑mm wide‑angle lens (short focal length), f/4.0 aperture, 1/125 s shutter speed, ISO 400. Everything in focus—no portrait‑mode depth‑of‑field blur; the background street and buildings are fully sharp. Slight tilt to the right for an off‑center, accidental framing.

- **Environment**  
  A busy downtown avenue with brick‑faced buildings on both sides, a traffic light glowing red in the distance, a silver sedan parked along the curb, and a few pedestrians blurred in the background. The street is paved with gray asphalt; a white lane marker runs along the right side. The time of day is early afternoon, with warm, slightly harsh sunlight casting soft shadows.

- **Composition & Framing**  
  No rule‑of‑thirds intentionality—frame the subject slightly off‑center, with the left side of her body cut off by the edge of the frame. The composition feels spontaneous, as if captured with a handheld camera in motion. Background elements (buildings, traffic light, cars) are all in focus, giving a sense of depth without any “portrait mode” isolation.

- **Post‑Processing**  
  Zero filters, zero color grading, no post‑processing aesthetic. Only raw, natural details—slight overexposure on the subject’s chest and shoulders to emphasize the white top, slight underexposure on the street to keep contrast balanced.

- **Final Prompt Summary**  
  “A candid, wide‑angle shot of a blonde woman in a white crop top and denim shorts standing on a downtown city street, captured with a 24‑mm lens at f/4.0 and 1/125 s, natural daylight with slight overexposure, everything in focus, background fully sharp, off‑center framing, spontaneous composition, no filters or post‑processing.”
