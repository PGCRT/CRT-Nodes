**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

- **Subject & Pose**  
  A young woman with wet, tousled hair sitting on a unmade bed in a bright, lived‑in bedroom. She’s wearing a black “SATISFACTION” crop‑tshirt tied at the front and matching black bikini bottoms. Her hands are pulling the shirt slightly tighter around her mid‑riff as she adjusts her outfit—mid‑action, candid, unposed.

- **Capture Style**  
  Candid, spontaneous, “in‑the‑moment.” The shot is taken while she’s still adjusting her clothes, so there’s a subtle motion blur on her hands and the shirt. Slight tilt and off‑center framing; the composition is accidental, not intentional. No rule‑of‑thirds—just a natural, handheld frame that captures the whole scene in one go.

- **Auto‑HDR / Auto‑Beauty**  
  Turn off auto‑HDR and auto‑beauty. Keep the look raw and unprocessed. No filters, no color grading, no post‑processing aesthetic. The exposure is slightly over‑exposed on the window light, giving a warm, natural glow.

- **Environment**  
  Specific real‑world location: a bright bedroom with a white radiator on the left wall, a large window letting in daylight, and a pile of clothes on the bed’s right side. Time of day: late morning, soft sunlight through the window, slight haze from the ambient light. Weather: clear, sunny.

- **Lens & Technical**  
  Short focal length (≈20‑35 mm), wide‑angle lens. No bokeh—everything is fully sharp, from foreground to background. No depth‑of‑field blur; no portrait‑mode isolation. The background (window, radiator, bed) stays in focus.

- **Additional Details**  
  - Slight overexposure on the window, creating a subtle halo around the subject’s head.  
  - The shirt’s text “SATISFACTION” is visible and slightly distorted by the motion blur.  
  - A few stray water droplets on her hair add realism.  
  - The bed’s white sheets are rumpled, with a pink garment and a white ball on the right side.  
  - The radiator is white, rectangular, and sits beneath the window.  
  - The window’s blinds are partially open, letting in natural daylight.
