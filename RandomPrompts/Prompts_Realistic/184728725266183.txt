**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

- **Subject & Pose**  
  A tall, slender woman with long, dark hair that blows lightly in the wind, standing bare‑footed in a sunlit kitchen. She’s wrapped in a soft, beige towel tied at the waist, her torso exposed and her hips slightly tilted as she reaches for a dish on the counter. Her head is bent downward, eyes focused on the surface, giving a candid, mid‑action feel.

- **Lighting & Exposure**  
  Natural daylight from a large window behind her creates a slight overexposure on her skin, with a warm, golden hue. The exposure is uneven—brighter at the top of her body, softer at the bottom—producing a raw, unprocessed look. No auto‑HDR or beauty filters; just a single shutter snap.

- **Camera Settings**  
  - Lens: 24 mm wide‑angle, short focal length, handheld.  
  - Focal depth: everything from her feet to the top of her head is fully sharp—no bokeh, no portrait‑mode isolation.  
  - Shutter speed: 1/60 s, giving a subtle motion blur on her hair and hands as she moves.  
  - ISO: 400–600 for natural grain.

- **Composition**  
  The frame is intentionally off‑center; the left side of the image is slightly cut off by a wooden countertop edge. The woman’s body occupies roughly two‑thirds of the vertical space, with her legs extending close to the bottom edge. The background shows a large window with sheer curtains that drapes softly, a white radiator beneath, and a glimpse of greenery outside—a real‑world, everyday setting.

- **Environment**  
  Location: A bright, modern kitchen in a suburban house. Time of day: late morning, when the sun is high but still warm. Ambient conditions: light breeze, slight dust motes dancing in the air. The floor is tiled, the walls are pale, and a small wooden table corner peeks into the lower left corner.

- **Technical Details**  
  - No rule‑of‑thirds intentionality—framing is accidental, as if captured by a photographer on the go.  
  - Slight tilt of the camera gives a dynamic angle.  
  - All elements in focus: her face, body, and the background are crisp.  
  - No post‑processing color grading; just raw JPEG output.

- **Prompt for Generation**  
  “A tall woman with long dark hair standing barefoot in a sunlit kitchen, wrapped in a beige towel tied at her waist, reaching for a dish on the counter.
