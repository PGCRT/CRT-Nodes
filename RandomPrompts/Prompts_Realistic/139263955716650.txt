**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

- **Subject & Pose**  
  A young woman with long, wavy brown hair, wearing large hoop earrings and a delicate silver necklace, leaning her chin and hands on a fresh green palm leaf. Her eyes are gently closed, lips slightly parted, as if she’s mid‑breath or about to speak. Her hands are stacked, one over the other, with a thin ring on her left ring finger visible. She’s in a relaxed, casual stance, body angled toward the camera but not perfectly centered.

- **Capture Style**  
  Candid, unposed, spontaneous — the subject appears unaware of the camera or is caught mid‑action. Slight tilt and off‑center framing; the composition is accidental, no rule‑of‑thirds intentionality. The shot has a raw, unprocessed look: no filters, no color grading, no post‑processing aesthetic. A hint of slight overexposure in the background to emphasize ambient light.

- **Motion & Focus**  
  Slight motion blur from handheld instability, natural freeze or soft blur on the subject’s hands and face. Everything is fully sharp — no portrait‑mode depth‑of‑field blur; background remains crisp.

- **Lens & Technical**  
  Short focal length (≈20–35 mm), wide‑angle lens. No bokeh. All elements in focus: subject, palm leaf, background. Auto‑HDR or auto‑beauty mode off. Wide‑angle captures the entire scene without cropping out context.

- **Environment**  
  Specific real‑world location: a sunny beach at late afternoon, with the ocean visible in the background and a few palm fronds swaying. Time of day: golden hour, warm ambient light. Weather: lightly breezy, clear sky, faint clouds. The background is fully sharp, no studio lighting.

- **Additional Details**  
  The woman’s skin has natural texture, slight imperfections, no retouching. Her nails are painted a soft pink, her makeup natural. She wears a thin-strapped top that shows a hint of a delicate chain necklace. A small diamond or crystal is visible on her ring finger. The palm leaf she rests her hands on has a few small white specks (maybe sea salt). The background shows a blurred horizon line of the ocean and a faint silhouette of a distant building or tree.

- **Prompt Summary**  
  “A candid, wide‑angle shot of a young woman with long wavy brown hair, hoop earrings, silver necklace, and a thin ring on her left hand, leaning on a green palm leaf at a beach during golden hour. Her eyes are closed, lips slightly parted, hands stacked. Slight motion blur from handheld shake, everything in focus, no filters or auto‑HDR, raw look, accidental off‑center framing, background fully sharp.”
