**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

- **Subject & Pose**  
  A young woman with dark, slightly wet hair, mid‑stretch, arms raised above her head, hands clasped together. She’s wearing a black mesh sports bra and high‑waisted leggings with an abstract pink‑gray pattern. Her face is turned toward the camera with a subtle smile, eyes bright, eyebrows relaxed. The pose captures a natural “mid‑action” moment—she’s not posing for the photo but rather in the middle of a gentle stretch or dance move.

- **Lighting & Exposure**  
  Ambient daylight from a large window, slightly overexposed on the right side to give a warm, soft glow. No auto‑beauty or filter processing; raw, unedited look. The exposure is a little bright at the top, creating a slight halo around her head and shoulders.

- **Camera Settings**  
  - Lens: 24‑mm wide‑angle (short focal length), handheld.  
  - Focal depth: everything in focus—no bokeh, no portrait‑mode isolation.  
  - Shutter speed: 1/60 s to allow a faint motion blur on the arms and hair.  
  - ISO: 400–600 for natural grain.  
  - No “rule of thirds” intention—framing is accidental, slightly off‑center, with a slight tilt to the left.

- **Composition & Framing**  
  The shot is cropped tightly around the subject’s torso to mid‑thigh, leaving a small margin of the tufted beige sofa behind her. The background is a plain, light‑colored wall with a single white switch plate visible on the upper left. The sofa’s tufts and the woman’s legs create a subtle diagonal line that leads the eye downward.

- **Environment**  
  Location: A bright living room in a modern apartment. Time of day: late morning, sun high but not directly overhead. Ambient conditions: mild temperature, slight humidity; the air is still, but the subject’s motion leaves a gentle blur.

- **Technical Details**  
  - Wide‑angle lens gives a slightly distorted perspective that enhances the “candid” feel.  
  - No depth‑of‑field blur—everything from foreground to background stays sharp.  
  - Slight overexposure on the upper part of the frame gives a warm, natural tone.  
  - Handheld shake is visible in the arms’ motion, adding realism.

- **Prompt for Generation**  
  “A young woman with dark, slightly wet hair stretching mid‑pose on a tufted beige sofa in a bright living room, wearing a black mesh sports bra and patterned leggings; captured with a 24‑mm wide‑angle lens at 1/60 s, handheld, slight motion blur on the arms, accidental off‑center framing, soft daylight overexposure on the right, everything in focus, raw unprocessed look.”

Provide only the structured prompt.
