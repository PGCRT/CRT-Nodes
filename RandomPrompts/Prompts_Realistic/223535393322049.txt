**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

- **Subject & Pose**  
  A woman with voluminous, curly dark hair standing in a modern kitchen, captured mid‑turn as she adjusts her black bikini top. Her back is slightly turned toward the camera, one hand on her neck, creating a natural, unposed candid feel. The shot should show subtle motion blur at the edges to suggest handheld shake, while keeping the entire frame sharply in focus—no portrait‑mode softness.

- **Clothing & Appearance**  
  She wears a thin‑strap black bikini bottom and a matching top with delicate straps. Her skin has a natural, unretouched texture; slight imperfections (like a small mole or a stray hair) are welcome. The lighting should be slightly overexposed on the upper body, giving a warm, ambient glow that mimics mid‑afternoon sunlight.

- **Environment**  
  The setting is a bright, contemporary kitchen with emerald‑green cabinetry, white subway tiles, and a marble countertop. A stainless‑steel faucet and a small plant in the corner add detail. The time of day is late afternoon; the windows let in soft, golden light that creates subtle highlights on her back and the countertop. Weather is clear, with a gentle breeze that slightly ruffles her hair.

- **Lens & Technical**  
  Use a wide‑angle lens (24–35 mm) to capture a broad view of the scene. Keep everything in focus—no bokeh or depth‑of‑field blur. The composition should be accidental: slight tilt and off‑center framing, no rule‑of‑thirds intentionality. Capture a full‑body shot that includes the subject’s legs, hips, and the kitchen counter, with a partial crop that cuts off a bit of the left side to enhance spontaneity.

- **Lighting & Post‑Processing**  
  No auto‑HDR or beauty filters—just raw, natural lighting. Slight overexposure on the upper body to emphasize the ambient light. Keep the image unprocessed; no color grading or vignetting. The final look should feel like a genuine, spontaneous snapshot taken with a DSLR or mirrorless camera in a real‑world setting.

- **Final Prompt**  
  “A woman with voluminous curly dark hair, mid‑turn adjusting her black bikini top, standing in a bright modern kitchen with emerald cabinets and white subway tiles. Capture the scene with a wide‑angle lens (24–35 mm), keeping everything sharp while allowing slight motion blur at the edges.
