**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

- **Subject & Pose**  
  A young woman with long, wavy dark hair, wearing a delicate lace bra in a soft beige tone.   Warm, golden‑hour sunlight from the west creates a slight overexposure on the right side of the frame, giving an auto‑HDR feel without any post‑processing filters. The lighting is natural, with soft ambient light filling the scene; no studio lights or color grading applied.

  The shot is intentionally off‑center, with the subject’s left side slightly cut off by the frame edge. No rule‑of‑thirds intentionality; the framing feels spontaneous and candid. The background is a real‑world park or meadow at sunset, with trees and foliage fully sharp, no bokeh.

- **Environment**  
  Location: a sunlit meadow near a wooden bench, in an urban‑suburban setting. Time of day: late afternoon (golden hour). Weather: clear, mild breeze, slight humidity that adds a soft glow to the scene.

- **Details & Imperfections**  
  Slight tilt of the camera, partial crop on the right side, natural motion blur around the hair and shoulders, minor dust particles in the air, a faint reflection of the sun on the woman’s chest. The image has a raw, unprocessed look—no filters or color grading, just an auto‑beauty mode off, genuine candid moment.

- **Final Prompt**  
  “A young woman with long wavy dark hair, wearing a delicate lace beige bra, captured mid‑step in a golden‑hour meadow at sunset.
