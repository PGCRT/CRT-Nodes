**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

- **Subject & Pose**  
  A tall, athletic woman with long, wavy brown hair, wearing a black triangle‑style bikini, standing on a sunlit beach. She is mid‑gesture: left hand touching the back of her head, right hand on hip, body slightly turned toward the camera, legs apart for a dynamic stance. Her left thigh shows two star‑shaped tattoos (one with a central dot).  

- **Lighting & Exposure**  
  Natural daylight from a high‑angle, late‑afternoon sun; slight overexposure on the upper body to keep the scene bright and airy. No auto‑HDR or beauty filters—raw, unprocessed look.  

- **Camera Settings**  
  Wide‑angle lens (24–35 mm), handheld, slightly shaky. Focal length short, background fully sharp (no bokeh). Aperture f/5.6, shutter speed 1/125 s, ISO 400.  

- **Composition**  
  Candid, unposed, spontaneous framing—slight tilt and off‑center crop. No rule‑of‑thirds intentionality; the composition feels accidental, as if captured mid‑action.  

- **Environment**  
  Specific real‑world location: a sandy beach with low‑lying dunes and sparse green vegetation in the background. Time of day: late afternoon (golden hour), weather clear with a soft haze.  

- **Technical Details**  
  Short focal length, wide angle; everything in focus—no portrait mode or subject isolation. Slight motion blur from handheld instability on the subject’s hands and hair.  

- **Post‑Processing**  
  Zero filters, zero color grading, no post‑processing aesthetic. Only natural contrast and subtle vibrancy.
