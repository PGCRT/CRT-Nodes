**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

- **Subject & Pose**  
  A young woman with long, wavy brown hair that catches the sunlight, wearing a simple black strap‑top. She is mid‑smile, head turned slightly to the side, as if she just finished a conversation or is about to start one. Her left ear is partially visible, and a subtle earring glints in the light. The framing is off‑center: her right shoulder cuts off the left edge of the frame, giving a casual, spontaneous feel.

- **Capture Style**  
  Candid, unposed, spontaneous — the subject appears unaware of the camera, caught in a natural moment. Slight tilt and partial crop create an accidental composition. No rule‑of‑thirds intentionality; the frame is handheld, with a tiny shake that gives a subtle motion blur around the edges. The image has a raw, unprocessed look: no filters, no color grading, no post‑processing aesthetic. A slight overexposure on the right side lets the ambient light bleed through the hair, while the left side remains slightly underexposed for contrast.

- **Environment**  
  Location: A sunlit meadow of white roses, with a gentle breeze ruffling the petals. The time of day is late afternoon, around golden hour, so the light is warm and soft. Weather is clear but not perfect—light humidity gives a faint haze that keeps everything in focus. The background is fully sharp, no bokeh or depth‑of‑field blur; the entire scene is in focus from foreground to background.

- **Lens & Technical**  
  Short focal length (24 mm) wide‑angle lens. No portrait‑mode subject isolation; the background remains fully sharp. The lens is handheld, producing a slight motion blur that follows the subject’s movement. All elements—subject, hair, clothing, flowers, and foliage—are in focus. Auto‑HDR or auto‑beauty mode is off. The exposure is set to capture the full dynamic range of the scene, with a slight overexposure on the right side and a subtle underexposure on the left, depending on ambient light.

- **Final Prompt**  
  “A candid, unposed portrait of a young woman with long wavy brown hair and a black strap‑top, captured in a late‑afternoon sunlit meadow of white roses. The image is framed off‑center with slight tilt and partial crop, handheld shake giving subtle motion blur. All elements are fully sharp, no filters or post‑processing, raw look. The subject is mid‑smile, slightly turned, as if caught in a spontaneous moment. The background is clear, no bokeh, wide‑angle short focal length lens, slight overexposure on the right side, underexposed left side, all in focus.”
