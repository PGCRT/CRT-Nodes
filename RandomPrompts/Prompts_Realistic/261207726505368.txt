**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

  A young woman with long, wavy brown hair, wearing a colorful off‑shoulder striped romper and white knee‑high boots, sitting mid‑pose on stone steps. - **Lighting & Exposure**  
  Slight overexposure on the bright midday sun, creating a warm, natural glow. Wide‑angle lens (24–35 mm) captures a broad view of the scene; no bokeh, background fully sharp.

- **Environment**  
  Outdoor stone steps in front of a light‑colored building with a wooden door and a terracotta planter on a railing behind her. A small potted plant sits on the railing, adding greenery.   No rule‑of‑thirds intentionality—accidental framing with a slight tilt and off‑center crop. The shot is candid, unposed, spontaneous; the subject appears unaware of the camera’s presence.

  Short focal length (24–35 mm), wide‑angle, no bokeh, everything in focus. Handheld instability gives a natural motion blur. No studio lighting, no professional composition, no editorial tone.
