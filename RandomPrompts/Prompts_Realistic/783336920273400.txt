**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

- **Subject & Pose**  
  A young woman with long, dark brown hair walking casually across a sunlit lawn. She wears a light‑blue denim jacket over a soft beige top and matching high‑waist jeans. Her left foot is mid‑step, heel slightly lifted, suggesting motion. Her head is turned to the right, a gentle smile on her face, as if she’s mid‑conversation or just noticing the camera.

- **Motion & Focus**  
  Slight motion blur at the feet and hands to convey movement. The entire scene is fully sharp—no bokeh, no depth‑of‑field blur—captured with a wide‑angle lens (24–35 mm). Handheld instability gives a subtle tilt and off‑center framing; the composition is accidental, not rule‑of‑thirds.

- **Lighting & Exposure**  
  Natural daylight, mid‑afternoon, with the sun high but slightly to the left, creating soft shadows on the grass. Slight overexposure in the highlights gives a raw, unprocessed look. No auto‑HDR or auto‑beauty mode—just a straightforward exposure.

- **Environment**  
  A spacious, well‑maintained park or suburban lawn. Tall evergreen trees line the left side, a deciduous tree on the right, and a few houses visible in the far background. The grass shows a pattern of mowed stripes. A few fallen leaves lie scattered near the woman’s feet. The sky is clear, pale blue with a faint haze.

- **Lens & Technical**  
  Short focal length (30 mm), wide‑angle, no bokeh. Everything from foreground to background is in focus. Shot on a DSLR or mirrorless with a prime or zoom lens set to a wide aperture (f/4–f/5.6) but with the entire frame sharp. No portrait‑mode subject isolation.

- **Capture Style**  
  Candid, unposed, spontaneous—subject unaware or mid‑action. Imperfections encouraged: slight tilt, off‑center framing, partial crop. Zero studio lighting, zero professional composition, zero editorial tone. Natural motion freeze or slight blur from handheld instability.

- **Post‑Processing**  
  No filters, no color grading, no post‑processing aesthetic. Raw, unprocessed look with only basic white‑balance correction and a slight exposure tweak.
