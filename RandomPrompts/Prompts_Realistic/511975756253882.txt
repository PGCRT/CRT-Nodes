**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

- **Subject & Pose**  
  A young woman with long, sun‑kissed blonde hair tied into high pigtails, wearing oversized pink hoop earrings and a thin gold belly ring. She is seated in a shallow, crystal‑clear swimming pool, knees bent, hands resting on the water’s surface, body slightly tilted to one side as if caught mid‑movement. Her gaze is direct, almost candid, with a subtle smirk that suggests she’s unaware of the camera’s presence.

- **Lighting & Exposure**  
  Natural daylight from a high‑angle, late‑afternoon sun creates a soft overexposure on the water’s surface, with slight flare and a warm, golden hue. The exposure is slightly uneven—brighter at the top of the frame, giving an auto‑HDR feel without any post‑processing filters.

- **Lens & Technical Settings**  
  24 mm wide‑angle lens, f/3.5 aperture, 1/60 s shutter speed, handheld shot with slight motion blur from the camera’s instability. Everything in the frame—from the subject’s face to the pool’s edge—is fully sharp; no bokeh or depth‑of‑field blur. The composition is accidental: the subject is off‑center, the framing is not rule‑of‑thirds, and the crop is partial, giving a spontaneous, candid feel.

- **Environment**  
  A tropical resort pool at sunset, with palm fronds and a stone ledge visible in the background. The water ripples around her thighs, reflecting the sky’s warm tones. The time of day is late afternoon, creating long shadows and a golden‑hour glow. Weather is clear, with a light breeze creating gentle waves on the pool surface.

- **Details & Imperfections**  
  Slight tilt of the camera, a partial crop that cuts off the lower edge of the pool, and a faint motion blur around the hands. The subject’s skin has a natural, raw texture—no auto‑beauty mode applied. A small, subtle shine on her chest and abdomen from the water’s reflection adds realism.

- **Prompt for Text‑to‑Image Generation**  
  “A young woman with long sun‑kissed blonde hair in high pigtails, wearing oversized pink hoop earrings and a thin gold belly ring, seated in a shallow crystal‑clear swimming pool at late afternoon sunset. She is caught mid‑movement, hands resting on the water’s surface, body slightly tilted, direct gaze with a subtle smirk. Natural daylight creates soft overexposure on the water, slight flare and warm golden hue. 24 mm wide‑angle lens, f/3.5 aperture, 1/60 s shutter speed, handheld shot with slight motion blur; everything in focus, no bokeh. Background shows palm fronds and a stone ledge, water ripples around her thighs reflecting the sky. Slight tilt, partial crop, accidental framing, raw unprocessed look, no filters.”
