**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

- **Subject & Pose**  
  A young woman with long, wavy dark hair, wearing a black lace bodysuit with a deep V‑neck and a jeweled choker, standing in a grand hall. She is mid‑smile, hands clasped in front of her, slightly tilted to the left, as if caught in an unplanned moment. Her earrings dangle, catching the light.

- **Lighting & Exposure**  
  Warm, ambient indoor lighting from a candelabra and a chandelier; slight overexposure on the upper half of the frame, giving a soft glow. No auto‑HDR or beauty filters—raw, unprocessed look. The exposure is slightly uneven, with the lower part of the image a bit darker than the top.

- **Camera Settings**  
  Wide‑angle lens (24 mm), short focal length, handheld. Slight motion blur from the camera’s instability, natural freeze of the subject’s movement. Everything in focus—no bokeh, no depth‑of‑field blur; background fully sharp.

- **Environment**  
  A richly decorated hall with deep red drapes, gold‑tipped curtains, and a tall candelabra on a marble pedestal. The time of day is late afternoon, with natural daylight mixing with interior warm lights. Weather is calm, no wind, ambient quiet.

- **Composition & Framing**  
  No rule‑of‑thirds intentionality—accidental framing that places the subject slightly off‑center. The crop is partial; the top of the candelabra cuts off at the upper edge, and the lower part of her dress extends beyond the bottom edge. Slight tilt to the left gives a candid feel.

- **Details & Imperfections**  
  Slight motion blur on the subject’s hands, a tiny smear on the candelabra’s flame. The choker’s gemstones have a slight overexposed highlight. The background drapes show subtle texture and a faint reflection of the candelabra’s light.

- **Post‑Processing**  
  Zero studio lighting, zero professional composition, zero editorial tone. Raw, unprocessed look with only minimal white‑balance adjustment. No color grading or filters—just natural tones.

**Prompt Summary**  
“Candid shot of a woman in a black lace bodysuit and jeweled choker, standing in a red‑draped hall with a candelabra, captured on a 24 mm wide‑angle lens with slight motion blur and overexposure, handheld, raw, unprocessed, everything in focus, background sharp, accidental off‑center framing, late afternoon natural light mixed with interior warm lights.”
