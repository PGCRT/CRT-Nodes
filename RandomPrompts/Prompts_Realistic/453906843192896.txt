**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

- **Subject & Appearance**  
  - A young woman with a short, layered brown bob and bangs, wearing a black cat‑ear headband, gold hoop earrings, a black choker with a double‑loop clasp, and long purple leather gloves that extend to the mid‑forearm.  
  - Her nails are painted black; she is lying on a plush white bed, hands clasped together in front of her chest, looking directly at the camera with a soft, engaging smile.  

- **Pose & Motion**  
  - Slight motion blur on the hands and fingers, indicating a handheld shot captured mid‑movement.  
  - The body is slightly tilted to the left, creating an off‑center, spontaneous composition.  

- **Lighting & Exposure**  
  - Natural daylight coming from a window on the left; slight overexposure in the upper right corner, giving a warm, golden hue.  
  - No auto‑beauty or filter processing – raw, unprocessed look.  

- **Environment**  
  - A cozy bedroom with a white quilted bedspread, a fluffy pillow behind the subject, and a small Christmas tree visible in the background on the right.  
  - Time of day: late afternoon, soft sunlight creating gentle shadows.  
  - Ambient sound: faint rustle of the quilt, the click of a camera shutter.  

- **Lens & Technical**  
  - 24‑mm wide‑angle lens, short focal length, everything in focus from foreground to background.  
  - No bokeh; background remains sharp, no portrait‑mode depth‑of‑field blur.  
  - Slight handheld shake imparting a natural, imperfect feel.  

- **Composition Notes**  
  - No rule‑of‑thirds intentionality – the frame is accidental, with the subject’s head near the upper third but slightly off‑center.  
  - The shot is a “realistic candid” capture: spontaneous, unposed, and raw.
