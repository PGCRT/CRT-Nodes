**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

- **Subject & Pose**  
  A young woman with long, dark brown hair, wearing a light beige button‑down shirt and blue jeans, standing on a city sidewalk. Her left hand is lightly raised near her neck, fingers slightly curled, as if she’s about to touch her hair or adjust her necklace. She wears a delicate silver cross necklace that catches the light. Her head is turned to the right, eyes focused off‑camera, mouth slightly open in a casual smile.  

- **Lighting & Exposure**  
  Natural daylight from a low‑angle sunset; slight overexposure on the upper part of the frame gives a warm, golden hue. No auto‑beauty or filter enhancement—raw, unprocessed look. The exposure is a little bright at the top, creating a subtle halo around her head.  

- **Motion & Focus**  
  Slight motion blur on the subject’s hand and background, indicating handheld instability. Everything remains fully sharp—no portrait‑mode depth of field blur.  

- **Lens & Technical Settings**  
  Wide‑angle lens (24‑35 mm), short focal length. No bokeh; background is completely in focus.  

- **Environment**  
  A tree‑lined street with a concrete sidewalk, a parked car and a traffic light in the background. The time of day is late afternoon, around dusk, with warm ambient light. Leaves are scattered on the grass beside the sidewalk.  

- **Composition Notes**  
  Candid, unposed, spontaneous—subject appears unaware of the camera. Slight tilt and off‑center framing; partial crop that cuts off the left side of her shirt. No rule‑of‑thirds intentionality.
