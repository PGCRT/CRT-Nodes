  Natural daylight from a high‑angle, late‑afternoon sun creates a soft overexposure on the subject’s face and upper body, with a slight warm tint. - **Motion & Focus**  
  A natural freeze of motion with a subtle blur around the edges from a handheld camera, suggesting slight instability. Everything—from the woman’s face to the cargo pockets—remains fully sharp, with no portrait‑mode depth‑of‑field blur. - **Lens & Technical**  
  24 mm wide‑angle lens, short focal length, no bokeh. The background is fully sharp, with no depth‑of‑field isolation. The lens is set to a wide aperture but the entire scene stays in focus.

  Location: A concrete alleyway in an urban loft, with a rough, textured wall and a small window that lets in the afternoon light. Time of day: late afternoon (around 5 pm), with warm, golden hues. Weather: mild, slightly breezy, with a faint haze that adds ambient softness.

  The shot is a “real‑life” candid capture—no rule‑of‑thirds intentionality. The woman’s jacket has a subtle sheen from the leather, and her cargo pants show a slight wrinkle at the thigh, enhancing realism.

- **Post‑Processing**  
  Zero filters, zero color grading, zero post‑processing aesthetic. Only raw, in‑camera data is preserved, with a slight overexposure on the subject’s upper body to capture the ambient light’s warmth.
