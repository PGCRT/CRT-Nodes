**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

- **Subject & Action**  
  A teenage girl with long, dark hair tied in a loose ponytail, wearing an off‑shoulder white cotton tee and dark denim jeans, standing mid‑turn in a sunlit classroom. Her left hand rests on her hip, right arm bent, head tilted downward as if she’s about to pick up a pencil or adjust her glasses. She’s wearing black Nike sneakers with white soles.  

- **Pose & Motion**  
  Slight motion blur at the knees and feet to suggest movement, captured with a handheld camera at a slightly low angle. The blur is subtle—just enough to give a natural, spontaneous feel.  

- **Lighting & Exposure**  
  Natural daylight from a large window on the left, creating soft highlights on her shirt and a faint overexposure on the ceiling lights. Slight warm tint from the ambient sunlight; no color grading or filters applied.  

- **Environment**  
  A typical high‑school classroom: a green chalkboard with “Revue” written in white chalk, a small potted plant on a shelf, a wooden desk, and a metal chair. The floor is a light tile, the walls are off‑white, and a window with a sheer curtain lets in bright light.  

- **Time of Day & Weather**  
  Late morning, around 10:30 AM, with the sun high but not too direct; the sky outside the window is clear, giving a bright, warm atmosphere.  

- **Lens & Technical Settings**  
  24‑mm wide‑angle lens (short focal length), f/4.5 aperture, 1/125 s shutter speed, ISO 400. Everything in focus from foreground to background; no bokeh or depth‑of‑field blur.  

- **Composition & Framing**  
  No rule‑of‑thirds intentionality—framing is accidental and slightly off‑center. The shot is a wide, candid capture that includes the subject’s full body, the desk she’s standing next to, a portion of the chalkboard, and a glimpse of the window.  

- **Details & Imperfections**  
  Slight tilt of the camera, partial crop at the top right corner, minor dust particles on the lens, natural imperfections in the focus and exposure that give the image a raw, unprocessed look.
