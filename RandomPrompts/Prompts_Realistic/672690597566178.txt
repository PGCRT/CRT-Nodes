**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

- **Subject & Pose**  
  A young woman with light brown hair tied in a loose ponytail, wearing a cream‑colored, frayed‑edge cropped jacket. She’s mid‑selfie: left hand holding a beige smartphone at an angle that captures a slight diagonal tilt of the frame. Her face is turned toward the camera with a bright, candid smile; her eyes are slightly focused on the phone’s screen. A small hoop earring glints on her left ear.

- **Lighting & Exposure**  
  Natural daylight from a high‑angle window, creating a soft lens flare across the upper right of the frame. Slight overexposure on the top of the image gives a warm, raw feel. No auto‑beauty or filter enhancement—just the raw, unprocessed look of a handheld shot.

- **Camera & Lens Settings**  
  Wide‑angle lens (24‑35 mm), short focal length. Handheld, with natural motion blur from slight instability. Everything in focus—no portrait‑mode depth‑of‑field blur; background remains sharp.

- **Composition & Framing**  
  Accidental, off‑center framing: the subject occupies the right‑hand side of the frame, leaving a small empty space on the left. The composition is not rule‑of‑thirds intentional; it’s a spontaneous, candid capture. The shot is partially cropped at the bottom right, hinting that the phone was held slightly out of frame.

- **Environment**  
  An office or studio setting: concrete walls, fluorescent ceiling lights, and a visible sign that reads “NMD” on the right side. A small yellow ball and a pair of sneakers lie on the floor in the lower right corner. The time of day is mid‑afternoon, with bright natural light streaming from a high window.

- **Details & Imperfections**  
  Slight motion blur around the phone’s camera module, a tiny lens flare streak across the top right, and a faint, natural overexposure that gives the image an authentic, raw feel. The background is fully sharp—no bokeh or subject isolation. The overall tone is zero‑studio, zero‑editorial, purely candid.
