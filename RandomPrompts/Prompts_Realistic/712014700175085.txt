**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

- **Subject & Pose**  
  A woman with long, dark hair and a casual, relaxed demeanor sits on a neatly made bed. She wears a light‑colored, patterned button‑down shirt tied at the front and black bikini bottoms; her hands rest lightly on the mattress, one near her hip, the other by her thigh. Her face is partially out of frame—only the lower half visible—giving an informal, spontaneous feel.

- **Lighting & Exposure**  
  Natural daylight from a side window creates a soft, slightly overexposed highlight on her torso and abdomen. The exposure is uneven: the upper part of the image is a bit brighter than the lower part, as if captured in ambient light without a tripod. No auto‑beauty or filter enhancement—just raw, unprocessed data.

- **Camera Settings**  
  - Lens: 24 mm wide‑angle, short focal length.  
  - Aperture: f/5.6 (mid‑range).  
  - Shutter speed: 1/125 s, producing a faint motion blur on the subject’s hands and legs.  
  - ISO: 400 – balanced for natural contrast.  
  - Focus: everything in focus—background, foreground, and subject—no portrait‑mode depth‑of‑field blur.

- **Composition & Framing**  
  The shot is handheld, slightly tilted to the right, with an off‑center crop that cuts off the top of the woman’s head and a portion of the pillow. No rule‑of‑thirds intentionality—framing is accidental, as if she was mid‑pose when the shutter was pressed.

- **Environment**  
  The setting is a bright, sunlit bedroom. A beige pillow rests against the headboard, a wooden frame visible at the top right. The bedspread has a subtle geometric pattern; the mattress is white with a faint texture. The time of day is late morning, with warm, diffused sunlight casting gentle shadows.

- **Details & Imperfections**  
  Slight tilt and partial crop give the image a candid, unposed vibe. The woman’s shirt has a faint leaf‑pattern, and her bikini bottoms are tied in a knot at the center of her chest. Her fingernails are painted a light pink, and a small hoop earring is visible on her left ear. The overall look is raw, natural, and spontaneous—no post‑processing or color grading.

- **Final Prompt**  
  *“A woman with long dark hair sits casually on a white patterned bed in a sunlit bedroom, wearing a light‑colored patterned button‑down shirt tied at the front and black bikini bottoms. Her hands rest on the mattress—one near her hip, the other by her thigh—while a beige pillow leans against the headboard behind her.
