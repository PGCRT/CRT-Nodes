**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

- **Subject & Action**  
  A young woman with long, dark hair, wearing a white and light‑blue crop tank and denim shorts, is mid‑selfie in a bustling outdoor shopping plaza. She holds a black smartphone in her right hand, angled slightly upward, capturing a photo of herself while walking. Her left hand clutches a small metallic clutch. The camera is positioned at a low angle, close to the subject’s waist, with a slight handheld shake that gives a natural motion blur.

- **Capture Style**  
  Candid, unposed, spontaneous — the woman appears unaware of the camera, mid‑step, slightly tilted frame, off‑center composition. No rule‑of‑thirds intentionality; the framing is accidental, with a partial crop that cuts off part of her left thigh and the background storefront. The image has a raw, unprocessed look: no filters, no color grading, no post‑processing aesthetic. A mild overexposure from the bright daylight gives a subtle halo around the subject.

- **Environment**  
  Location: a modern, open‑air shopping mall with glass facades and a wide tiled walkway. The time of day is late morning, around 10:30 AM, with bright, warm sunlight casting sharp shadows. Ambient conditions are clear, slightly hazy sky, with a few pedestrians in the background walking in different directions. A red “STOP” sign is visible on the right side of the frame, and a storefront with the logo “GXR” can be seen on the left.

- **Lens & Technical**  
  Use a short focal‑length wide‑angle lens (e.g., 24 mm or 35 mm). Capture everything in focus — no bokeh, no depth‑of‑field blur; background remains fully sharp. No portrait‑mode subject isolation. The image is taken with a handheld camera, producing slight motion blur from the camera’s instability and a natural freeze of the subject’s movement.

- **Composition Notes**  
  Keep the composition casual: the subject occupies the central‑left portion of the frame, her body slightly angled toward the camera. The background includes a few passersby, a shopping bag, and a white t‑shirted woman walking leftward. The lighting is natural, with the sun high overhead, creating a slight glare on the phone’s screen. The overall tone is realistic, unedited, and spontaneous.
