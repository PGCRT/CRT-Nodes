**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

- **Subject & Pose**  
  A tall, athletic woman with long, wavy blonde hair, wearing a black triangle‑style bikini, leaning casually against a concrete bridge railing. One arm is raised behind her head, the other resting on the rail; her body is slightly angled toward the camera, capturing a natural “mid‑action” candid moment.  

- **Lighting & Exposure**  
  Slight overexposure from ambient daylight (mid‑afternoon, soft diffused light). No auto‑HDR or auto‑beauty filters—raw, unprocessed look. The exposure is a little bright at the highlights but still retains detail in the shadows.  

- **Camera Settings**  
  Wide‑angle lens (24–35 mm), short focal length. Handheld shot with slight motion blur from instability; everything in focus—no bokeh, no depth‑of‑field blur, background fully sharp.  

- **Composition & Framing**  
  No intentional rule‑of‑thirds: the frame is slightly tilted and off‑center, a partial crop that gives a spontaneous, candid feel. The subject occupies the central‑right portion of the frame, with the bridge railing extending horizontally across the lower third.  

- **Environment**  
  Location: urban bridge, concrete rail, open sky above. Time of day: mid‑afternoon, overcast but bright. Weather: light cloud cover, gentle wind. The background shows a faint silhouette of distant trees and a hint of water or a river below, adding context without distracting.  

- **Technical Details**  
  - Lens: 35 mm, wide‑angle, handheld.  
  - Focus: everything in focus—subject and background.  
  - Sharpness: high‑resolution detail on the subject’s skin, hair, and the concrete rail.  
  - Color: natural tones, slight warmth from the daylight.  

- **Prompt Summary**  
  “A tall woman with long wavy blonde hair in a black bikini leans against a concrete bridge railing, one arm raised behind her head, the other on the rail, captured with a 35 mm handheld lens at mid‑afternoon. Slight motion blur and overexposure give a raw, candid feel; the frame is slightly tilted and off‑center, partial crop, everything in focus—no bokeh. The background shows a faint line of trees and a hint of water below, all sharp. No filters, no post‑processing, just a natural, spontaneous photograph.”
