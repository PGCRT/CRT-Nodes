**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

- **Subject & Pose**  
  A young woman with long, wavy blonde hair sitting on a white bed, wearing a delicate white lace lingerie set. She is mid‑pose, slightly turned toward the camera, with one hand resting on the bed and her head tilted a few degrees to the left. Her expression is natural, relaxed, and unposed—like she’s caught in a spontaneous moment.

- **Lighting & Exposure**  
  Natural daylight from a side window creates a soft, warm glow on her face and body. The exposure is slightly overexposed, giving a subtle halo effect around her hair and shoulders. No auto‑HDR or beauty filters—just raw, unprocessed light.

- **Camera Settings**  
  - Lens: 24‑mm wide‑angle, short focal length, handheld.  
  - Aperture: f/5.6 (mid‑range) for a fully sharp background with no bokeh.  
  - Shutter speed: 1/125 s, producing a faint motion blur on the subject’s hair and hands.  
  - ISO: 400 – balanced for ambient light.

- **Composition & Framing**  
  The frame is intentionally off‑center; the subject’s left side extends near the right edge of the shot, creating a casual, spontaneous composition. The top of the frame cuts off just above her head, and the bottom cuts slightly below her thighs, giving a partial crop that adds to the candid feel. No rule‑of‑thirds—just natural framing.

- **Background & Environment**  
  The background is a muted, patterned wallpaper with soft pink‑gray tones, a white bed sheet, and a small portion of a wooden nightstand visible on the left. The time of day is late morning, with warm sunlight streaming in from the left, casting gentle shadows. The weather is clear, with no wind, but the handheld shake adds a subtle motion blur.

- **Details & Imperfections**  
  Slight tilt of the camera, minor vignetting at the corners, and a tiny dust speck on the lens are present—adding realism. The lace details on her lingerie are crisp, with visible threadwork and slight overcast texture. Her skin has natural pores and subtle imperfections, no retouching.

- **Technical Notes**  
  - Everything is in focus—from her face to the bed sheet—thanks to a wide‑angle lens set to a mid‑range focal length.  
  - No portrait mode or subject isolation; the background remains fully sharp.  
  - The image has an auto‑beauty mode off, preserving raw contrast and color fidelity.

**Prompt Summary**  
> “A candid, unposed photo of a blonde woman in white lace lingerie sitting on a white bed, captured with a 24‑mm wide‑angle lens at f/5.6 and 1/125 s shutter speed, natural daylight from the side creating a soft overexposure. The frame is slightly tilted and off‑center, with a partial crop that includes a glimpse of a wooden nightstand, all in sharp focus with a subtle motion blur on the subject’s hair. The background features a muted pink‑gray patterned wallpaper, late‑morning sunlight, and a clear, still environment. No filters or post‑processing—just raw, realistic imagery.”
