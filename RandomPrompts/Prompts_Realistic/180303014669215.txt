**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework – “Boria’s Street” (New Image)**  

- **Subject & Pose**  
  - A young woman with dark hair tied in a messy high ponytail, wearing an off‑white polo‑style sweatshirt and denim shorts, captured mid‑conversation on a mobile phone.  
  - Her left hand holds the phone to her ear, head slightly tilted, eyes smiling, mouth open as if speaking.  
  - Slight motion blur at the fingertips and hair, indicating handheld shake and spontaneous movement.  

- **Lighting & Exposure**  
  - Natural daylight from a bright window in a hallway; slight overexposure on the subject’s face, soft highlights on the white shirt.  
  - No auto‑beauty or filter processing – raw, unedited look.  
  - Ambient lighting creates subtle shadows and a faint warm tone.  

- **Camera Settings**  
  - Wide‑angle lens (24‑35 mm), short focal length, everything in focus from foreground to background.  
  - No bokeh; background remains sharp, no depth‑of‑field blur.  
  - Slight tilt and off‑center framing – accidental composition, no rule‑of‑thirds.  

- **Environment**  
  - A bright, white‑painted hallway in a modern office or school building.  
  - A black tote bag with “BORIAS Street” printed on it hangs on the left side of the frame.  
  - In the background, a man in a dark jacket is seated at a desk, and a small table with a green sign is visible on the right.  
  - Time of day: mid‑afternoon, natural light coming from a window at the far end of the corridor.  

- **Details & Imperfections**  
  - Slight partial crop on the right edge; the woman’s left shoulder extends slightly beyond the frame.  
  - Small imperfections in focus: a faint smudge on the phone’s screen, a slight wrinkle in the sweatshirt cuff.  
  - Background elements (clothes hanging on a rack, a pair of sneakers on the floor) are fully sharp.  

- **Capture Style**  
  - Candid, unposed, spontaneous – subject unaware of camera, mid‑action.  
  - Natural motion freeze or slight blur from handheld instability.  
  - Zero studio lighting, zero professional composition, zero editorial tone.  

- **Final Prompt Summary (for image generation)**  
  *“A candid, wide‑angle shot of a young woman with dark hair in a messy high ponytail, wearing an off‑white polo sweatshirt and denim shorts, talking on a mobile phone in a bright hallway.
