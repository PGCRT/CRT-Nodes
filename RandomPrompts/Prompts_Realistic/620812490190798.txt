**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

- **Subject & Pose**  
  A young woman with long, wavy brown hair and round, thin‑rimmed glasses, wearing a snake‑print, deep‑V, one‑piece swimsuit. She is mid‑step on a sunlit beach, leaning slightly forward with her left hand relaxed by her side and right arm bent at the elbow, as if she just finished a playful dip in the water. Her face is turned toward the camera but not directly aimed—her eyes are slightly off‑center, giving a candid, spontaneous feel.

- **Motion & Focus**  
  Slight motion blur on the hair and hands to suggest movement, while the background remains fully sharp. Use a wide‑angle lens (24–35 mm) with a short focal length to capture a broad view of the scene. Everything in focus—no portrait‑mode depth‑of‑field blur.

- **Lighting & Exposure**  
  Natural midday sunlight, with a slight overexposure on the subject’s front to keep the image bright and airy. No auto‑HDR or beauty filters; raw, unprocessed look. The exposure is slightly uneven—warm tones on the sand, cool tones on the sea—creating a natural contrast.

- **Environment**  
  Tropical beach at high noon, with palm trees lining the shoreline and the ocean visible in the background. The sand is light beige, the sea a pale blue‑green, and the sky is a soft white‑blue. A few scattered palm fronds drift into the frame from the left, adding organic detail.

- **Lens & Technical**  
  Short focal length (24–35 mm), wide‑angle, handheld shot with slight tilt and off‑center framing. No rule‑of‑thirds intentionality—accidental composition. Background fully sharp, no bokeh. The image has a natural, raw aesthetic with zero post‑processing color grading.

- **Composition Notes**  
  - Crop the frame so the subject’s head is slightly above the center of the image, leaving a small portion of the palm tree on the left side.  
  - Keep the background fully in focus—no depth‑of‑field blur.  
  - Capture the scene as if the photographer was moving, with a slight motion blur on the hair and hands.
