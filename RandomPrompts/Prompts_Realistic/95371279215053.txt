**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

- **Subject & Action**  
  A young woman with long, dark hair, wearing a faded denim jacket and black skinny jeans, walking away from the camera across a polished concrete floor. Her blue sneakers are mid‑step, leaving a faint trail of motion blur. She’s carrying a black messenger bag slung over her left shoulder.  

- **Pose & Movement**  
  Slight motion blur on the feet and lower back, as if captured with a handheld camera at 1/60 s shutter speed. Her head is turned slightly to the right, eyes focused on a computer screen in front of her, giving an impression of casual purpose.  

- **Lighting & Exposure**  
  Natural daylight from large windows on the right side of the room creates a soft, warm glow; slight overexposure on the upper left corner gives a raw, unprocessed look. No auto‑HDR or beauty filters—just a natural, documentary feel.  

- **Environment**  
  The setting is a modern open‑plan office with white desks, gray partition walls, and large floor‑to‑ceiling windows. A small potted plant sits on a desk to the left; a framed photograph hangs on the wall. In the background, a man is seated at a computer, and another woman is visible further back near a coffee machine. The time of day is mid‑afternoon, around 3:30 pm, with bright, diffused sunlight.  

- **Lens & Technical**  
  24 mm wide‑angle lens, f/4 aperture, 1/60 s shutter speed, ISO 400. Everything is in focus from foreground to background—no bokeh or depth‑of‑field blur. The composition is accidental: the frame is slightly tilted, the subject is off‑center, and the crop cuts off part of her left foot, enhancing candid spontaneity.  

- **Details & Imperfections**  
  Slight tilt of the camera, partial crop on the left side, natural handheld shake. A small smudge on the denim jacket’s shoulder adds realism. The floor shows a subtle crack running diagonally across the lower right corner.  

- **Final Prompt**  
  “A young woman in a denim jacket and black jeans walks away from the camera across a polished concrete office floor, carrying a black messenger bag. Her blue sneakers are mid‑step with slight motion blur, her head turned right toward a computer screen.
