**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

- **Subject & Pose**  
  A young woman with long, straight, light‑brown hair standing in a bright, airy room. She wears a ribbed, taupe crop‑top with thin straps and high‑waisted, tan denim jeans that sit snugly at the hips. Her left hand rests lightly on her hip, right arm relaxed by her side, head tilted slightly to the right, eyes focused forward with a soft, natural smile. Small gold hoop earrings dangle, a delicate chain rests around her neck.

- **Lighting & Exposure**  
  Natural daylight from a large window behind her creates a slight overexposure on the upper body; the exposure is uneven, giving a raw, unprocessed feel. The light hits her face and torso evenly, but the background is only partially illuminated, preserving the ambient texture of the exterior view.

- **Camera Settings**  
  - Lens: 24‑mm wide‑angle, short focal length, handheld.  
  - Focal depth: everything from subject to background fully sharp – no bokeh, no portrait‑mode isolation.  
  - Shutter speed: 1/60 s (slight motion blur on the subject’s hair and arms).  
  - ISO: 400–600 (ambient noise captured).  
  - No auto‑HDR or auto‑beauty mode – raw, unfiltered image.

- **Composition & Framing**  
  - Candid, spontaneous capture; the frame is slightly tilted, off‑center, and partially cropped on the left side.  
  - No rule‑of‑thirds intentionality – the composition feels accidental, as if she was mid‑action.  
  - The window’s white frame occupies the left edge, while the view outside shows palm trees and distant mountains under a pale blue sky.

- **Environment**  
  - Location: A bright, modern apartment with a large sliding glass door that opens to a balcony.  
  - Time of day: Late morning, around 10:30 AM, when the sun is high but not directly overhead.  
  - Weather: Light breeze, clear sky, mild humidity.

- **Technical Details**  
  - Wide‑angle lens captures the entire scene in one shot; background fully sharp.  
  - Slight motion blur on the subject’s hair and arms from handheld instability.  
  - No post‑processing color grading or filters – just raw, natural tones.
