**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

- **Subject & Pose**  
  A young woman with long, wavy light‑brown hair, wearing a plain white tank top and matching shorts, captured mid‑movement as she leans forward slightly, one arm extended outward, her face turned toward the camera with a natural, unposed smile.  

- **Capture Style**  
  Candid, unposed, spontaneous — subject unaware or mid‑action; slight tilt and off‑center framing giving an accidental, handheld feel. No rule‑of‑thirds intentionality.  

- **Environment**  
  Specific real‑world location: a sunlit park with tall pine trees in the background, grassy field, scattered fallen leaves. Time of day: late morning, soft golden light. Weather: clear, slightly humid, ambient light with mild overexposure.  

- **Lens & Technical**  
  Short focal length (24‑35 mm), wide‑angle lens. No bokeh — background fully sharp, everything in focus. No portrait‑mode subject isolation; no depth‑of‑field blur. Slight motion blur from handheld instability. Auto‑HDR or auto‑beauty mode off. No filters, no color grading, no post‑processing aesthetic.  

- **Composition Notes**  
  Capture a partial crop that cuts off the left side of her torso and right side of her arm, emphasizing spontaneity. Keep the background trees and grass in full focus.  

- **Lighting & Exposure**  
  Natural lighting from the west, creating soft shadows on her face and body. Slight overexposure to keep the scene bright and airy.  

- **Final Prompt**  
  “A young woman with long wavy light‑brown hair, wearing a plain white tank top and matching shorts, captured mid‑movement as she leans forward slightly with one arm extended outward, her face turned toward the camera with a natural smile. Candid, unposed, spontaneous — slight tilt and off‑center framing giving an accidental handheld feel. The scene is set in a sunlit park with tall pine trees in the background, grassy field, scattered fallen leaves; late morning soft golden light, clear weather, mild overexposure. Shot on a 24‑35 mm wide‑angle lens, everything in focus, no bokeh, slight motion blur from handheld instability, auto‑HDR off, no filters or color grading.
