**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

- **Subject & Pose**  
  A young woman with a warm, natural smile, captured mid‑gesture while holding a disposable coffee cup. Her hair is loosely tied back with a few strands framing her face; she wears an olive‑green field jacket over a white top, and a thin brown leather strap from a shoulder bag is visible across her right shoulder. Her left hand grips the cup, fingers slightly curled, with a subtle motion blur indicating movement. The camera is positioned at a low angle, almost at eye level, creating a wide‑angle, “handheld” feel.

- **Capture Style**  
  Candid, unposed, spontaneous — the subject appears unaware of the camera, caught in a natural moment. Slight tilt and off‑center framing give an accidental, informal composition. The shot is partially cropped on the right side, leaving a small portion of a passerby’s white sleeve out of frame. No rule‑of‑thirds intentionality; the framing is organic and imperfect.

- **Environment**

A few pedestrians are visible in the distance, blurred slightly due to motion. The time of day is around 4 pm, with soft, warm ambient light that creates a mild overexposure on the subject’s face and a gentle shadow under the coffee cup.

  Short focal length (20–35 mm) wide‑angle lens, handheld. Everything is in focus from foreground to background; no bokeh or depth‑of‑field blur. The image has a raw, unprocessed look: no filters, no color grading, no post‑processing aesthetic. Slight overexposure on the subject’s face and cup, giving a natural brightness. Auto‑HDR or auto‑beauty mode is off. The overall tone is natural, with a slight warmth from the ambient light.
