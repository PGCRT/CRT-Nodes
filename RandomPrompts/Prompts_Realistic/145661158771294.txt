**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

- **Subject & Pose**  
  A young woman with long, wavy blonde hair, wearing a bold orange‑and‑black striped bikini, is captured mid‑crouch on a white bed. Her body is slightly tilted to the left, her right arm bent and her left hand resting on a zebra‑print blanket that drapes across the bed. She looks directly at the camera with a soft, engaging smile, her face illuminated by warm, natural daylight coming from a large window behind her.

- **Lighting & Exposure**  
  The scene is lit by bright, ambient daylight—slight overexposure on the left side of the frame gives a subtle halo effect to her hair. No auto‑HDR or beauty filters are applied; the image retains a raw, unprocessed look. The exposure is slightly uneven, with the window’s glare creating a gentle vignette.

- **Camera & Lens Settings**  
  Shot on a wide‑angle lens (24 mm) with a short focal length, capturing a broad view of the subject and her surroundings. Everything is in focus—no bokeh or depth‑of‑field blur; the background (a bright window with a faint outdoor view) remains sharp. The composition is accidental: the frame is off‑center, slightly tilted, and the subject is mid‑action, giving a candid, spontaneous feel.

- **Environment**  
  The location is a bright, airy bedroom with a large, multi‑paned window on the left. The time of day is late morning, when the sun is high but still casts soft shadows. The weather is clear, with no clouds visible outside the window, adding to the natural lighting.

- **Details & Imperfections**  
  Slight motion blur is present on the subject’s arm and the blanket, indicating handheld instability. The framing is partial—only a portion of the bed and the window are visible, enhancing the candid feel. The image has no post‑processing color grading; it looks natural and unpolished, with minor imperfections that add realism.

- **Prompt for Text‑to‑Image Generation**  
  *“A young woman with long wavy blonde hair in an orange‑and‑black striped bikini is crouching on a white bed, her right arm bent and left hand resting on a zebra‑print blanket. Bright daylight from a large window behind her creates slight overexposure on the left. Shot on a 24 mm wide‑angle lens, everything in focus with no bokeh. The frame is slightly tilted and off‑center, capturing a candid, spontaneous moment. The scene includes a bright, clear morning view outside the window, raw and unprocessed, with subtle motion blur from handheld shooting.”*
