**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

- **Subject & Pose**  
  A tall, athletic woman with long, wavy blonde hair, wearing a sheer white lace lingerie set (bra and thong) with floral motifs. She stands in a relaxed, mid‑step pose, one hand on her hip, the other near her thigh, slightly turned to the side. Her face is turned toward the camera with a subtle smile, eyes bright, makeup natural.  

- **Lighting & Exposure**  
  Natural daylight from a high‑angle window, mid‑afternoon, soft but warm. Slight overexposure on the upper body, creating a gentle halo effect. No auto‑HDR or beauty filters—raw, unprocessed look.  

- **Camera Settings**  
  - Lens: 24‑mm wide‑angle, short focal length.  
  - Aperture: f/4.0 (wide opening).  
  - Shutter speed: 1/60 s (handheld, slight motion blur on the subject’s movement).  
  - ISO: 400 (for clean noise).  
  - Focus: everything in focus—background and subject.  

- **Composition & Framing**  
  Candid, unposed, spontaneous framing; the shot is slightly tilted and off‑center, with a partial crop that cuts off a bit of the background on the left side. No rule‑of‑thirds intentionality—just a natural, accidental composition.  

- **Environment**  
  Location: A bright, airy loft in an urban apartment building, with a large window facing the south‑west. The backdrop is a plain beige curtain that drapes softly, giving a neutral canvas. Time of day: late afternoon, about 4:30 pm. Weather: clear, light breeze.  

- **Technical Details**  
  - No bokeh; background fully sharp.  
  - Slight motion blur on the subject’s hands and arms to convey movement.  
  - No depth‑of‑field blur—everything from foreground to background is in focus.  
  - No portrait mode or subject isolation.  

- **Post‑Processing**  
  Zero filters, zero color grading, no post‑processing aesthetic. Only raw capture data, with a slight natural overexposure that gives the image an airy, candid feel.  

**Prompt Summary**  
> “A tall woman in sheer white lace lingerie, long wavy blonde hair, captured mid‑pose on a bright afternoon in a loft with beige curtains. 24‑mm wide‑angle lens, f/4.0, 1/60 s shutter, ISO 400. Slight motion blur on the subject, everything in focus, candid off‑center framing, natural daylight overexposure, no filters—raw, unprocessed look.”
