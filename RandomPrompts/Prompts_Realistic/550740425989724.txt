**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

- **Subject & Pose**  
  A young woman with a high ponytail, wearing a light‑blue polka‑dot bikini with white ruffled trim, kneeling on a polished wooden floor in a bright, sunlit living room. Her left hand rests on the floor for balance, right hand slightly off the ground, head tilted upward as if she’s about to pick up a glass of wine from a small side table next to her.  

- **Lighting & Exposure**  
  Natural daylight coming from a large window on the left; slight overexposure in the upper‑right corner to capture the warm glow of the day. No auto‑HDR or beauty filters—raw, unprocessed look.  

- **Camera Settings**  
  Wide‑angle lens (24‑35 mm), short focal length, handheld. Slight motion blur on the subject’s hands and background to hint at spontaneous movement. Everything in focus—no portrait‑mode depth‑of‑field blur.  

- **Composition & Framing**  
  Imperfect, off‑center framing: the subject is slightly tilted, the crop cuts off a bit of the window frame on the left and a potted plant on the right. No rule‑of‑thirds intentionality—just a candid, spontaneous snapshot.  

- **Environment**  
  A bright, airy living room with light wood flooring, a small wooden side table holding a bottle of red wine and a glass, a tall green potted plant in the corner, and a patterned rug partially visible on the right. Time of day: late morning, soft sunlight streaming through the window.  

- **Details & Texture**  
  Capture the texture of the bikini fabric, the slight sheen on the wood floor, the leaves of the potted plant, and the faint motion blur on the woman’s hands.  

- **Post‑Processing**  
  Zero filters, zero color grading, no post‑processing aesthetic—just a raw, realistic capture.
