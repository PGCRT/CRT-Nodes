**Realistic Candid Photography Prompt Framework**

- **Subject & Pose**  
  A young woman with a straight, shoulder‑length blonde hairstyle and bangs, wearing a blue polka‑dot dress with white ruffled off‑shoulder sleeves, a pearl necklace, and a pearl bracelet. She is holding a pink handheld microphone close to her mouth, as if mid‑song or mid‑speech, leaning forward slightly on a bed with white linens. Her left hand supports the mic, her right arm rests on her hip, and she looks toward the camera with a subtle, candid expression.

- **Lighting & Exposure**  
  Natural daylight from a western window, mid‑afternoon (≈ 4 pm). Slight overexposure in the highlights; the overall exposure is a little bright but still raw, no auto‑HDR or beauty filters applied. The light hits the subject from the left, creating soft shadows on the right side of her face and body.

- **Camera & Lens Settings**  
  - Lens: 24 mm wide‑angle (short focal length), handheld.  
  - Aperture: f/5.6 – everything in focus, no bokeh, background fully sharp.  
  - Shutter speed: 1/125 s – slight motion blur on the mic and hand to convey movement.  
  - ISO: 400 – natural grain, no post‑processing.

- **Composition & Framing**  
  - No rule‑of‑thirds intentionality; the frame is slightly tilted, off‑center, with a partial crop that cuts off part of the bed’s headboard on the left and a small portion of the wall behind her.  
  - Background: a light‑colored wall with a simple framed botanical print hanging above the bed, a small green plant visible to the right.  
  - The shot captures a spontaneous, candid moment; the subject appears unaware of the camera, mid‑action.

- **Environment Details**  
  - Location: a cozy bedroom with a wooden headboard, white bedding, and a small potted plant in the corner.  
  - Time of day: late afternoon, warm natural light.  
  - Weather/ambient conditions: clear, no wind, ambient noise of a distant street or house.

- **Technical Notes**  
  - No portrait‑mode depth‑of‑field blur; background is fully sharp.  
  - Slight tilt and off‑center framing give an accidental, informal feel.  
  - Raw image with zero color grading or filters – only natural motion freeze or slight blur from handheld instability.
