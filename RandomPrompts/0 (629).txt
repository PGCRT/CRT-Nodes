A tense, claustrophobic shot inside a deep-sea submersible shows Dr. Kenna Sharma watching a sonar screen. A massive, unknown biological signature is moving below them, deeper than any known life can survive. | Suddenly, all power cuts out, and the sub begins to sink rapidly, alarms blaring. The pressure on the hull is increasing to a critical level. | Just as the viewport begins to crack, a swarm of small, bioluminescent, jellyfish-like creatures surround the sub. They press against the hull, and instead of crushing it, their combined energy field stabilizes the pressure and the sub's lights flicker back on. They are not attacking; they are rescuing her, and she has no idea why.
