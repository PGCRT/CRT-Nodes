In a city where all citizens have their emotions regulated by chemical injections, a woman named Ana, a "feeler," has managed to flush her system. She is experiencing true sadness for the first time. | She sits on a bench and weeps, and her tears are the first real tears the city has seen in a century. | A security drone descends and analyzes her tears. Instead of flagging her for re-education, its programming glitches, and it begins to emit a soft, mournful, synthesized crying sound, confused by this new, powerful data.
