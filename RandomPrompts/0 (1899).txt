A woman known as 'Mama Bones' lives in a Louisiana bayou shack, surrounded by strange trinkets. A desperate man comes to her for a charm. | She listens to his story, then takes a small alligator tooth and whispers a single, secret word into its root. | She gives it to the man and tells him it will work, but as he leaves, the camera focuses on a shelf filled with identical teeth, implying a terrible, unseen price for her magic.
