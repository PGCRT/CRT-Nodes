A slow pan across a lavish, 1920s party. A flapper named Lily, known for her carefree spirit, is dancing wildly. | She bumps into a fortune teller, who grabs her hand and her jovial expression turns to one of horror. The fortune teller whispers, "Your lifeline... it has no end." | Lily pulls her hand away, laughing it off, but later, alone in front of a mirror, she looks at her own palm. She sees the line that stretches off her hand and seems to continue, faintly glowing, into the air beside her, and her smile finally falters.
