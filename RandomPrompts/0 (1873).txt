A world-class chef, Genevieve, known for her logic and precision, inherits a cookbook from her mystical grandmother. The recipes are bizarre ("a pinch of moonlight," "a cup of laughter"). | In a fit of frustration and grief, she decides to follow a recipe for "soup that mends a broken heart" literally, using symbolic ingredients. | She takes a sip of the strange, shimmering soup, and for the first time since her grandmother's death, she smiles a genuine, soul-healing smile.
