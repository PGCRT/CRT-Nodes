A powerful sorceress, Morgana, is imprisoned in a cell made of magic-dampening crystal. She has been there for a century. | A new, young guard shows her a small kindness, leaving her an extra piece of bread. | Morgana thanks him and asks for his name. He tells her, and she smiles, because his name is the final component she needed for a devastating, long-form sympathetic curse that she can now unleash.
