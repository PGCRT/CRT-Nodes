(Sci-Fi/Action) A cyborg gladiator enters a futuristic arena. His opponent is a swarm of thousands of smaller, insect-like robots that move as a single, liquid-like entity. A dynamic, multi-angle sequence shows him fighting the shapeshifting swarm, which forms itself into giant fists, walls, and whirlpools of razor-sharp metal. High-octane, visually complex combat.
